# Open Mysteries

Questions for players to explore. Don't answer them all—let some remain unknowable.

## About [[Hollowstars]]
1. What are they, truly? Divine? Natural? Something else?
2. Why did they stop falling for 700 years?
3. Why are the old ones failing now?
4. Is the approaching star a blessing or curse?
5. Can they be controlled?
6. What happened in [[Age Before|the Age Before]]?

## About [[The Darkness]]
1. Is it connected to the stars, or separate?
2. Is it evil, or just change?
3. Can it be stopped, or only survived?
4. What will the world become if it continues?

## About the Gods
1. Are they real?
2. Which religion is correct?
3. Or are all beliefs partially true?
4. What do the gods want, if anything?

## About the World
1. What created the "[[Isle of the Broken Sphere|Broken Sphere]]"?
2. What lies in the deep oceans?
3. Are there more intact Hollowstars hidden?
4. What secrets do the [[Stillward Enclaves]] guard?

## About the Future
1. Where will the new star land?
2. Who will control it?
3. What will it change?
4. Can the old stars be saved?

## GM Guidance
- Present contradictory clues
- Let player theories inform truth
- Some mysteries have no answer
- Discovery is the reward, not always resolution
