## QUICK REFERENCE

**The approaching Hollostar:** Frostwell Tundra impact zone, ~6 months. Largest and most coherent fall on record. Already resonating with all four ancient sites.

**The four ancient sites activating simultaneously:**
- Frostwell Ember Cradle — core memory thought warming
- Verdant Crescent waking valley — dream-resonance through ley lines
- Glittering Marshes deep shards — nightmare fragment stirring
- Isle of the Broken Sphere — Dreamer's flesh cracking at the fault line

**The three working cores:**
- Lumencrest Heart — thought of motion/invention, fading and contracting
- The Endless Eye — thought of sanctuary/growth, dream-bleed radius expanding
- The hidden third — location TBD by GM

**The factions who know partial truth:**
- Somnarchs — most complete (still missing key pieces)
- Elder Tess — closest continent-side intuition
- Flame-Keeper Sorith — correct instinct, no framework
- Mad arcanists (Lumencrest undercity) — right signal, wrong interpretation

## CAMPAIGN THEMES

**The central moral question:** Is it right to mine the mind of something that cannot consent, even if it doesn't know you exist?

**The preservation/acceptance axis:** Every major faction sits somewhere on this line. None of them know the stakes are literally "wake the Dreamer or let the cycle continue."

**What the players will eventually learn:** The world is alive. They are living on it. Everything they have done — every shard used, every core tapped, every ancient site disturbed — was done to a sleeping mind.
