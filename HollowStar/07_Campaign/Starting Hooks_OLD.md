# Starting Hooks

Five ways to begin the campaign, each with different tone and focus.

## 1. The Dimming City
**Location:** [[Lumencrest]]  
**Hook:** Hired by [[Council of Lumens]] faction  
**Tone:** Political intrigue, urban investigation  
**Players are:** Agents, investigators, mercenaries

## 2. The Shrinking Lake
**Location:** [[The Endless Eye]]  
**Hook:** Travelers stranded when rationing begins  
**Tone:** Cultural exploration, community tension  
**Players are:** Merchants, pilgrims, wanderers

## 3. The Seeker Expedition
**Location:** Any region  
**Hook:** Join/stop [[Starfall Seekers]] dig  
**Tone:** Adventure, exploration, moral questions  
**Players are:** Scholars, treasure hunters, guardians

## 4. The Northern Lights
**Location:** [[Frostwell Tundra]]  
**Hook:** In the north when phenomena begin  
**Tone:** Frontier survival, cosmic wonder/dread  
**Players are:** Locals, traders, scouts

## 5. The Green Resistance
**Location:** [[Verdant Crescent]]  
**Hook:** Caught between [[Green Covenant]] and [[Lumencrest]]  
**Tone:** Moral complexity, wilderness survival  
**Players are:** Druids, refugees, mediators

## Customizing
Mix elements from multiple hooks. Let player backgrounds guide the choice.
