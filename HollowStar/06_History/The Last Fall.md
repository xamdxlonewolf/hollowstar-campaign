# The Last Fall

**Date:** 700 years ago  
**Location:** [[Starfall Basin]]  
**Result:** Creation of [[The Endless Eye]]

## The Event
A [[Hollowstars|Hollostar]] fell in the eastern desert, creating an impossible lake overnight.

## Witness Accounts (Contradictory)
- "A second sun at midnight"
- "Silent as snow, then the sound of oceans"
- "The desert wept tears of starlight"
- Different cultures remember differently

## Immediate Consequences
- Desert peoples settled permanently
- Trade routes shifted to the lake
- Ribbet and Fairy cultures merged
- Religious movements formed

## The 700-Year Silence
**Longest gap in history.** No falls since. Until now.

## Questions
- Why so long without a fall?
- Was this fall different somehow?
- What made the lake instead of just power?
- Why is it failing now?
