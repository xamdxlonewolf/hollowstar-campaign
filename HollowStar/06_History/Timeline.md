# Timeline

## Age Before (Unknown - ~1,500 years ago)
- Civilizations before [[Hollowstars]]
- Ruins suggest advanced cultures
- No intact records
- See: [[Age Before]]

## The Age of Falling (~1,500 - 800 years ago)
- **Two confirmed Hollostar falls** in this period (the first ~10,000 years ago, the second ~1,200 years ago)
- Many additional "falls" recorded in fragmented histories — most now believed to be fragments, meteor showers, or misidentified events
- Early kingdoms form around or flee from stars
- Religious schisms over interpretation
- Somnarchs maintain only 4 true falls have ever occurred — most scholars publicly disagree, few privately do
- **~1,100–1,150 years ago:** The [[06_History/War_of_Skies|War of Skies]] — Frostwell Drakona Drake Riders vs. proto-Northern Expanse Great Eagle Riders. Decades-long territorial war. Eagle Riders win through attrition and the Arathandë formation doctrine. Drakona withdraw. Ice-drakes retreat into unmapped tundra. The Drakona *Edrathar* (drake-riding bloodline) goes dormant but is not destroyed.
- See: [[The First Falls]]

## The Last Fall (700 years ago)
- [[Hollowstars|Hollostar]] creates [[The Endless Eye]]
- Longest gap between falls in known history begins
- See: [[The Last Fall]]

## The Silent Centuries (700 years ago - Present)
- No new falls for 700 years
- Rise of [[Lumencrest]]
- Current power structures solidify
- Recent decades: growing unease

## The Present Crisis (Campaign Start)
- [[Lumencrest]] begins dimming
- [[The Endless Eye]] shrinking
- Approaching star confirmed
- Factions mobilize
- [[The Darkness]] measurable
