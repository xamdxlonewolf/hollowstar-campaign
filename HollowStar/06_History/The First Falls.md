# The Disputed Falls

**Period:** ~10,000 years ago - 700 years ago

## The Two Confirmed Falls

**The First Fall (~10,000 years ago):**
- Unknown impact location
- Destroyed or transformed whatever civilization came before
- Evidence: pre-Hollostar ruins in Frostwell, impossible ancient structures
- No intact records survive — only ruins and legend

**The Second Fall (~1,200 years ago):**
- Impact: Central Heartlands (created Lumencrest's Heart)
- Buried intact star now powers Lumencrest
- First fall with reliable historical documentation
- Kingdoms formed, wars fought, Lumencrest rose to dominance

## The Scholarly Debate

Most historians record **5-7 additional "falls"** in the period between the first and second confirmed falls — catastrophic events with Hollostar-like effects documented in fragmentary records from early kingdoms.

**The Somnarch position:** These were not true falls. They were large fragment showers, misidentified meteors, and in some cases deliberate fabrication by early kingdoms seeking to legitimize power by claiming proximity to a star. Only four true Hollowstars have ever fallen. Their calculations leave no room for more.

**Why it matters:** If the Somnarchs are right, the "fallen stars" that early kingdoms built their power around were fragments at best, frauds at worst. Several modern noble lineages and religious institutions trace their authority to these disputed events.

**Current consensus:** Publicly, most scholars accept 5-7 falls. Privately, the Somnarch position is gaining ground — especially as their prediction of the fourth fall proves accurate.
