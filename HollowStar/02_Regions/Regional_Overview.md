# Regional Overview

Quick reference for all regions. See each region's index for details.

## The Regions

### [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Central_Heartlands_Index|Central Heartlands]]
- Temperate, fertile, most populated
- Home to [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Locations/Cities/Lumencrest|Lumencrest]]
- Political and cultural center
- The Ironspine Mountains (north-south range) divide the region — the Heartland Accord cities to the west, the Rivermark Sovereignty to the east
- **Crisis:** Declining crop yields, power failures

### [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/The_Rivermark_Sovereignty|Rivermark Sovereignty]]
- Eastern Central Heartlands, beyond the Ironspine Mountains
- Elven-Faun kingdom, allied with the Accord for 135 years
- Controls Starsilver deposits and eastern trade routes to Starfall Basin
- Seeking formal membership as the Accord's Sixth Seat
- **Crisis:** Sixth Seat campaign stalled; Starfall Basin merchants applying increasing pressure

### [[02_Regions/Starfall_Basin/Starfall_Basin_Index|Starfall Basin]]
- Eastern desert, [[02_Regions/Starfall_Basin/Locations/Cities/The_Endless_Eye|The Endless Eye]] oasis
- **Crisis:** Lake slowly shrinking (early stages)

### [[02_Regions/Salt_Shore/Salt_Shore_Index|The Salt Shore]]
- Eastern coastline north-to-south of Endless Eye
- Rocky coast, islands, fishing communities
- Desert-to-coast transition culture

### [[02_Regions/Silverbelt_Coast/Silverbelt_Coast_Index|Silverbelt Coast]]
- Western/central coast near Central Heartlands
- Old money + military noble families
- One large prestigious port city + river towns

### [[02_Regions/Desert_of_Glass/Desert_of_Glass_Index|Desert of Glass]]
- Volcanic Skyspine Mountains + glass desert
- Home to Clanks, Drakona, Infernis, Dwarves, Fungril
- Already chaotic — no Hollostar crisis here

### [[02_Regions/Verdant_Crescent/Verdant_Crescent_Index|Verdant Crescent]]
- Northwestern forests
- [[04_Factions/Verdant_Crescent/The Green Covenant|Green Covenant]] stronghold

### [[02_Regions/Frostwell_Tundra/Frostwell_Tundra_Index|Frostwell Tundra]]
- Northern frozen wastes
- **Target for approaching Hollostar**

### [[02_Regions/Northern_Expanse/Northern_Expanse_Index|Northern Expanse]]
- Grasslands, frontier settlements
- Gateway between north and south

### [[02_Regions/Glittering_Marshes/Glittering_Marshes_Index|Glittering Marshes]]
- Southwestern wetlands

### [[02_Regions/Isle_Broken_Sphere/Isle_Broken_Sphere_Index|Isle of the Broken Sphere]]
- Southeast islands
