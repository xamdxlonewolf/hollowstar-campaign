# The Northern Expanse

> *"The king has watched a hundred generations of us live and die. He mourns us all."*

---

**Climate:** Cool to cold, four true seasons  
**Population:** ~45,000  
**Capital:** [[02_Regions/Northern_Expanse/Locations/Cities/Elarinde_Lakemere|Elarindë / Lakemere]] (~14,000)  
**Government:** Monarchy — King Elowen Arathas (Elf, ~280 years)  
**Major Rivers:** The Nendalië (west-east, main population corridor), Coldrun (northern tributary, refugee route), Arathandë/Skyspine (western border river)  
**Crisis:** Frostwell refugees arriving; southern towns absorbing them, northern frontier closing gates

---

## Core Identity

Kingdom of two names (elven and common) and one very old king. River towns along the Nendalië; capital at the lake. Refugee crisis fracturing unity while the king plans at a timescale nobody else can match.
