# Northern Expanse Geography Corrections

**CRITICAL:** The following corrections must be applied to all Northern Expanse documents.

---

## RIVER SYSTEM (CORRECTED)

### Nendalië River (WEST TO EAST - Main River)
**Elvish name:** Nendalië ("water-way")  
**Flow:** WEST → EAST across central Northern Expanse

**Settlements (west to east):**
1. Riverbend (~900) - near Redwater Lake/Elarindë
2. Neldast/Greenwater (~1,100) - forest exit, Green Gate
3. Greenbridge (~800) - elvish bridge
4. Aranthem/Millhaven (~2,400) - Halfling agricultural, BEST integration success (~300 refugees)
5. Riverwatch (~900) - quiet success (~150 refugees)
6. Ithilvast/Farhold (~3,200) - political center, tent city crisis (~400 refugees)
7. Coldwater (~1,200) - secondary processing (~60-80/week)
8. Celebrantford/Stonecross (~1,800) - GROUND ZERO, where Coldrun meets Nendalië

### Coldrun River (NORTH TO SOUTH - Northern Tributary)
**Flow:** Frostwell border mountains → SOUTH → meets Nendalië at Celebrantford

**Settlements:**
- Highwatch (~900) - on Coldrun River, 20 miles from Ironmere, refugee waystation

**Refugee route:** Silver Pass → Coldrun south → Celebrantford → distributed west along Nendalië

### Arathandë/Skyspine (NORTH TO SOUTH - Western Border)
**Flow:** NORTH → SOUTH along western border (divides Northern Expanse from Verdant Crescent)  
**Continental scale:** Continues south through Central Heartlands to Salt Shore

**Settlements (south to north):**
1. Reed's Bend (~500) - southern border
2. Whisperford (~300) - central border
3. Northbank (~400) - northern border

## MOUNTAIN GEOGRAPHY (CORRECTED)

**Northern Mountains:** Separate Northern Expanse from Frostwell Tundra (incomplete barrier, passable in places)

**Silver Pass/Celebrant:** Primary crossing in central mountains

**Eastern Mountains:** Where frontier towns sit:
- **Greypass** (~600) - Far northern foothills, top protection point
- **Highwatch** (~900) - On Coldrun River, eastern mountains
- **Ironmere** (~1,400) - Southern tip of eastern mountains, 20 miles from Highwatch

**NO northeastern mountain range separating Northern Expanse from Central Heartlands** - this was an error

---

## THE THREE ZONES (CORRECTED)

1. **Northern Frontier:** Mountain border with Frostwell, frontier towns, refugee crisis point
2. **River Valleys:** Central open grassland along Nendalië (west-east), main population corridor
3. **Southern Forest:** Deepwood/Neldoreth around Elarindë/Lakemere capital

---

## REFUGEE FLOW (CORRECTED)

**Route:** Frostwell → Silver Pass → Coldrun River south → **Celebrantford** (ground zero) → **Coldwater** (processing) → distributed **WEST** along Nendalië to placement towns

**Success stories:** Aranthem/Millhaven (~300), Riverwatch (~150), scattered homesteads

**Crisis points:** Celebrantford (60-80/week arriving), Ithilvast/Farhold (tent city ~400), Highwatch (~300 inadequate conditions)

---

All previous references to "north-south Skyspine" for the MAIN RIVER are incorrect.  
The main river is Nendalië (west-east).  
The Skyspine/Arathandë is the BORDER river (north-south, western edge).
