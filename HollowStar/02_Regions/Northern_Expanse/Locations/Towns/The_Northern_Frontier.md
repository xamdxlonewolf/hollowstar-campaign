# The Northern Frontier

**Region:** [[02_Regions/Northern_Expanse/Northern_Expanse_Index|Northern Expanse]]  
**Identity:** The mountain border with Frostwell — where the refugee crisis is most acute

---

## THE FRONTIER TOWNS

### Celebrantford / Stonecross (~1,800)

**Location:** Far eastern Nendalië, where Coldrun River meets Nendalië  
**Identity:** GROUND ZERO — first town refugees reach, completely overwhelmed

The first town south of Silver Pass. Gateway from Frostwell. Ground zero for the refugee crisis — more arrivals than the town can process.

**Mayor Thom Riverstone** (human, 56) has petitioned the crown eleven times. Received three responses. All saying "we're aware of the situation." Still open. Still trying.

**Elder Kess Brightwater** (elf, 334) organized the bridge watchers — volunteers who watch for arriving refugees. First response: water, food, emergency shelter. They've been doing this for months. They're exhausted. They can't stop.

**Current arrivals:** 60-80 per week, continuous. Temporary population: ~200 in various processing stages.

---

### Highwatch (~900)

**Location:** Eastern mountains, on Coldrun River, 20 miles from Ironmere  
**Identity:** Desperate, still open, inadequate resources

Mountain watchtower settlement on the Coldrun River that refugees pass on their way south from Silver Pass. The town was built as waystation and observation post. Never designed for this.

**Captain Brennan Tusk** (human, 52) commands a watchtower town that refugees pass on their way to Celebrantford. The town can't turn them away. It also can't adequately house them.

**The numbers:** ~300 refugees in conditions that won't survive winter. Eleven messages sent to the crown. Three responses.

**Current status:** Still open. Barely holding. Captain Tusk is considering closing the gate and lives with that thought.

---

### Ironmere (~1,400)

**Location:** Southern tip of eastern mountains, 20 miles from Highwatch  
**Identity:** Closed gate, eight died, living with both facts

Closed the gate two months ago.

**Captain Sera Vane** (human, 48) made the call. The town had exceeded capacity. Resources were depleted. The math was clear.

Eight people died on the wrong side of the gate. Froze in the mountains because Ironmere's gate was closed.

Vane was right about capacity. Eight people still died. The town lives with both facts. Vane doesn't sleep well.

**Current status:** Gate remains closed. The town has supplies for its residents. It has nothing left to give.

---

### Greypass (~600)

**Location:** Far northern foothills, eastern mountains, top protection point  
**Identity:** Distant outpost, minimal refugee impact, isolation

The northernmost settlement, high in the eastern mountains. Remote enough that few refugees reach here — most take Silver Pass further west.

**Character:** Isolation, self-sufficiency, observes the crisis from distance

---

## THE COLDFAST EXILES

A specific group of Nine Hearths refugees — people who voted to stay in Coldfast but left anyway when it became impossible.

They named themselves the **Coldfast Exiles**. It is a name of shame they chose themselves. They are difficult to help because they don't believe they deserve it.

They're scattered across frontier towns. Identifiable by their behavior: they accept help reluctantly, work harder than required, and carry a weight that isn't physical.

---

## THE THREE WARDEN CANDIDATES

King Elowen has left the Warden of the North post vacant for two years. Three candidates:

**Commander Yara Stonefield** — Frontier-born, would close the passes. Practical. Popular in frontier towns. Feared in Lakemere.

**Advisor Theron Mael** — Elf, court-aligned, would enforce open borders as policy. Popular in Lakemere. Hated in frontier towns.

**Captain Brek Coldmarch** — Giant, Nine Hearths refugee. Walked through Silver Pass four months ago with nothing. Trusted by both refugees and locals. Doesn't want the job. Is the right person for it.

---

## THE CRISIS IN NUMBERS

**Arrivals:** 60-80 per week through Celebrantford, increasing

**Successfully integrated:** ~800 total across all Northern Expanse settlements

**Still in temporary housing:** ~600-800 across frontier and river towns

**The gap:** More arriving than being successfully placed. System approaching break point.
