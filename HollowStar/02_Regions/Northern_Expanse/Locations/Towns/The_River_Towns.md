# The River Towns

The settlements along the **Nendalië River** (west-east), the **Arathandë/Skyspine** (north-south border), and **Coldrun River** (northern tributary).

---

## THE NENDALIË RIVER (West to East)

The Nendalië flows **WEST TO EAST** across the central Northern Expanse.

### Riverbend (~900)
Western Nendalië, closest to Redwater Lake/Elarindë. Forest-edge transition town.

### Neldast/Greenwater (~1,100)
Where river exits Deepwood. The Green Gate color change phenomenon.

### Greenbridge (~800)
Ancient elvish stone bridge. Small town, manageable refugee absorption (~60).

### Aranthem/Millhaven (~2,400)
Halfling agricultural center. **BEST integration success** (~300 refugees).  
Elder Mira Greenwater coordinated perfect labor match.

### Riverwatch (~900)
Quiet success story. Council-Head Tess Watermark absorbed ~150 refugees through careful placement.

### Ithilvast/Farhold (~3,200)
Largest river town, political center. Mayor Davan Cole.  
Tent city crisis: ~400 refugees stuck in temporary shelters.

### Coldwater (~1,200)
Secondary processing center. Coordinator Maren Swiftbrook runs skills assessment.  
~60-80/week processed for placement west.

### Celebrantford/Stonecross (~1,800)
**GROUND ZERO**. Where Coldrun meets Nendalië.  
Mayor Thom Riverstone desperate. Elder Kess Brightwater's bridge watchers.  
60-80 arriving/week. Completely overwhelmed.

---

## ARATHANDË/SKYSPINE (North-South Border)

Border river between Northern Expanse and Verdant Crescent.

### Northbank (~400)
Northern border. Grain storage.

### Whisperford (~300)
Central border. Ferry crossing.

### Reed's Bend (~500)
Southern border. Fishing village.
