# Adventure Hooks - Northern Expanse

---

## THE REFUGEE CRISIS (5)

### 1. "The Warden Decision"
**Hook:** King Elowen finally asks for an outside opinion on the Warden appointment — someone without political allegiance
**Employer:** Lord Chancellor Miren Ashvale (the king won't ask directly — too proud)
**Goal:** Evaluate all three candidates honestly, report to the chancellor
**Complication:** Each candidate approaches party privately to make their case. All three are sincere. All three would be problematic.
**Level:** 3-5

### 2. "Highwatch Is Falling Apart"
**Hook:** Captain Brennan Tusk sends a desperate message — his town is 3 days from a food crisis
**Employer:** Tusk (no official backing, paying from his own funds)
**Goal:** Get emergency supplies to Highwatch before the situation turns violent
**Complication:** The road north is dangerous (winter), the crown's supply depot is in Farhold (Mayor Cole controls access), and Cole wants political concessions first
**Level:** 3-5

### 3. "The Closed Gate"
**Hook:** A family was turned away from Ironmere and two of them didn't make it to the next town
**Employer:** Surviving family member (grief, wants accountability)
**Goal:** Determine what happened, whether Ironmere's closure directly caused the deaths
**Moral complexity:** Ironmere was genuinely full. Captain Vane made a terrible choice in terrible circumstances. Nobody is wrong. People still died.
**Level:** 2-4

### 4. "The Coldfast Exiles"
**Hook:** The Coldfast Exile community wants to send a message back to Elder Durn — will he come south before it's too late?
**Employer:** Community leader (pays in Frostwell survival knowledge — invaluable)
**Goal:** Travel to Frostwell, find Elder Durn, deliver the message and bring back his answer
**Reality:** Durn's answer will break someone's heart either way
**Level:** 4-6

### 5. "The River Count"
**Hook:** Nobody knows how many refugees have actually entered the kingdom — the Keeper of the Passes is overwhelmed
**Employer:** Lord Chancellor Ashvale (needs real numbers before the king will act)
**Goal:** Conduct a census of refugees in frontier towns
**Discovery:** The real number is twice the crown's estimate. And a significant portion have already moved south toward Heartlands without being processed.
**Level:** 2-4

## THE KING AND COURT (4)

### 6. "The King's Audience"
**Hook:** Mayor Cole of Farhold has been requesting an audience for 4 months — the king finally grants one, needs trustworthy neutral escorts and witnesses
**Employer:** Both Cole AND the king's chamberlain (separately, without knowing about each other)
**Goal:** Escort Cole to Elarindë, facilitate the audience
**Complication:** Someone doesn't want this meeting to happen — two "accidents" on the road
**Level:** 3-5

### 7. "The Elvish Memory"
**Hook:** An elvish elder in the Deepwood claims to remember the LAST time a Hollostar fell — offers to share the memory with someone who can use it
**Employer:** [[04_Factions/Starfall Seekers|Starfall Seekers]] (learned of the elder, need a party to make contact — elves don't trust the Seekers)
**Goal:** Earn the elder's trust, receive the memory/account, bring it back
**What they learn:** The last impact was survivable — but only for those who were underground. The Deepwood has caverns. The elder has been preparing for this.
**Level:** 4-6

### 8. "The King's Grief"
**Hook:** Elowen's oldest human advisor just died of old age — the 23rd person by that title he has outlived
**No employer:** This hook begins as a simple "attend the funeral" situation
**Development:** The king, in private grief, says something to the party that reveals he's been waiting for something before making the Warden decision — something connected to the Frostwell ruins
**Level:** Any (character/story hook)

### 9. "The Forest Dispute"
**Hook:** Firbolg community accuses Halfling farmers of clearing forest edge for fields — the Deepwood elders are angry
**Employer:** Lord Chancellor Ashvale (king won't act fast enough, needs someone to mediate NOW)
**Goal:** Mediate between Firbolg, farmers, and forest elves before it becomes violence
**Complication:** The Halfling farmers are clearing land because refugee pressure has reduced their crop fields — it's a symptom, not a cause
**Level:** 2-4

## RIVER MYSTERIES (3)

### 10. "The High Water"
**Hook:** The Arathandë / Skyspine River is running higher than usual — Halfling farmers in Millhaven are worried
**Employer:** Millhaven farming council
**Goal:** Investigate the river upstream, find the cause
**Discovery:** Glacial melt in the Skyspine Mountains is accelerating — star approach is affecting the volcanic system. The floods will get worse.
**Level:** 3-5

### 11. "The River Spirit"
**Hook:** A river spirit has been appearing to fishermen — unusual (they normally stay out of mortal business)
**Employer:** Path of Spirits practitioner in Greenwater (concerned, needs someone to communicate with the spirit)
**Goal:** Establish contact with the spirit, understand what it wants
**Message:** The river knows something is coming. It is trying to warn the valley.
**Level:** 3-5

### 12. "The Drowned Cache"
**Hook:** A Nine Hearths refugee arrived with a waterproof map — claims a Frostwell family buried their valuables in the river before fleeing
**Employer:** The refugee (needs help retrieving it — river is cold and dangerous)
**Complication:** Two other parties are also after the cache (they saw the map)
**What's in it:** Mostly sentimental items, but also — a small carved stone tablet. Same symbols as the Frostwell ruins.
**Level:** 2-4

## FRONTIER SURVIVAL (3)

### 13. "The Winter Route"
**Hook:** A scouting report suggests a faster, safer pass through the mountains — but it's unverified
**Employer:** Captain Tusk (desperately needs better logistics)
**Goal:** Scout the new route, determine if it's viable for refugee caravans
**Hazard:** Mountain spirits in the pass — not hostile, but territorial. Wrong move and they close the route permanently.
**Level:** 3-5

### 14. "The Night Attack"
**Hook:** Something has been raiding the refugee camps outside Stonecross — not bandits (moves too fast, leaves no tracks)
**Employer:** Stonecross mayor
**Goal:** Identify and stop the attacks
**Discovery:** A cold-adapted creature followed the refugee column south from Frostwell — displaced by the approaching star, confused, not malicious
**Resolution:** Multiple options (drive it away, find it territory, help it return north)
**Level:** 3-5

### 15. "Before the Pass Closes"
**Hook:** Deep winter approaching — Silver Pass will be impassable in 3 weeks
**Goal:** One last supply run north to Highwatch before the route closes for the season
**Stakes:** Everything Highwatch needs for winter must go now or not at all
**Complication:** A Nine Hearths family is still in the pass trying to come south — and a blizzard is coming
**Level:** 2-4

---

**Levels:** 2-4 (community, investigations, frontier) · 3-5 (political intrigue, refugee crisis, river) · 4-6 (court, Frostwell connections, elvish knowledge)
