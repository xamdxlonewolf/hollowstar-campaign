# Peninsula Towns - L'Empire Verdienne

Smaller mainland settlements supporting the imperial cities.

---

## Cabagne (~8,000)

**Location:** Eastern interior peninsula, between Belacroix and forest  
**Identity:** Interior agriculture, livestock, food supply  

Cabagne sits in the peninsula's agricultural interior, producing grain and raising livestock that feeds the coastal cities. The town is surrounded by farmland managed under imperial agricultural coordination.

**Economy:** Grain farming, livestock (cattle, sheep, pigs), food processing, agricultural supplies

**Character:** Rural prosperity, farming families, practical people doing essential work

**Current role:** As empire builds military supplies, Cabagne's food production becomes strategically important. Increased demand, increased prices, farmers prospering.

---

## Avilliers (~7,000)

**Location:** Southern peninsula, inland from Canau/Montauseau  
**Identity:** Wine production, vineyard center, quality spirits  

Avilliers produces the empire's best wines. The town's vineyards benefit from perfect soil and climate - rolling hills, warm days, cool nights, mineral-rich earth.

**Economy:** Wine production (dominant), viticulture, cooperage (barrel-making), wine trade

**Character:** Vintner culture, generations of family wineries, pride in craft, exports across empire and to Vossomar

**The vineyards:** Hundreds of acres surrounding the town, multiple family operations plus larger commercial vineyards. Avilliers wine is premium product.

**Current prosperity:** Increased demand from Vossomar traders - empire's wines are exotic there. Avilliers is quietly wealthy from export trade.

---

## Perpilles (~5,000)

**Location:** Northern coastal strip, between Draguisart and Sainteloise  
**Identity:** Coastal fishing, northern trade waystation  

Perpilles is a working fishing town on the northern coast, smaller than Draguisart but essential to the northern fishing industry. The town processes local catches and provides waystation services for coastal traffic.

**Economy:** Fishing, fish processing, coastal trade services, waystation hospitality

**Character:** Honest labor, tight community, everyone knows everyone, hard work valued

---

## Sarnin (~4,000)

**Location:** Eastern interior, between Belacroix and Aurigneux  
**Identity:** Timber processing, interior trade hub  

Sarnin processes timber from interior forests before it reaches coastal cities. The town operates sawmills powered by the river running through it.

**Economy:** Timber milling, lumber processing, river transport coordination

**Character:** Sawmill town, the sound of cutting wood constant, workers skilled with dangerous equipment, safety culture strong

---

## Vierteaux (~3,000)

**Location:** Southern peninsula, between Calvaire and Marticourt  
**Identity:** Hemp farming, rope fiber production  

Vierteaux grows the hemp that becomes Marticourt's rope. The fields surrounding the town produce massive quantities of raw fiber processed locally then shipped north.

**Economy:** Hemp cultivation, fiber processing, agricultural support for Marticourt

**Character:** Agricultural town tied to naval supply chain, understands their work matters to empire's strength

---

## Orlet (~3,000)

**Location:** Eastern interior, north of Belacroix  
**Identity:** Mixed agriculture, livestock market  

Orlet is a market town where interior farms bring livestock and produce for sale. The town operates a twice-weekly market that draws traders from surrounding areas.

**Economy:** Agricultural market operations, livestock trading, food distribution

**Character:** Market culture, negotiation and trade, middlemen connecting producers to buyers

---

## Alenris (~2,500)

**Location:** Northwestern peninsula, mountains approach  
**Identity:** Mountain foothills, small-scale mining, quarrying  

Alenris sits where the peninsula's hills become mountains. The town operates small-scale mining (copper, tin) and quarrying operations (building stone).

**Economy:** Small-scale mining, quarrying, stone processing

**Character:** Mountain community, harder life than coastal towns, proud of toughness

---

## Montveil (~6,000)

**Location:** Northwest coastal, between Varemonde and western approaches  
**Identity:** Coastal agriculture, fishing, northern produce  

Montveil is a northern coastal town combining agriculture and fishing. The climate here is cooler than southern peninsula but the soil is good. The town produces northern crops (root vegetables, hardy grains) and operates a modest fishing fleet.

**Economy:** Coastal agriculture, fishing, mixed farming, northern produce export

**Character:** Mixed economy town, diversified rather than specialized, stable rather than wealthy
