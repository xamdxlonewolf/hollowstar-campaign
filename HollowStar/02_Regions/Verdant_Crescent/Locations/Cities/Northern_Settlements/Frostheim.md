# Frostheim

**Population:** ~4,500 | **Region:** [[02_Regions/Verdant_Crescent/Verdant_Crescent_Index|Verdant Crescent]]  
**Location:** Far northern island, ice-bound most of year  
**Ancestry:** Giant majority (~85%), others (~15%)  
**Identity:** Giant cultural hub, ice-bound settlement, traditional Giant community

---

## THE SETTLEMENT

Frostheim sits on a remote northern island surrounded by ice for 8-9 months of the year. Only Giants would consider this location viable for permanent settlement. To them, it's home.

**The island:** Rocky, wind-blasted, ice-locked except for brief summer thaw. Natural harbor frozen solid most of year. Only accessible by ice-walking during winter freeze.

**Why Giants thrive here:** Cold doesn't bother them the way it affects smaller ancestries. Ice fishing and seal hunting provide food year-round. The isolation allows traditional Giant culture to continue unchanged.

---

## ECONOMY

**Ice fishing:** Giants cut holes through thick ice, fish deep cold waters year-round. Catches feed the community and trade when ships can reach the island.

**Seal hunting:** Seals congregate around the island. Giant hunters are patient, skilled, successful.

**Summer trade:** During brief thaw (2-3 months), ships reach Frostheim. The community trades preserved fish, seal products, and Giant-crafted goods for supplies they can't produce.

**Self-sufficiency:** The settlement must be self-sufficient 9 months of year. They've mastered this. Food preservation, fuel management, winter survival — Giants know how.

---

## CULTURE

**Giant traditions:** Frostheim maintains traditional Giant ways more purely than integrated communities elsewhere. Songs, stories, customs unchanged for generations.

**The Long Winter Gathering:** Annual festival during darkest winter weeks. All families gather in the Great Hall (carved from island rock). Stories are told, songs sung, bonds renewed.

**Isolation as strength:** Most ancestries see isolation as hardship. Giants see it as preservation. Here, they don't adapt to others — others aren't here.

---

## RELATIONSHIP TO OUTSIDE

**Minimal contact:** Ships reach the island 2-3 months per year. That's enough. Giants conduct necessary trade, then return to their lives.

**Respect from afar:** Other northern settlements respect Frostheim's existence without trying to integrate with it. The island is Giant territory. That's understood.

**The Covenwind connection:** Northern shipping center Covenwind coordinates trade with Frostheim during thaw season. Professional relationship, minimal social interaction.
