# The Simiah

> *"They move through the canopy like we move through rooms. The forest is their house. We are guests in it."*

---

## OVERVIEW

The Simiah are an ancestry of anthropomorphic simians — resembling the full spectrum of primate life, from the largest great apes to the smallest marmosets, with all variations between. Long limbs, prehensile feet, and an instinctive relationship with vertical space that no other ancestry quite matches.

They are among the oldest peoples of the Verdant Crescent. Some Simiah communities predate every other settlement in the forest. The oldest Simiah elders remember things that elvish records only partially describe.

They are fully playable as a character ancestry.

---

## BIOLOGY & APPEARANCE

**The spectrum:** Simiah resemble all simian creatures — no two communities look alike:
- **Great-folk:** Large, powerful builds (gorilla/orangutan-like) — rare, deeply respected
- **Common-folk:** Medium builds (chimpanzee/macaque-like) — most numerous
- **Small-folk:** Compact, fast, incredibly agile (marmoset/tamarin-like) — common in high canopy

**Physical traits all Simiah share:**
- Prehensile feet (can grip branches, tools, weapons with feet as well as hands)
- Long limbs relative to body (reach and swing)
- Tail (most Simiah — length varies; Great-folk often have reduced tails)
- Dense body hair (color varies widely — black, brown, red, grey, white, mixed)
- Expressive faces (Simiah communicate enormous nuance through facial expression)

**Movement:**
- In trees: Fluid, fast, instinctive — brachiation, leaping, climbing all natural
- On ground: Comfortable but slower — most Simiah prefer canopy when available
- Prehensile feet mean they can carry things while climbing (hands-free)

## CULTURE & SOCIETY

### Canopy Communities
Most Simiah live in the forest canopy — platforms, bridges, and structures built between and around living trees without harming them. Communities range from 50 to 800 individuals.

**Structure:**
- **Bands** (immediate family/companions, 8-20) — primary social unit
- **Troops** (several bands sharing territory, 50-200)
- **Groves** (multiple troops recognizing common territory, 200-800) — loose political unit

**Leadership:**
- Band leaders chosen by consensus (process involves a lot of vocalization)
- Troop leaders emerge through demonstrated wisdom and conflict resolution
- No Grove-wide ruler — decisions requiring Grove consensus take time (Simiah are comfortable with this)

### Values

**Attention:** Simiah pay extraordinary attention to their environment. Noticing things — changes in the forest, shifts in animal behavior, new smells — is a core social value.

**Memory:** Oral tradition is crucial. Simiah who can recite history and teach others are highly respected.

**Playfulness:** Not frivolity — Simiah believe play is how intelligence develops and maintains itself. Elders play. Serious matters are sometimes resolved through play.

**Community:** No Simiah exists alone by choice. Isolation is considered a sign of crisis, not preference.

### Communication
- Vocalization (complex, nuanced — outsiders hear "chattering," Simiah hear paragraphs)
- Facial expression (equally complex — Simiah can hold entire conversations face-to-face without sound)
- Gesture (especially useful at distance or for silence)
- Prehensile feet tap-codes on branches (subtle, fast — effectively a private channel)

## ANCESTRY FEATURES (Daggerheart)

Simiah are an official ancestry in the Daggerheart rulebook — use the features as written there. The lore above reflects how Simiah fit specifically into the Verdant Crescent and the World of Hollowstars.

---

## SIMIAH IN THE VERDANT CRESCENT

**Relationship with other peoples:**
- Elves: Long alliance — Simiah memory + Elven records = the most complete historical picture in the forest
- Faun: Friendly — different relationship with the forest, compatible
- Druids: Respected — Simiah often serve as guides for Covenant initiates
- Humans (edge): Cautious — not hostile, just watchful. Humans' relationship with trees is complicated.
- Katari: Warm — both peoples value route knowledge and memory

**Green Covenant:**
Many Simiah are Covenant members — their canopy perspective and environmental sensitivity make them exceptional Wardens and Rangers. The current Warden of Memory is Simiah.

**The ancient waking:**
Simiah elders noticed the changes first — 8 years ago. They brought it to the Covenant. They are still waiting for an explanation they consider adequate.

---

## SIMIAH OUTSIDE THE VERDANT CRESCENT

Simiah living outside the forest are uncommon but not rare:
- Covenant agents operating in other regions
- Traders following Katari routes
- Individuals who left (various reasons — adventurers, exiles, seekers)
- Some settled in [[02_Regions/Northern_Expanse/Northern_Expanse_Index|Northern Expanse]] forest communities

Outside the Verdant Crescent, Simiah attract attention — most peoples have never seen them. This ranges from curious fascination to wariness. Simiah generally find it tiring.
