# The Verdant Crescent

> *"The forest does not ask if you belong here. It simply decides."*

---

**Climate:** Temperate to warm, wet, heavy canopy  
**Population:** ~35,000  
**Government:** None — each settlement independent  
**Major Organization:** [[04_Factions/Verdant_Crescent/The Green Covenant|The Green Covenant]] (based here)  
**Crisis:** Something ancient waking in the deep forest; the Covenant knows, most settlements don't yet

---

## Core Identity

Great western forest — old, deep, ungoverned. **Deep Forest People** (Elves, Simiah, Faun, Druid); **Edge Settlements** (human/mixed, forest border with Central Heartlands); **The Green Covenant** at the Heartgrove.
