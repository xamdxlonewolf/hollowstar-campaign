# Adventure Hooks - Verdant Crescent

---

## THE ANCIENT WAKING (5)

### 1. "What the Faun Know"
**Hook:** A Faun elder approaches party outside the Verdant Crescent — won't go back in yet, wants someone else to investigate first
**Goal:** Find out why the Faun stopped visiting the waking site 3 years ago
**Discovery:** The site has been changing for longer than the Covenant admitted — and the Faun have been keeping their own records
**Level:** 3-5

### 2. "The Third Settlement"
**Hook:** Covenant Memory Warden finds reference to Oldwood settlement "incorporated" by the forest 2,000 years ago — wants someone to find it
**Goal:** Locate Oldwood (or what it became) in the deep forest
**Discovery:** It's still there. The people are still there. Changed, but there. They've been waiting for someone to find them.
**Level:** 5-7

### 3. "The Triangle"
**Hook:** A cartographer hired by the Starfall Seekers notices the three responding Sacred Groves form a perfect triangle — with the waking site at center
**Employer:** Seekers (want access, Covenant refused)
**Goal:** Convince Maren to share data, or find another way in
**Complication:** Maren already knows about the triangle. She's known for two years. Why hasn't she acted?
**Level:** 4-6

### 4. "The Subsonic"
**Hook:** A Simiah elder in a canopy community near the waking site has gone silent — won't vocalize, won't move, just sits
**Employer:** Their band (frightened, don't know what to do)
**Goal:** Find out what the elder is listening to and whether they can be reached
**Discovery:** The elder is in conversation with something at the waking site — and has been for three weeks
**Level:** 4-6

### 5. "Maren's Secret"
**Hook:** The Covenant's Memory Warden comes to party privately — found records Maren confiscated and didn't share
**Goal:** Determine what the 2,000-year records fully say and whether Maren is keeping the Covenant safe or keeping it blind
**Complication:** Maren is not wrong to be cautious. She is wrong to be alone with this.
**Level:** 5-7

## THE COVENANT VS. LUMENCREST (4)

### 6. "The List"
**Hook:** The Warden of the Edge needs someone to deliver a final warning to a Timberwatch family who took Lumencrest money
**Goal:** Deliver the warning, assess whether the family can be pulled back from the arrangement
**Complication:** The family has already signed a contract — Lumencrest has leverage over them now
**Discovery:** The contract contains a clause requiring them to report Covenant patrol routes
**Level:** 2-4

### 7. "The Logging Camp"
**Hook:** A new Lumencrest logging camp has appeared overnight — large, well-supplied, positioned just outside Covenant territory
**Employer:** Warden of the Edge (needs assessment before responding)
**Goal:** Infiltrate the camp, understand its purpose and scale
**Discovery:** This isn't a logging camp — it's a staging area. For what, exactly, is the question.
**Level:** 4-6

### 8. "The Reach Agent"
**Hook:** The Covenant's Warden of the Reach has been operating in Lumencrest without Maren's authorization
**Employer:** Maren (needs to know what her Warden has done before it becomes a crisis)
**Goal:** Find the Reach agent in Lumencrest, assess what they've done
**Discovery:** The agent has made contact with the anti-Expansionist faction in Lumencrest — a secret alliance that could change everything. Also possibly illegal by Heartlands law.
**Level:** 4-6

### 9. "Mayor Woodmarch's Choice"
**Hook:** Timberwatch Mayor Sera Woodmarch needs outside advisors — someone with no stake in either Covenant or Lumencrest
**Goal:** Help her understand her actual options
**Reality:** There are only two: Covenant relationship or Lumencrest money. Both have real costs. Neither is obviously correct. The party has to help her think through it, not make the choice for her.
**Level:** 2-4

## DEEP FOREST (3)

### 10. "Into the Heartgrove"
**Hook:** Maren grants the party rare access to the Heartgrove — she needs outside eyes on something she doesn't want Covenant members to see
**Goal:** Observe and report on changes to the Anchor trees
**What they see:** The twelve oldest trees are slowly, imperceptibly, moving — roots pulling through earth, trunks rotating. On a timescale of decades. Measurable only with the right instruments. Moving toward the waking site.
**Level:** 5-7

### 11. "The Rootdeep Request"
**Hook:** A Rootdeep Grove elf emerges from underground (unprecedented) and asks for help finding something stolen from their archives
**Goal:** Recover stolen records — taken by a Covenant member, not an outsider
**Discovery:** The records describe the previous waking event in detail — including what stopped it and what the cost was
**Level:** 4-6

### 12. "The Valley"
**Hook:** Someone needs to go to the waking site — Maren won't send Covenant members (too visible), needs outside party
**Goal:** Spend three days at the valley, document everything, return
**Experience:** The subsonic is overwhelming for Simiah characters. Animals behave strangely but not aggressively. On night two, the stone at the center does something.
**Level:** 5-8

## EDGE SETTLEMENT STORIES (3)

### 13. "The Greengate Theft"
**Hook:** A rare forest good has been stolen from Greengate market — unique enough that selling it identifies the buyer
**Employer:** The trade council (losing trust damages their livelihood)
**Goal:** Track the stolen goods, find who took them and why
**Twist:** Stolen by a Simiah who needed it for a community medical emergency — couldn't afford it, didn't know how to ask
**Level:** 2-3

### 14. "Mosshaven's Medicine"
**Hook:** A new disease in edge settlements — Mosshaven's herbalists have a treatment but can't produce enough
**Employer:** Mosshaven's healer collective
**Goal:** Help gather rare marsh-forest boundary ingredients, protect gatherers from territorial creatures
**Complication:** Lumencrest agents are also collecting the same ingredient — for a different purpose
**Level:** 2-4

### 15. "The Last Katari Route"
**Hook:** The main Katari trade route through the Crescent is being disrupted — animals blocking the path in unusual numbers
**Employer:** Katari trade caravan (stranded)
**Goal:** Find an alternate route or resolve the blockage
**Discovery:** The animals are gathering along a path that passes directly through the waking triangle's outer edge — the route has become spiritually significant to them
**Level:** 3-5

---

**Levels:** 2-4 (edge settlements, Lumencrest pressure) · 3-5 (Covenant politics, forest mysteries) · 4-7 (deep forest, staging, records) · 5-8 (waking site, Heartgrove, Oldwood)
