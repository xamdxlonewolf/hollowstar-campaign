# Adventure Hooks - The Salt Shore

---

## THE SALTMERE PROBLEM (5)

### 1. "The Tide King's Problem"
**Hook:** Voss approaches party directly at The Anchor — needs outsiders with no local loyalties
**Goal:** Investigate how deep Saltmere's influence runs in Driftmere and two other towns
**Complication:** The Saltmere factor Maret Dulce is very good — finding proof is hard
**Twist:** One of Voss's own Shore Eyes is on Saltmere's payroll
**Level:** 3-5

### 2. "The Council Vote"
**Hook:** Driftmere votes in three weeks on a formal Saltmere trade treaty
**Employer:** Multiple factions want party's help swinging the vote
**Goal:** Investigate what the treaty actually contains (nobody's read the full document)
**Discovery:** Treaty contains a clause that would grant Saltmere jurisdiction over disputes — effectively ending Voss's authority in Driftmere
**Level:** 2-4

### 3. "The Bought Captain"
**Hook:** One of Voss's fleet captains missed a scheduled patrol — three fishing boats robbed in the gap
**Employer:** Voss (privately — can't let his own captains look compromised publicly)
**Goal:** Find the captain, determine if it was Saltmere bribery, accident, or something else
**Complication:** Captain claims illness — maybe true, maybe cover story
**Level:** 3-5

### 4. "Maret's Real Job"
**Hook:** Saltmere factor Maret Dulce was seen meeting with someone who definitely isn't a merchant
**Employer:** Harbormaster Liss (suspicious, wants confirmation before telling Voss)
**Goal:** Surveil Maret, identify her contact, determine what's being planned
**Twist:** Her contact is from a third party entirely — not Saltmere, not Voss. Someone new.
**Level:** 3-5

### 5. "The Defection"
**Hook:** A Tidewrack family wants to relocate to Driftmere (Saltmere money is appealing)
**Employer:** Elder Tobas (not officially — just asks party to talk to them)
**Goal:** Understand why they're leaving, whether it can be addressed
**Real issue:** Their fishing boat needs expensive repairs — Saltmere offered to cover the cost
**Resolution:** Find another solution (Voss's fund exists for this — but Tobas didn't tell them)
**Level:** 1-3

### 5b. "The Silver Expansion"
**Hook:** Voss asks party to escort a Salt Shore intermediary making his first contact run with Silver Belt merchants — quietly, without the Houses noticing
**Goal:** Introduce three Silver Belt merchant captains to Voss's protection network
**Complication:** One captain is nervous and considering informing House Vethara
**Twist:** Captain Rynn Saltblood of Vethara's fleet is distantly aware something is happening in Silver Belt merchant traffic — if she connects the dots, Voss's quiet expansion becomes very loud
**Level:** 3-5

## ISLAND MYSTERIES (4)

### 6. "The Sunken Crown"
**Hook:** A salvage crew ignored Voss's discouragement and dived the Sunken Crown
**Employer:** Voss (find them — they haven't returned)
**Goal:** Locate the crew, determine what's down there
**Discovery:** Structure is pre-civilization, impossibly deep, and something inside it is still active
**Voss's secret:** He knows what it is. He's been keeping people away from it for years.
**Level:** 5-7

### 7. "The Far Isle"
**Hook:** A merchant claims to have landed on the Far Isle and found something valuable
**Employer:** Merchant (needs escort back — won't go alone)
**Goal:** Sail to Far Isle, retrieve whatever was found
**Complications:** Compass failure, the two previous expeditions left evidence, something watches the island
**Level:** 4-6

### 8. "Redcliff's Secret"
**Hook:** The Firbolg/Faun community on Redcliff sends a rare message — they need outside help
**Employer:** Community elder
**Goal:** Travel to Redcliff, help with unspecified problem
**Discovery:** They found a sealed cave system with ancient markings — and opened it. Something came out.
**Level:** 4-6

### 9. "The Underwater Lights"
**Hook:** Salthook salvage crew reported strange lights near a standard wreck site
**Employer:** Salthook's Goblin repair-yard leader (curious, wants to know before others do)
**Goal:** Investigate the lights
**Discovery:** Hollostar shard in deep reef — Saltmere's Maret already knows and has a diving team coming
**Level:** 3-5

## TIDE KING STORIES (3)

### 10. "The Uncut"
**Hook:** A large merchant operation has been operating in Salt Shore waters for months without paying
**Employer:** Voss (this one's personal — the operation is well-organized, not just ignorant)
**Goal:** Identify the operation, stop it, find out who funded it
**Discovery:** The operation is a [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Locations/Cities/Lumencrest|Lumencrest]] test — seeing if Voss can be circumvented before a larger move
**Level:** 4-6

### 11. "Sela's Job"
**Hook:** Voss's daughter Sela needs backup for something she won't fully explain
**Employer:** Sela (approaches party independently, hasn't told her father)
**Goal:** Help Sela with what turns out to be: infiltrating the Saltmere factor house to copy documents
**Complication:** Maret Dulce is better-prepared than expected
**Twist:** Documents reveal something Voss won't like — and Sela has to decide whether to tell him
**Level:** 3-5

### 12. "The Succession Question"
**Hook:** Someone starts a rumor: Voss is dying (he isn't — but the rumor is deliberate)
**Employer:** Voss (find who started it and why)
**Goal:** Trace the rumor back to its source
**Effect:** Watching how the Salt Shore reacts to the rumor reveals exactly how dependent on Voss the system is — and how fragile
**Level:** 2-4

## MARITIME ADVENTURES (4)

### 13. "The Storm Season Run"
**Hook:** Critical medical supplies needed at Greenholm — storm season starting, no one will make the run
**Employer:** Brinehaven physician (people will die without it)
**Goal:** Sail to Greenholm and back through worsening weather
**Complications:** Weather, a damaged boat, and someone sabotaged the supplies before departure
**Level:** 2-4

### 14. "The Ghost Ship"
**Hook:** A fishing boat returned to Brinehaven unmanned — crew missing, everything intact
**Employer:** Family of missing crew (desperate)
**Goal:** Sail the same route, find what happened
**Discovery:** Tied to the Far Isle compass interference — crew were pulled off course
**Level:** 3-5

### 15. "The Reef Pilots"
**Hook:** The main reef navigation route has changed — three ships ran aground in one week
**Employer:** Merchant consortium (losing ships is losing money)
**Goal:** Re-map the changed reef section, determine why it shifted
**Discovery:** Something on the reef floor is physically moving — slowly, but definitely moving
**Level:** 3-5

---

**Levels:**
- **1-3:** Community stories, simple investigations
- **3-5:** Saltmere intrigue, island exploration, Tide King work
- **4-7:** Deep island mysteries, the Sunken Crown
