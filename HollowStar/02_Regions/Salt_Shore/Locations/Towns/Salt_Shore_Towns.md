# Salt Shore Towns

The fishing towns and coastal settlements of the Salt Shore region.

---

## Tidewrack (~2,400)

**Location:** Northern coast, loyal to Voss  
**Identity:** Fishing town, Tide King supporter  

Tidewrack is a northern fishing town that strongly supports the Tide King. Elder Tobas has publicly backed Voss's consolidation efforts and provides intelligence on other towns' positions.

**Economy:** Fishing, coastal trade, information gathering for Voss

**Leadership:** Elder Tobas (human, 74, male) - Community leader, Voss loyalist

---

## Driftmere (~1,800)

**Location:** Coast near Brinehaven  
**Identity:** Took Saltmere's deal, conflicted town  

Driftmere took House Dorian's (Saltmere) generous offer for exclusive fish sales. The town is now caught between obligation to Saltmere and pressure from Voss.

**Economy:** Fishing (contracted to Saltmere), processing

**Current tension:** Regrets the deal, can't escape it, worried about Voss's response

---

## Saltholm (~1,200)

**Location:** Neutral ground between Brinehaven and Keeper's Bay  
**Identity:** Neutral meeting place, diplomatic ground  

Saltholm maintains strict neutrality. Madam Cress hosts negotiations between Salt Shore factions and refuses to take sides.

**Economy:** Hospitality, meeting facilitation, fishing

**Leadership:** Madam Cress (human, 68, female) - Innkeeper, neutral arbiter

---

## Tidepost (~1,400)

**Location:** Salt flats region  
**Identity:** Salt extraction, mineral processing  

Tidepost operates the salt flats - extracting sea salt through evaporation pools. The town supplies salt across the region.

**Economy:** Salt extraction and trade, mineral processing

**Leadership:** Headman Garrick Salt (human, 52, male) - Salt operations coordinator

---

## Gullhaven (~900)

**Location:** Island access point  
**Identity:** Island trade, Drift Chain connection  

Gullhaven serves as access point to outer islands (Drift Chain). The harbor connects mainland to island communities.

**Economy:** Island trade, fishing, ferry operations

**Leadership:** Harbor Captain Senna Drift (human, 44, female) - Coordinates island access

---

## Shallowmere (~700)

**Location:** Coastal marshes  
**Identity:** Ribbet fishing village  

Shallowmere is a Ribbet-majority fishing village operating in coastal marsh waters. The community catches marsh fish and coastal species other towns can't reach.

**Economy:** Marsh fishing, coastal gathering, Ribbet traditional methods

**Leadership:** Eldest Croaker Greenmire (Ribbet, age unknown, female) - Community elder

**Population:** Ribbet majority (~80%), mixed others (~20%)

---

## Reaper's Bay (~1,600)

**Location:** Northwestern coast  
**Identity:** Fishing, northern outpost  

Reaper's Bay is a northern fishing town operating in colder waters. The town catches species that prefer northern temperatures.

**Economy:** Cold-water fishing, fish processing, northern trade

---

## Aldric's Landing (~800)

**Location:** Southern coast  
**Identity:** Small port, southern trade  

Aldric's Landing is a small southern port town connecting Salt Shore to regions further south. The town handles modest trade volume.

**Economy:** Small-scale port operations, fishing, coastal trade
