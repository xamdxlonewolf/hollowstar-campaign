# Salt Shore Islands

Island settlements in the Salt Shore waters - the Kept Islands and outer chain.

---

## Lantern's Rest (~500 permanent, 500-1,500 total)

**Location:** Kept Islands, largest Salt Shore island  
**Identity:** Nine-Lanterns pirate headquarters, fluctuating population, criminal haven

Lantern's Rest is the Nine-Lanterns criminal organization's headquarters - where the pirate fleet coordinates operations, refits ships, and divides plunder.

**Population:** ~500 permanent residents (shipwrights, suppliers, families), plus 500-1,000 pirates depending on crews in port. Total population fluctuates wildly.

**Character:** Pirate haven disguised as fishing village. Everyone knows. Coastal authorities pretend not to. The Nine-Lanterns pay enough "taxes" to keep scrutiny away.

**Economy:** Ship repair and refitting, black market trade, pirate resupply, stolen goods fencing

**The Nine-Lanterns:** Professional thieves targeting cargo, not people. Based here, protected by island isolation and strategic bribes.

**Voss relationship:** The Tide King tolerates Lantern's Rest because the Nine-Lanterns pay him percentage and don't prey on his towns.

---

## Wrecker's Rest (~450)

**Location:** Northern Salt Shore island, outer chain  
**Identity:** Salvage operations, storm survivors, wreck scavengers

Wrecker's Rest sits on a rocky northern island where storms frequently drive ships onto reefs. The community salvages wrecks - rescuing survivors and cargo, though the line between rescue and scavenging is blurry.

**Economy:** Wreck salvage, storm rescue operations, recovered cargo sales, maritime salvage expertise

**Character:** Practical survivors. They help - and charge for it.

**Reputation:** Some call them heroes who save lives. Others call them vultures profiting from disaster. The islanders call themselves practical.
