# Saltmere

**Population:** ~9,000 | **Region:** [[02_Regions/Salt_Shore/Salt_Shore_Index|Salt Shore]]  
**Location:** Southern shore of the Gulf of Tides, across from Brinehaven  
**Government:** Merchant Council — five trading houses, rotating chair  
**Identity:** Brinehaven's rival; the city that thinks it can replace Voss

---

## THE CITY

Saltmere sits across the gulf from Brinehaven — close enough that on a clear day each city's harbor is visible from the other's. This proximity is the defining fact of both cities' politics. Saltmere was a fishing village when Brinehaven was already a city. It has been catching up for two generations and is no longer content with the gap.

The Merchant Council is an accurate description of how Saltmere runs: five trading houses, each with a seat, each pursuing its own interests and periodically combining against the others. The city is wealthy, ambitious, and not interested in anyone's protection arrangements.

---

## THE BRINEHAVEN RIVALRY

Saltmere doesn't pay Voss's cut. This is their proudest policy and their biggest gamble.

**The argument:** Saltmere's harbor is on the protected inner gulf. Grey Sails operate in the Silver Belt Sea, not the gulf. The "protection" Voss offers is protection from a threat he largely controls — paying his cut is extortion dressed as service.

**The reality:** Voss has not tested Saltmere directly. He has instead applied pressure through the coastal routes — Saltmere-bound shipping has experienced a 15% higher incident rate than Brinehaven-bound shipping over the past two years. Correlation is not proof. The Merchant Council has chosen not to treat it as proof.

**The escalation:** Saltmere is now actively recruiting merchants who currently route through Brinehaven, offering preferential harbor fees and trade terms. Two of Brinehaven's established merchant families have moved significant operations to Saltmere in the past year.

Voss has not yet made a direct response. The fact that he hasn't is being interpreted as weakness by Saltmere and as patience by everyone who knows him.

---

## CURRENT ACTIVITY

**Port expansion:** The Merchant Council has approved construction of three new deep-water berths. If completed, Saltmere will be able to handle larger cargo vessels than Brinehaven.

**The Block:** Saltmere has its own information market (smaller, less established than Brinehaven's). Current conversation topics: whether the Isle of the Broken Sphere rumors are credible, what Lumencrest's survey ship found, whether the Grey Sails' unusual route avoidance means opportunity.

**The Northern Expanse trade route:** Saltmere has been negotiating directly with Mayor Davan Cole of Farhold/Ithilvast to establish a river-to-coast trade route that bypasses Brinehaven entirely.
