# The Salt Shore

> *"The Tide King doesn't claim the sea. He just makes sure everyone on it knows his name."*

---

**Climate:** Warm coastal, humid  
**Population:** ~28,000  
**Major City:** [[02_Regions/Salt_Shore/Locations/Cities/Brinehaven|Brinehaven]] (~11,000) — Tide King's seat  
**Government:** The Tide King (de facto)  
**Crisis:** Saltmere expanding influence; buying councils, funding rivals to the Tide King

---

## Core Identity

Working coast towns, fishing boats, hard weather. Tide King Voss runs things — useful, connected, dangerous to remove. Mainland towns independent in spirit; islands as close to lawlessness as anywhere.
