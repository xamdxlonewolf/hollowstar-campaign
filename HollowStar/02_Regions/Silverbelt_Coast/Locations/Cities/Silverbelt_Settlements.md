# Silverbelt Coast - Cities and Towns

Settlements along the northeastern coast (Silverbelt Coast region).

---

## CITIES

### Vetheris (~13,000)

**Location:** Central Silverbelt Coast, at major river confluence  
**Ancestry:** Human majority (~55%), elf (~20%), half-elf (~15%), others (~10%)  
**Identity:** River trade hub, Four Houses coordination center  
**Government:** The Four Houses council

Vetheris sits at the confluence of multiple rivers flowing from the interior, making it natural trade hub. The Four Houses (merchant families controlling interior trade) coordinate operations here.

**Economy:** River trade (dominant), warehousing, trade coordination, river transport

**The Four Houses:** Merchant families controlling upriver trade routes - House Silverrun, House Clearwater, House Goldstream, House Brightflow

**Character:** Commercial prosperity, merchant culture, river logistics expertise

**Current activity:** Coordinating increased trade volume as continental tensions drive demand

---

## TOWNS

### Vethport (~3,500)

**Location:** Coast near Vetheris  
**Identity:** Coastal port, maritime connection  

Vethport handles coastal shipping for Vetheris - goods arriving by river transfer to ships here for coastal trade.

**Economy:** Port operations, river-to-sea transfer, warehousing

---

### Highcrest (~2,800)

**Location:** Upriver from Vetheris  
**Identity:** River town, interior connection  

Highcrest is upriver trade town connecting interior regions to Vetheris. The town operates river docks and waystation services.

**Economy:** River trade, waystation services, interior goods coordination

---

### Votheris (~2,200)

**Location:** South of Vetheris  
**Identity:** Smaller river town, fishing  

Votheris (sometimes confused with Vetheris due to similar names) is smaller river town south of the main hub. Primarily fishing and local trade.

**Economy:** Fishing, local river trade, small-scale operations

---

### Saltmere (~1,400)

**Location:** Coastal  
**Identity:** Fishing, salt water operations  

Saltmere is coastal fishing town operating in salt water areas near the Silverbelt. Not to be confused with Salt Shore's settlements.

**Economy:** Coastal fishing, salt water operations

---

### Shallowmere (~900)

**Location:** Coastal shallows  
**Identity:** Shallow water fishing, coastal operations  

Shallowmere operates in coastal shallow waters, catching species that prefer those conditions.

**Economy:** Shallow water fishing, coastal gathering

---

### Gullhaven (~800)

**Location:** Small harbor  
**Identity:** Minor port, local operations  

Small harbor town providing local port services for coastal communities.

**Economy:** Small-scale port operations, local fishing

---

### Rivermeet (~3,800)

**Location:** River junction near Endless Eye, where river from basin meets desert edge  
**Identity:** Trade hub, basin-to-coast connection, river junction town

Rivermeet sits where the river from the Endless Eye approaches the desert's edge before flowing to the coast (via Calderford and Aldric's Landing). The town's position near the basin's major population center makes it a crucial trade hub.

**Economy:** River trade coordination, warehousing, desert-to-water transfer, waystation services

**Character:** Busy, commercial, benefits from proximity to Endless Eye. Goods from the basin city transfer here for river shipment to coast.

**Population:** Mixed (human ~60%, Katari ~20%, others ~20%) - Katari traders frequent this junction

**Current activity:** Increased trade volume as continental tensions drive commerce. Rivermeet warehouses are full, river traffic constant.

---

### Saltmouth (~1,900)

**Location:** Coastal river mouth, where Vetheris branch river meets sea  
**Identity:** River-mouth fishing, coastal trade, estuary operations

Saltmouth sits where a branch river from Vetheris meets the Silverbelt Coast. The estuary provides excellent fishing and serves as transfer point between river and coastal shipping.

**Economy:** Estuary fishing, river-mouth trade, coastal shipping coordination

**Character:** Working port town, not glamorous but functional. Fishers who know estuary waters, traders moving goods between river boats and coastal ships.

**Current status:** Steady operations, modest prosperity, essential link in Silverbelt trade network
