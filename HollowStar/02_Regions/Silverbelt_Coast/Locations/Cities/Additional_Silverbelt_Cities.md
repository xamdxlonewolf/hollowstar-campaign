# Silverbelt Coast - Additional Cities

Two additional coastal cities of the Silverbelt Coast region.

---

## Harborpoint (~8,500)

**Location:** Southern Silverbelt Coast, western peninsula south of Mournwatch  
**Ancestry:** Human majority (~60%), mixed others (~40%)  
**Identity:** Major harbor city, southern maritime hub, trade coordination  
**Government:** Harbor Council (merchant oligarchy)

Harborpoint sits on the western peninsula jutting into southern Silverbelt waters. The city operates a major natural harbor serving as southern maritime trade hub for the entire Silverbelt Coast.

**The harbor:** Deep, protected, capable of handling large vessels. Second-busiest Silverbelt port after Tidewall. The harbor connects southern trade routes to northern coastal commerce.

**Economy:** Maritime trade (dominant), shipbuilding, warehousing, fishing fleet operations, trade route coordination

**Character:** Merchant prosperity. Harborpoint's location makes it essential connection point between southern waters and northern Silverbelt settlements. The city knows its geographic advantage and uses it.

**The Harbor Council:** Seven merchant houses govern collectively:
- House Wavecrest (shipping)
- House Deepwater (fishing fleet)
- House Goldstream (luxury goods)
- House Ironhull (shipbuilding)
- House Stormwatch (navigation/charts)
- House Clearport (warehousing)
- House Fairwind (coastal trade coordination)

**Relationship to Tidewall:** Professional but competitive. Tidewall is larger and has the Council of Banners' authority. Harborpoint is smaller but controls southern approaches. Both cities need each other while maneuvering for advantage.

**Current activity:** Increased trade volume from continental tensions. Ships avoiding northern routes use Harborpoint as alternative. The city is prospering while staying politically neutral.

**Notable figures:**
- **Council-Chair Maren Wavecrest** (human, 56, female) - Current council head, shrewd negotiator
- **Harbor-Master Kael Deepwater** (human, 48, male) - Manages port operations, coordinates fleet
- **Master Shipwright Senna Ironhull** (human, 62, female) - Oversees shipbuilding yards

**Geographic position:** Western peninsula south of Mournwatch, protected harbor, southern Silverbelt Coast

## Seafall (~6,000)

**Location:** Eastern peninsula, Silverbelt Coast (peninsula east of Harborpoint)  
**Ancestry:** Human majority (~55%), elf (~20%), half-elf (~15%), others (~10%)  
**Identity:** Coastal city, fishing and trade, scenic waterfall feature  
**Leadership:** Mayor-Captain Thora Clearwater (human, 51, female)

Seafall is built where a coastal river creates a dramatic waterfall into the sea — the city's namesake and most distinctive feature. The waterfall provides both beauty and practical water power for the city's mills.

**The waterfall:** Coastal river drops 80 feet directly into the sea. The mist creates rainbows on sunny days. The constant sound of falling water is the city's background. Visitors call it peaceful. Residents don't notice anymore.

**Economy:** Fishing (significant), coastal trade, milling operations (water-powered), tourism (modest - people come to see the falls)

**Character:** Smaller than Harborpoint or Vetheris, less politically ambitious, focused on comfortable prosperity rather than commercial dominance. The city knows it's beautiful and uses that for modest tourism trade.

**The mills:** Five water-powered mills use the falls for grain processing, lumber cutting, and textile work. Free power makes milling operations more profitable than inland competitors.

**Relationship to larger cities:** Seafall operates in Harborpoint and Vetheris's shadow without resentment. The city supplies both larger neighbors with milled goods and fish. They provide manufactured items Seafall can't produce. Everyone benefits.

**Current status:** Stable, prosperous in modest scale, no grand ambitions. The falls keep bringing tourists (scholars, artists, wealthy travelers). Fishing stays productive. Mills keep running. That's enough.

**Notable figures:**
- **Mayor-Captain Thora Clearwater** (human, 51, female) - Practical leader, focuses on city services not politics
- **Miller-Chief Brok Waterwise** (human, 67, male) - Coordinates five mills, master of water-power engineering
- **Fisher-Master Senna Netwise** (human, 43, female) - Fishing fleet coordinator

**Geographic position:** Eastern peninsula near Harborpoint, coastal waterfall feature, Silverbelt Coast
