# Adventure Hooks - Silverbelt Coast

---

## SUCCESSION CRISIS (5)

### 1. "Find the Heir"
**Hook:** Steward Glast secretly hires party (can't trust House agents)
**Goal:** Find Mira Caldren-Ashvale, assess her, report back honestly
**Complication:** All four Houses also searching — with agendas
**Twist:** Mira already knows she's the heir. She's been hiding from everyone.
**Level:** 2-4

### 2. "The Puppet"
**Hook:** Vethara agent approaches party — find Mira, bring her to Vethara quietly
**Goal:** Locate Mira in river towns, escort to Tidewall
**Moral choice:** Vethara wants to control her. Is helping them helping her?
**Twist:** Mira will only go if party promises she keeps her independence
**Level:** 3-5

### 3. "The Forged Document"
**Hook:** A second Caldren heir appears — documents proving legitimacy
**Employer:** House Aldric (they want Mira discredited, backed this candidate)
**Goal:** Verify or expose the documents
**Discovery:** Forged — but the forger found real documents that were even more inconvenient
**Level:** 3-5

### 4. "The Naval Service Problem"
**Hook:** Mira accepts the title — but must complete naval service first
**Employer:** Mira herself (needs trustworthy escort and advisors)
**Goal:** Survive one year of naval service with Mira (on Aldric fleet, as tradition requires)
**Threats:** Pirates, political assassination attempts, and Mira learning to lead
**Level:** 4-7

### 5. "Dryford is Leaving"
**Hook:** Dryford residents petitioning to simply leave Caldren territory and become independent
**Employer:** Town council (need outside mediation)
**Goal:** Mediate between dying town and absent House
**Stakes:** If Dryford leaves, other towns may follow — Caldren collapses faster
**Level:** 2-4

## HOUSE POLITICS (4)

### 6. "The Switching Alliance"
**Hook:** Lady Sera Mourne's secret communication with Aldric intercepted
**Employer:** Lord Cassius Vethara (furious, needs evidence before confronting Sera)
**Goal:** Verify the communications, assess Mourne's true intentions
**Complication:** Sera's reasons for switching are legitimate — Vethara overreached
**Level:** 3-5

### 7. "The Coral Secret"
**Hook:** Someone is threatening to publicly reveal Lady Sera Mourne's Coral ancestry
**Employer:** Lady Sera (frantic, paying extremely well)
**Goal:** Find the blackmailer, neutralize the threat
**Twist:** The blackmailer is a Coral elder from Silverspan Reef — wanting formal acknowledgment, not money
**Level:** 3-5

### 8. "Commander Lysa's Gambit"
**Hook:** Brennan Aldric's heir wants to secretly meet with Lumencrest representatives
**Employer:** Lysa Aldric (needs go-betweens who won't report to her father)
**Goal:** Facilitate meeting, protect Lysa's secret
**Complication:** Brennan finds out — party caught between father and heir
**Level:** 3-5

### 9. "The Council Vote"
**Hook:** Critical Council vote — Lumencrest trade mission. Vethara just needs one more vote.
**Employer:** Multiple factions bidding for party's influence
**Goal:** Navigate all four Houses, determine what each actually wants
**Stakes:** This vote shapes the next decade of regional politics
**Level:** 4-6

---

## THE SEA (4)

### 10. "The Grey Sails Problem"
**Hook:** Pirate fleet hits three ships in one week — escalating
**Employer:** Merchant consortium (Houses won't cooperate, merchants will pay)
**Goal:** Infiltrate The Grey Sails, find their base
**Complication:** Brotherhood is organized — has informants in Tidewall
**Twist:** The three ships hit were all paying Voss's cut through Salt Shore intermediaries — The Grey Sails just unknowingly attacked ships under Voss's protection. Voss knows. He hasn't decided what to do about it yet. The party is caught between two powers neither of which they fully understand.

### 11. "The Ancient Below"
**Hook:** Ship disappears without trace — no storm, no distress signal
**Employer:** Grieving captain's family (won't accept "lost at sea")
**Goal:** Investigate disappearance
**Discovery:** Ancient sea creature (enormous, solitary, territorial) has woken. Not evil — just present.
**Decision:** Warn shipping, negotiate routes around it, or try to drive it deeper?
**Level:** 5-8

### 12. "The Reef Attack"
**Hook:** Silverspan Reef community sends distress — Grey Sails pirates using reef as base
**Employer:** Coral elder from reef
**Goal:** Remove pirates from reef without destroying Coral community
**Complication:** Pirates have hostages. And they know about Lady Sera's ancestry.
**Level:** 4-6

### 13. "The Naval Service"
**Hook:** Party needs passage — only option is serving on a House ship temporarily
**Employer:** Whichever House ship they can get onto
**Goal:** Complete a patrol circuit, survive naval politics
**Encounters:** Pirate skirmish, discovering smuggling by ship's officer, navigating rank vs. survival
**Level:** 2-5

---

## RIVER TOWNS (4)

### 14. "The Copper Poisoning"
**Hook:** Coppergate residents getting sick — river more toxic than usual
**Employer:** Town council
**Goal:** Travel upstream, find source of increased toxicity
**Discovery:** Illegal mining operation dumping waste upstream (in [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Central_Heartlands_Index|Central Heartlands]])
**Complication:** Mining operation is Lumencrest-affiliated
**Level:** 2-4

### 15. "The Dying River"
**Hook:** Dryford's river is nearly gone — desperate mayor asks for help
**Employer:** Mayor of Dryford
**Goal:** Investigate why river is failing faster than others
**Mystery:** Something upstream in the Heartlands is diverting water — but what and why?
**Level:** 3-5

### 16. "The Goblin Artisans"
**Hook:** Coppergate's Goblin metalworkers have produced something unusual — accidentally tapped into Hollostar shard in copper deposit
**Employer:** Goblin elder (frightened, doesn't understand what they found)
**Goal:** Assess the shard, help Goblins safely handle it
**Complication:** Lumencrest wants it. So does [[04_Factions/Starfall Seekers|Starfall Seekers]]. Goblins just want to go back to making pots.
**Level:** 3-5

### 17. "Mira's Route"
**Hook:** River pilot Mira Caldren-Ashvale quietly asks for escort (she knows someone's following her)
**Employer:** Mira herself (pays in river knowledge — will show party every secret the Oldrun holds)
**Goal:** Protect Mira on her regular route, identify who's watching her
**Revelation:** Multiple Houses have watchers — party must decide who to trust
**Level:** 2-4

---

**Levels:** 2-4 (river towns, finding Mira, social navigation) · 3-5 (House politics, Coral stories, river mysteries) · 4-6 (pirates, Council votes, succession) · 5-8 (ancient sea creature, major naval operations)
