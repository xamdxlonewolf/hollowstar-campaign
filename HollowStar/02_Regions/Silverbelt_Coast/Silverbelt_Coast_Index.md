# Silverbelt Coast

> *"Service before inheritance. Salt before silver. The sea decides who leads."*

---

**Climate:** Temperate coastal, mild  
**Population:** ~55,000  
**Major City:** [[02_Regions/Silverbelt_Coast/Locations/Cities/Tidewall|Tidewall]] (~18,000) — fortified port  
**Government:** Four Noble Houses (Council of Banners)  
**Crisis:** House Caldren succession — no clear heir, power vacuum

---

## Core Identity

Old power; noble families; every heir must complete naval service before inheriting. Tidewall anchors the north; river towns are the heart of daily life. House Caldren dying — who absorbs their territories?
