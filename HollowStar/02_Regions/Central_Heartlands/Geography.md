# Geography - Central Heartlands

## Landscapes & Terrain

**The Great Plains (Western & Central):**
- Endless farmland stretching to horizons
- Rolling hills (not flat—gentle undulations)
- Rich dark soil (Hollostar-infused, visibly darker than normal earth)
- Dotted with farmsteads, villages, irrigation channels
- **The Grain Sea:** Local name for the western plains during harvest

**River Valleys:**
- **The Silverthreads:** Network of rivers crisscrossing the region
- Largest: **The Hearthflow River** (runs north-south through Lumencrest)
- Provide irrigation, transportation, trade routes

**Eastern Mountains (The Ironspine):**
- Not tall peaks (more like large hills/small mountains)
- Rich in iron, copper, silver, coal
- [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Locations/Cities/Ironhold|Ironhold]] (Karak-Tharum) built into mountainside)

**Scattered Forests:**
- **Thornwood** (north): Dense, dark
- **Ember Grove** (south): Autumn colors year-round

## Climate & Weather

**Four Distinct Seasons:**
- **Spring:** Mild, rainy, planting season
- **Summer:** Warm, dry, growing season
- **Autumn:** Harvest season, pleasant
- **Winter:** Cold but not brutal (compared to [[02_Regions/Frostwell_Tundra/Frostwell_Tundra_Index|Frostwell Tundra]])

**Recently:** Weather increasingly unpredictable (earlier/later seasons, temperature swings, irregular precipitation)

## Natural Resources

**Agricultural:** Grains, vegetables, fruits, livestock. Ancient Hollostar shards enriched soil; yields declining as shards weaken (~15–20% over past decade).

**Mineral (Ironhold):** Iron, copper, silver, coal, stone.

**Hollostar Shards:** Scattered throughout farmland; Lumencrest hoards most; black market thriving.

## Flora & Fauna

**Unique:** Starwheat, Emberleaf Trees, Starlings (iridescent birds, omen-watchers).

**Transformed Creatures:** Glimmerrats, Thornwood Prowlers, Starblind Crows, Ironspine Grubs.
