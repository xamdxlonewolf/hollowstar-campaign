# Accord Coastal Towns

The smaller coastal settlements of the western Central Heartlands, connecting the Accord to maritime trade routes.

---

## Southmere

**Population:** ~1,800  
**Location:** Southern coast, south of Kingsrest (not on Hearthflow River)  
**Identity:** Trade port, coastal commerce, Kingsrest's maritime connection

Southmere sits on the southern coast where the Central Heartlands meets the gulf. The town serves as Kingsrest's primary maritime trade connection — goods flowing south from the royal capital are transferred to ships here for coastal and gulf trade.

**Economy:** Port operations, warehousing, shipbuilding (small scale), trade facilitation between land routes and sea routes

**Relationship to Kingsrest:** Officially under Kingsrest authority, practically independent in daily operations. King Aldric IV appoints the harbor-master, but the merchant community runs itself.

**Current status:** Benefiting from increased maritime trade, some tension with Keeper's Bay (Salt Shore) over trade route competition

**Notable figure:** Harbor-Master Bren Southwell (human, 52) — King's appointee, competent administrator, balances royal interests with merchant realities

**Geographic position:** Southern coast, south of Kingsrest, coastal settlement without major river connection

## Ironmouth

**Population:** ~2,100  
**Location:** Eastern coast, at mouth of river flowing from Ironspine Mountains to gulf  
**Identity:** Industrial port, receives Ironspine goods, metalworking, trade

Ironmouth exists where an Ironspine mountain river (the **Copperrun River**) reaches the gulf. The town receives metal goods, ore, and industrial products from Ironspine operations (including Ironhold and Coppertown) and ships them coastwise or refines them further.

**The river:** Copperrun River flows from eastern Ironspine Mountains south to the gulf, passing through or near mining operations before reaching Ironmouth. The river is heavily used for bulk ore transport.

**Economy:** Metal refining (secondary processing of Ironspine ore), port operations, industrial warehousing, metalworking, shipment of metals to other regions

**Character:** Working-class, industrial, less refined than other Accord towns. Smelting smoke, forge heat, dock workers, pragmatic people doing hard work.

**Relationship to Ironhold:** Unofficial extension of Ironhold's economic sphere. Many residents are human workers connected to dwarven mining operations upstream. Some Ironhold families maintain trade factors here.

**Notable figure:** Dock-Master Kael Ironhand (dwarf, 156) — Represents Ironhold interests, coordinates metal shipments, respected by the working community

**Geographic position:** Eastern coast where Copperrun River (from Ironspine Mountains) meets the gulf, industrial port

## Clearhaven

**Population:** ~1,600  
**Location:** Southern coast, south of Hazelhaven, at mouth of river from Coppertown  
**Identity:** Fishing and farming community affected by upstream industrial pollution

Clearhaven sits at the mouth of the **Ashflow River** which flows from the southern Ironspine Mountains (passing Coppertown) to the gulf. The town traditionally thrived on fishing and riverbank farming. Currently struggling with pollution from Coppertown's upstream mining and smelting operations.

**The pollution problem:** Coppertown's industrial waste enters the Ashflow River. By the time the river reaches Clearhaven, the water carries metallic residue, smelting runoff, and industrial contamination. Fish populations declining. Crops grown with river water showing problems. Community health concerns.

**The dispute:** Clearhaven has petitioned Hazelhaven (nearest Accord seat) multiple times for intervention. Coppertown claims they're following all regulations (technically true — regulations are minimal). Hazelhaven is caught between environmental concerns and economic interests (Coppertown's metal production matters).

**Current status:** Strained. Clearhaven fishing fleet shrinking. Younger residents leaving. The community feels abandoned by the Accord system that's supposed to protect them.

**Hazelhaven's position:** Accord-Speaker Maret Dunfor has been charged with mediating. She's sympathetic but Hazelhaven has its own crisis (irrigation failure). The pollution dispute is stalled while Hazelhaven deals with immediate survival.

**Notable figures:**
- **Elder Maren Clearwater** (human, 68, female) — Community leader, persistent advocate, increasingly frustrated
- **Fisher-Captain Torn Netmender** (human, 45, male) — Leads declining fishing fleet, documenting catch reduction
- **Healer Senna** (halfling, 52, female) — Treating increased illness, suspects water contamination, collecting evidence

**Geographic position:** Southern coast, south of Hazelhaven, at mouth of Ashflow River (flows from southern Ironspine, past Coppertown, to gulf)
