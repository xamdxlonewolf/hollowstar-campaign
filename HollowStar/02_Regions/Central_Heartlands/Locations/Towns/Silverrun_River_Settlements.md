# Silverrun River Settlements

The settlements along the Silverrun River, which flows from the western Ironspine Mountains to the southwestern coast.

---

## THE SILVERRUN RIVER

The **Silverrun River** flows from the western Ironspine Mountains (Ironhold region) southwest to the coast, where it meets the gulf near Ironmouth. The river is named for the silver-rich runoff from Ironhold's mining operations - the water literally carries trace amounts of silver that give it a distinctive shimmer in sunlight.

**Course:** Originates in western Ironspine Mountains → flows through Deepforge → southwest through foothills and plains → reaches coast at Rivermouth → empties near Ironmouth

**Character:** Cold, fast in the mountains, calmer in the plains, carries mineral-rich water from mining operations

**Use:** Ore transport (barges from Deepforge to coast), irrigation (limited - mineral content), trade route

## Deepforge (Khazad-Celeb)

**Population:** ~3,500  
**Location:** Western Ironspine Mountains, upper Silverrun River, Accord territory  
**Dwarven name:** Khazad-Celeb ("Silver-hall"); **Common name:** Deepforge  
**Ancestry:** Dwarven majority (~80%), human (~15%), others (~5%)  
**Identity:** Mountain dwarven settlement, mining and metalworking, Ironhold's western neighbor

Deepforge (or **Khazad-Celeb** in the dwarven tongue) is a dwarven mining settlement in the western Ironspine Mountains, built where rich silver and iron veins meet the upper Silverrun River. The settlement exists in Ironhold's sphere of influence but maintains distinct identity.

**Relationship to Ironhold:** Sister settlement, not subordinate. Deepforge dwarves are from different clans than Ironhold's major families. The two settlements cooperate on mining coordination, trade, and defense, but Deepforge governs itself.

**The settlement:**
- Carved into mountainside (typical dwarven construction)
- Two levels: Upper Forges (metalworking) and Deep Mines (extraction)
- Uses the Silverrun for ore transport and forge cooling
- Residential quarters inside the mountain

**Economy:**
- **Silver mining** (primary) - Rich veins make Deepforge valuable
- **Iron mining** (secondary) - Less than Ironhold but significant
- **Metalworking** - Dwarven smiths produce quality tools, weapons, trade goods
- **Ore transport** - Barges carry ore down Silverrun to Rivermouth for coastal shipping

**Clan structure:** Three clans - Silverbeard (miners), Ironhammer (smiths), Stonehold (governance/defense). The **Clan Council** of three leaders makes decisions by consensus.

**Current Clan Leaders:**
- **Thane Boren Silverbeard** (dwarf, 198, male) - Elder, conservative, mining expert
- **Master Thora Ironhammer** (dwarf, 167, female) - Master smith, practical, trade-focused  
- **Captain Drak Stonehold** (dwarf, 189, male) - Defense, coordination with Ironhold

**Relationship to the Accord:** Acknowledges Accord authority (technically part of Accord territory) but practically autonomous. Pays taxes through Ironhold. Not represented directly at Accord council.

**Culture:** Traditional dwarven - work is honor, clan is family, stone is eternal. Slightly less rigid than Ironhold (younger settlement, only 300 years old vs Ironhold's greater age).

**Current activity:** Increased silver demand (Lumencrest wants it, Rivermark wants it for comparison to Starsilver, everyone wants it). Deepforge is prospering but stretched for labor.

**Geographic position:**
- Western Ironspine Mountains, upper Silverrun River
- West/southwest of Ironhold (sister settlement in same mountain range)
- Mountain position, carved into stone
- Upper river location (ore flows downstream from here)

## Rivermouth

**Population:** ~1,800  
**Location:** Southwestern coast, at mouth of Silverrun River, near Ironmouth  
**Identity:** River port town, ore receiving, works with Ironmouth operations

Rivermouth sits where the Silverrun River meets the southwestern coast, functioning as the receiving port for ore shipped down from Deepforge and Ironhold's western operations. The town works closely with nearby Ironmouth (larger industrial port).

**The relationship to Ironmouth:** Rivermouth handles river barges arriving from upstream. Ore is unloaded, sorted, and either processed locally (small-scale smelting) or transferred to Ironmouth's larger facilities for major refining and coastal shipping. The two towns are essentially one economic unit with separate names.

**Economy:**
- **Ore receiving** (primary) - Barges from Deepforge arrive here
- **Small-scale smelting** - Initial ore processing before transfer to Ironmouth
- **River port operations** - Warehousing, barge maintenance, transport coordination
- **Support services** - Supplies Ironmouth workforce (housing, food, services)

**Population:** Human majority (~60%), dwarven (~25%, connected to upstream operations), others (~15%)

**Character:** Working-class, practical, less industrial than Ironmouth proper. River workers, bargemen, warehouse staff, supporting families.

**Current Harbor-Master:** **Kael Riverstone** (human, 48, male) - Coordinates river barge traffic, manages ore receiving

**Geographic position:**
- Southwestern coast, at Silverrun River mouth
- Immediately adjacent to/near Ironmouth (larger industrial port)
- River mouth location where barges arrive from upstream
- Connected to Deepforge via river route

## IRONMOUTH (Description Correction)

**Population:** ~2,100  
**Location:** Southwestern coast, near Silverrun River mouth  
**Identity:** Industrial port, metal refining, coastal shipping

**CORRECTED DESCRIPTION:** Ironmouth is the southwestern coast's primary industrial port, receiving metal ore from the **western Ironspine Mountains** via the **Silverrun River** (delivered to nearby Rivermouth, then transferred here). The town specializes in metal refining, industrial processing, and shipping refined metals to other coastal settlements.

**The Silverrun connection:** Ore mined at Deepforge and Ironhold's western operations flows down the Silverrun River to Rivermouth. From there it's transferred to Ironmouth's larger smelting facilities for major refining operations.

**Economy:** Metal refining (large-scale smelting), industrial warehousing, metalworking, shipment of refined metals to other regions

**Character:** Working-class, industrial, smelting smoke, forge heat, dock workers, pragmatic people doing hard work.

**Relationship to Ironhold:** Ironmouth processes ore from Ironhold's western sphere (Deepforge and related operations). Many residents have connections to dwarven mining families upstream.

**Notable figure:** **Dock-Master Kael Ironhand** (dwarf, 156) - Represents Ironhold/Deepforge interests, coordinates metal shipments, respected by working community

**Geographic position:** 
- Southwestern coast (NOT eastern coast as previously written - CORRECTED)
- Near Silverrun River mouth (works with Rivermouth for ore receiving)
- Industrial port position
- Receives western Ironspine goods via Silverrun River
