# Rivermark Sovereignty - Towns

The smaller settlements of the Rivermark Sovereignty, supporting the major cities and filling the territory between them.

---

## Northflow Landing

**Population:** ~3,200  
**Location:** Northflow River, between Meadowvale and Twinwater  
**Identity:** River port, timber operations, trade waystation

Northflow Landing sits on the Northflow River roughly halfway between Meadowvale (northern valleys) and Twinwater (confluence capital). The town serves as river port and timber operations center — logs floated down from northern forests are processed here before continuing to Twinwater.

**Economy:** Timber milling, river port services, warehousing for agricultural goods from Meadowvale

**Population:** Mixed (elven ~45%, human ~30%, Faun ~15%, others ~10%)

**Geographic position:** Northflow River, between Meadowvale (north) and Twinwater (south)

---

## Southflow Crossing

**Population:** ~2,800  
**Location:** Southflow River, primary ford crossing  
**Identity:** Ferry operations, trade waystation, bridge town

Southflow Crossing exists because geography created a chokepoint — the primary ford across the Southflow River before it reaches Twinwater. The town operates ferry services and provides resupply for caravans traveling between Flamekeep, Twinwater, and southern regions.

**Economy:** Ferry tolls, inn/tavern services for travelers, wagon repair, stabling

**Population:** Mixed (human ~50%, elven ~25%, Drakona ~15%, others ~10%)

**Geographic position:** Southflow River, south of Twinwater, between Twinwater and Flamekeep

---

## Silverdeep

**Population:** ~2,400  
**Location:** Eastern Ironspine foothills, mining town  
**Identity:** Mining operations, Starsilver support, mountain community

Silverdeep is a mining town in the eastern Ironspine foothills that supports Flamekeep's operations. While Flamekeep controls the primary Starsilver veins, Silverdeep mines other metals (tin, zinc, rare earth elements) and houses workers who support the broader mining industry.

**Economy:** Mining (non-Starsilver metals), smelting, support services for Flamekeep operations

**Population:** Drakona majority (~55%), mixed others (~45%) — smaller Drakona community connected to Flamekeep

**Geographic position:** Eastern Ironspine Mountains foothills, supporting Flamekeep (which is higher in mountains)

---

## Greenwatch

**Population:** ~1,800  
**Location:** River valley between Meadowvale and forests  
**Identity:** Faun village, medicinal plant cultivation, seasonal gathering site

Greenwatch is a smaller Faun-majority village that specializes in medicinal plant cultivation using practices even more traditional than Meadowvale's. The village tends specific groves where rare healing plants grow, harvesting them with ritual care and preserving ancient Faun knowledge.

**Economy:** Medicinal plants (specialized varieties), herbal preparation, seasonal guide services

**Population:** Faun majority (~85%), scattered elven (~10%), others (~5%)

**Cultural note:** Greenwatch is more traditional than Meadowvale — some practices here are maintained specifically because they predate Sovereignty formation

**Geographic position:** River valleys north/northwest of Meadowvale, approaching forested regions

---

## Stonebridge

**Population:** ~1,600  
**Location:** Mountain pass connecting eastern and western Heartlands  
**Identity:** Bridge town, mountain pass waystation, connects Rivermark to western Accord

Stonebridge sits at a crucial mountain pass through the Ironspine Mountains — the primary route connecting Rivermark Sovereignty territory (east) with western Central Heartlands (Accord territory). The town exists to maintain the bridge/pass and serve travelers crossing between the two regions.

**Economy:** Pass tolls, waystation services (food, lodging, animal care), bridge/road maintenance

**Population:** Mixed (roughly equal human, elven, dwarven from both sides of mountains)

**Strategic importance:** This pass is how Rivermark and Accord maintain overland connection when not using river routes

**Geographic position:** Ironspine Mountains pass, connects eastern Rivermark to western Heartlands (Accord cities)
