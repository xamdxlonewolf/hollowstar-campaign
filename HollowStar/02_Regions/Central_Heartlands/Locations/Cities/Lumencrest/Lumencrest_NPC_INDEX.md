Cross-reference individual district documents for full profiles._

## HOW TO USE THIS INDEX

**Arc Relevance Tags:**

- 🔴 **CRITICAL** — directly drives or resolves a major Act One thread
- 🟠 **SIGNIFICANT** — involved in a thread, provides key information, or shapes a faction relationship
- 🟡 **USEFUL** — provides information, access, or resources that support player activity
- ⚪ **COMMUNITY** — gives the city texture, may develop into something more
