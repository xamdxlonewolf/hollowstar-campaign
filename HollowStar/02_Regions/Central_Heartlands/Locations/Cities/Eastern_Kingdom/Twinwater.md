# Twinwater

**Population:** ~19,000 | **Region:** [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Central_Heartlands_Index|Central Heartlands]] - [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/The_Rivermark_Sovereignty|Rivermark Sovereignty]]  
**Location:** At the Silverfork confluence where Northflow and Southflow rivers meet  
**Government:** Confluence Council seat  
**Identity:** Elven-Faun capital built at sacred river confluence

---

## THE CITY

Twinwater exists because geography demanded it. The city sits at the precise point where the Northflow and Southflow rivers merge to form the Silverfork — a confluence considered sacred by both the original elven settlers and the Faun who joined them. The city is built on both banks and the delta island formed between the merging waters.

**The Three Districts:**

**Northbank** — Elven quarter, government buildings, Confluence Council Hall
- Carved stone architecture in elven style
- The Council Hall: Built around a living silveroak tree (900+ years old)
- Libraries, academies, administrative offices
- Residential areas for elven families (multi-generational homes)

**Southbank** — Faun quarter, markets, agricultural coordination, seasonal festivals
- Organic architecture that flows with the land
- The Seasonal Pavilions: Four open-air structures for seasonal gatherings
- Markets selling Faun-cultivated goods, medicinal plants, river valley produce
- Living roofs, gardens integrated into every structure

**The Delta** — Mixed district, trade hub, docks, warehouse district
- Where the city's commerce happens
- River docks handle trade goods flowing east/west
- Warehouse district stores goods in transit
- Most diverse population (elves, Faun, Drakona traders, human merchants, others)
- The Confluence Plaza: Public square where all three districts meet, major agreements sealed here

## THE CONFLUENCE

The merging of Northflow and Southflow is not just geography — it's the symbolic and spiritual heart of the Sovereignty. The Confluence Council takes its name from this place. Major treaties are signed here. Seasonal ceremonies happen at the water's edge.

**The sacred ground:** A natural amphitheater where the rivers meet. Accessible to all. Used for public assemblies, religious ceremonies (multiple traditions respected), celebrations, and the rare moments when the entire city gathers.

**River ceremony tradition:** Important agreements are "witnessed by the rivers" — participants wade into the confluence and speak their oaths while the waters flow around them. The symbolism: like the two rivers becoming one, separate parties become united in purpose.

---

## ECONOMY

**Trade hub:** Goods moving between western Heartlands and Starfall Basin pass through Twinwater. The city takes modest taxes and provides warehousing, security, and trade facilitation.

**River port:** Flat-bottomed barges can navigate both the Northflow and Southflow. Twinwater's docks are the largest on either river. Shipbuilding industry supports the river trade.

**Administrative center:** As capital, Twinwater houses Sovereignty bureaucracy. Civil servants, record-keepers, tax collectors, diplomats.

**Crafts:** Elven woodworking (musical instruments from river valley hardwoods), Faun textile weaving (river reed patterns), mixed workshops producing quality goods for export.

---

## POPULATION

**Elven majority** (~60%): Most are descendants of the original settlers 287 years ago. Multi-generational families. Long memories and long-term thinking.

**Faun presence** (~25%): Integrated over generations. Seasonal cycles deeply influence city rhythms. Agricultural expertise and river cultivation knowledge.

**Drakona traders** (~5%): Permanent representatives from Flamekeep plus traders passing through. Respected minority.

**Others** (~10%): Humans (mostly merchants), halflings (mostly Delta district), scattered ancestries. The capital attracts diversity.

## THE CONFLUENCE COUNCIL

The Council Hall sits on Northbank but the Council itself represents the entire Sovereignty. When in session, the 17 members (12 River Voices, 4 Seasonal Speakers, 1 Stone Seat) meet in a circular chamber around the ancient silveroak tree.

**Current Facilitator:** **Mirelen Starweaver** (elf, 347, female) manages proceedings with practiced patience. Serving second 3-year term. Universally respected. Carefully steering the Sixth Seat campaign without forcing confrontation.

**Notable River Voices:**
- **Theron Brightwater** (elf, 412, male) — Twinwater's own representative, eldest Council member, cautious on Sixth Seat
- **Meadowsong** (Faun, 87, non-binary) — Meadowvale representative, strong Sixth Seat supporter
- **Emberscale** (Drakona, 156, female) — Eastmarch representative, pragmatic, watching Basin carefully

**The Stone Seat:** **Ironheart** (Drakona, 203, male) from Flamekeep, has held the seat for 47 years, deeply respected, protective of Starsilver interests.

## CURRENT SITUATION

**The Sixth Seat campaign:** Twinwater is campaign headquarters. Mirelen coordinates lobbying efforts to the Accord cities. Delegations travel west regularly. Patience is maintained but younger Council members grow restless.

**Trade prosperity:** The city is economically successful. River commerce is increasing. Starfall Basin merchant interest means more trade volume and higher revenues. Some question why Sixth Seat membership matters when prosperity already exists.

**Cultural integration:** After 135 years of alliance, Twinwater has significant cultural exchange with western Heartlands. Common language (mostly), shared festivals (some), intermarriage (especially with Hazelhaven border communities now that it's moving west). The formal separation feels arbitrary to many residents.

**The Basin question:** Merchant Circle representatives visit Twinwater frequently, making generous offers for preferential trade terms. So far the Sovereignty honors Accord partnerships, but the offers keep improving and some merchants wonder if alliance loyalty is costing them profit.

## GEOGRAPHIC POSITION FOR MAP

**Position relative to other settlements:**
- West of Eastmarch (border city)
- Northeast of Flamekeep (mountain city)
- South of Meadowvale (northern river valleys)
- East of Ironspine Mountains (western Heartlands beyond)

- Northflow River comes from north
- Southflow River comes from south

- Silverfork continues east toward Starfall Basin

**The confluence is the defining geographic feature** — city built around this point
