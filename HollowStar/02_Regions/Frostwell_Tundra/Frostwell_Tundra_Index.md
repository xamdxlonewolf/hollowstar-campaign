# Frostwell Tundra

> *"We have lived here through worse than cold. We are not sure we have lived through worse than this."*

---

**Climate:** Arctic, brutal  
**Population:** ~18,000 (Nine Hearths ~12,000, Dwarves ~3,500, Drakona ~1,500)  
**Government:** The Nine Hearths Confederation (9 villages)  
**Crisis:** Hollostar approaching — estimated 1–2 years. Confederation divided: stay or flee.

---

## Core Identity

Northern frozen wastes. Three peoples: **The Nine Hearths** (nine villages, human and Giant), **the Dwarves** (mountain mines), **the Drakona** (settlement at the Ember Cradle ruins). Ancient ruins preserved in ice across the tundra.

**The Approaching Star:** Every astronomer agrees on the target. The Nine Hearths is splitting into Stayers vs. Leavers. Outsiders arriving: Lumencrest researchers, Starfall Seekers, Path of Spirits pilgrims.
