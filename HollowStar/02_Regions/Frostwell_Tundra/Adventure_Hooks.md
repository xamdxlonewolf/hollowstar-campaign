# Adventure Hooks - Frostwell Tundra

---

## THE STAR CRISIS (5)

### 1. "The Deciding Vote"
**Hook:** Frostmeet's Hearthspeaker Mava needs two more votes to pass evacuation — Warmrock is leaderless
**Employer:** Mava (quietly — can't be seen lobbying)
**Goal:** Travel to Warmrock, help them choose a new elder, influence the vote
**Complication:** Three candidates — one Stayer, one Leaver, one genuinely undecided but corruptible
**Level:** 3-5

### 2. "Coldfast's Children"
**Hook:** Young people leaving Coldfast in secret — Elder Durn is losing his village without a vote
**Employer:** A leaving family (need escort south before Durn finds out and stops them)
**Goal:** Escort group to [[02_Regions/Northern_Expanse/Northern_Expanse_Index|Northern Expanse]] border safely
**Complication:** Durn sends trackers. And a blizzard is coming. And one family member refuses to leave.
**Level:** 3-5

### 3. "The Calculations"
**Hook:** Icewatch's observational data conflicts with Lumencrest's calculations — one says 14 months, one says 26
**Employer:** [[04_Factions/Starfall Seekers|Starfall Seekers]] (need the discrepancy resolved — everything depends on which is right)
**Goal:** Gather Icewatch data, verify it, determine which calculation is correct
**Stakes:** If Lumencrest is wrong, people evacuating now will survive. If Icewatch is wrong, people staying will die.
**Level:** 4-6

### 4. "Sponsored Relocation"
**Hook:** Grayspire has been secretly negotiating with Lumencrest — Lumencrest will fund relocation in exchange for excavation rights to the Sunken Library
**Employer:** Hearthspeaker Mava (this deal will fracture the confederation if it goes through)
**Goal:** Investigate the deal's full terms, determine if Lumencrest will actually honor it
**Twist:** The deal is real and fair. The question becomes: is it the right thing to do?
**Level:** 3-5

### 5. "The Evacuation Route"
**Hook:** The main southern route is blocked — a glacier calved, new crevasse field impassable
**Employer:** Thornholt (they need a new route south, urgently)
**Goal:** Scout alternate routes through the tundra to Northern Expanse
**Complication:** The alternate routes pass through contested territory — Dwarven mining lands and areas near Ember Cradle
**Level:** 3-5

## THE ANCIENT RUINS (5)

### 6. "The Sunken Library"
**Hook:** A Ribbet diver who visited Frostmeet managed to reach the Library bottom — brought up one tablet before running out of air
**Employer:** Starfall Seekers (the tablet mentions something about "the return" — need more)
**Goal:** Arrange deeper dive, retrieve tablets
**Complication:** Mava's prohibition is real — she knows something about what's in the Library that she's not sharing
**Level:** 4-6

### 7. "The Warming Cradle"
**Hook:** Flame-Keeper Sorith realizes she can't hide the Ember Cradle's warming much longer — needs advice
**Employer:** Sorith (approaches party secretly, outside Ashkeep)
**Goal:** Help Sorith understand what's happening without triggering panic or outsider exploitation
**Discovery:** The sealed lower chambers are... responding. To what? That's the question.
**Level:** 5-7

### 8. "The Watchtower Alignment"
**Hook:** Starfall Seekers realize the two different towers in the Watchtower Fields align with the Hollostar's exact approach vector — someone planned this
**Employer:** Seekers (need party to protect them while they investigate — locals increasingly hostile to outsiders)
**Goal:** Document the alignment, search the towers for anything inside
**Discovery:** Something inside one tower — a sealed room, no door, with contents visible through a crack in the stone
**Level:** 4-6

### 9. "Varra's Secret"
**Hook:** Deep-Keeper Varra Ironmantle of the Greywall Dwarves is dying — wants to pass on what she knows about "the old work" before she goes
**Employer:** Varra herself (doesn't trust anyone in her clan to use it wisely)
**Goal:** Travel into Greywall mountains, reach Varra, receive the knowledge
**Complication:** Her clan doesn't know she's doing this — they'll try to stop you leaving with the information
**Level:** 5-7

### 10. "The Hollow Circle"
**Hook:** Something is growing inside the Hollow Circle — a structure, emerging from the bare ground
**Employer:** Emberveil village elder (frightened, Path of Spirits pilgrims ecstatic — combination concerning)
**Goal:** Investigate the structure, determine if it's dangerous
**Level:** 4-6

## COMMUNITY STORIES (3)

### 11. "The Last Winter"
**Hook:** A family in Saltrime — grandmother insists on staying for "one last proper winter" before the evacuation
**Employer:** The family (need someone to watch over her, or convince her, or just be there)
**Goal:** Spend a winter in Saltrime with this woman — protect, accompany, maybe understand why she won't leave
**Note:** This is a quiet, character-focused hook. No combat required. Profoundly sad potential.
**Level:** Any

### 12. "The Aurora Message"
**Hook:** Icewatch villagers believe the aurora is showing unusual patterns — spelling something
**Employer:** Icewatch elder (can't read it — needs someone with different knowledge)
**Goal:** Document the aurora patterns, determine if they're natural or meaningful
**Discovery:** Partial message — in the same symbolic language as the ruins. Someone is communicating.
**Level:** 3-5

### 13. "The Path of Spirits Problem"
**Hook:** Path of Spirits pilgrims in Emberveil are claiming the ruins are theirs and blocking Drakona access to the Hollow Circle
**Employer:** Either Emberveil elder (mediate) or Ashkeep Drakona (remove them)
**Goal:** Resolve the territorial dispute before it becomes violence
**Complication:** The pilgrims have a genuinely compelling spiritual argument. So do the Drakona. Both are right.
**Level:** 2-4

---

## OUTSIDER INTRIGUE (2)

### 14. "The Lumencrest Agenda"
**Hook:** Lumencrest's research team leader has been sending encoded messages back to the Expansionist faction — what are they planning?
**Employer:** Starfall Seekers (suspicious of Lumencrest's real motives)
**Goal:** Intercept or decode the messages, determine if Lumencrest plans to exploit the impact
**Discovery:** Lumencrest plans to be at the impact site — they believe the Hollostar's shard field will be the largest ever recorded. They want first access.
**Level:** 4-6

### 15. "Before It Hits"
**Hook:** Someone wants the party at Ember Cradle when the star hits — an anonymous message, prepaid guide, no explanation
**Employer:** Unknown
**Goal:** Follow the invitation, be at the Ember Cradle at the moment of impact
**This is the capstone hook — designed for the climax of a Frostwell arc**
**Level:** 7-9

---

**Levels:** 2-4 (community, mediation) · 3-5 (stay/flee, ruins, intrigue) · 4-7 (deep ruins, Warming Cradle, Varra) · 7-9 (the impact)
