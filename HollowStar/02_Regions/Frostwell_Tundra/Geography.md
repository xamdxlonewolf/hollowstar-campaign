# Geography - Frostwell Tundra

**Climate:** Frigid year-round, brutal winters, brief cool summers, permafrost beneath surface  
**Terrain:** Open tundra (north/central), southern mountain range, eastern coastal areas, scattered boreal forest pockets  
**Population:** ~18,000 (rapidly declining due to evacuation)  
**Crisis:** Projected Hollostar impact zone — evacuation ongoing, some communities refusing to leave

---

## THE IMPACT ZONE

The northern/central tundra is projected impact zone for the incoming fourth Hollostar. Somnarch calculations and Starfall Seeker projections agree — the star will land somewhere in northern Frostwell within 2-5 years.

**Effect radius:** Estimated 200-300 mile devastation zone from impact point, with catastrophic environmental changes extending far beyond

**Evacuation status:** Approximately 60% of population has fled south through Silver Pass to Northern Expanse. Remaining 40% either voted to stay (Coldfast, Frostvale, Whitepeak, Frostridge, The Last Hearth) or are still evacuating (Snowmere ongoing)

---

## THE NINE HEARTHS TERRITORY

The traditional human/Giant/mixed-ancestry settlements scattered across the tundra. Once ~14,000 population combined, now divided between those who left and those who stayed.

**Locations (west to east across map):**
- Iceholm (northwestern)
- Snowmere (western)
- Frostridge (southern mountains)
- Whitepeak (central-southern mountains)
- Winterhold (southern interior)
- The Last Hearth (northeastern coast)
- Longmarch (eastern interior)
- Coldfast (eastern interior - largest)
- Frostvale (eastern coastal)

---

## THE SOUTHERN MOUNTAINS

Mountain range separating Frostwell from Northern Expanse. Not impassable — Silver Pass/Celebrant provides primary crossing.

**Silver Pass / Celebrant:** Five-day journey in good conditions, deadly in winter storms. Ancient elvish waymarkers guide the route. Three shelters provide emergency refuge. Currently seeing unprecedented refugee traffic flowing south.

**Dwarven holds:** Three holds carved deep into southern mountains - all staying, believing mountain stone will protect them.

---

## THE EASTERN TERRITORIES

Eastern tundra and coastal areas where Drakona communities are concentrated. More mobile than humans or dwarves, most Drakona evacuated early.

---

## ANCIENT RUINS

Pre-Hollostar structures scattered across Frostwell — evidence of civilizations that existed before the first star fell 10,000 years ago.

**The Shattered Spire** - Northwestern tundra, tower fragment jutting from permafrost

**Frostvault** - Central tundra, buried complex partially excavated, most remains underground

**The Sleeping Gate** - Near projected impact zone, massive arch of unknown material, has survived 10,000 years without weathering

---

## TERRAIN TYPES

**Open Tundra (North/Central):** Flat, exposed, permafrost beneath thin topsoil, brutal wind. Impact zone.

**Southern Mountains:** Steep, snow-covered, dwarven holds carved within. Natural barrier.

**Eastern Coastal:** Rocky coastline, slightly warmer, Drakona communities, frigid water fishing.

**Boreal Forest Patches:** Scattered hardy trees in protected valleys and southern regions.

**Permafrost:** Permanently frozen ground below surface layer everywhere.

---

## CLIMATE & SURVIVAL

**Winter (8 months):** Well below freezing, deadly storms, travel dangerous

**Summer (4 months):** Above-freezing briefly, tundra blooms, frantic food gathering

**Survival requirement:** Every community must store 8+ months of food or starve

---

## CURRENT STATUS

**Total remaining:** ~10,500 residents refusing to leave or unable to evacuate

**Timeline:** 2-5 years until projected impact
