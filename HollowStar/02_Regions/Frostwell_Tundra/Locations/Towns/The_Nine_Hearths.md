# The Nine Hearths

The nine traditional tundra settlements — human, Giant, and mixed-ancestry communities that have survived Frostwell's brutal climate for generations.

---

## THE VOTE

Each hearth held a vote: stay or leave.

**Voted to leave (4):** Longmarch, Iceholm, Winterhold, Snowmere  
**Voted to stay (5):** Coldfast, Frostvale, Whitepeak, Frostridge, The Last Hearth

---

## STAYING HEARTHS

### Coldfast (~1,800)
Largest hearth, 73% voted stay. Elder Durn Frostborn leads. Determination without denial.

### Frostvale (~1,200)
Eastern coast, 58% voted stay. Headwoman Kira Seawatch. Fishing supplements hunting.

### Whitepeak (~900)
Southern mountains, 64% voted stay. Near dwarven holds. Elder Council leads.

### Frostridge (~700)
Southern mountains, 81% voted stay. Hunter-Chief Torn Snowborn. Mountain hunters, stubborn.

### The Last Hearth (~800)
Northeastern coast, 69% voted stay. Elder Mira Lastlight (Giant, 92). Defiant isolation.

---

## EVACUATING HEARTHS

### Longmarch (~1,400, 90% evacuated)
76% voted leave. Former-Elder Senna Marchwise coordinates refugees. Most organized.

### Iceholm (~1,000, 95% evacuated)
72% voted leave. Essentially abandoned. Northwestern tundra.

### Winterhold (~600, 80% evacuated)
68% voted leave. Partial ghost town. Southern interior.

### Snowmere (~800, 60% evacuated, ongoing)
61% voted leave. Elder Kael Snowborn. Chaotic, fragmenting. Western tundra.

---

## THE DIVIDE

Families divided. Friends on opposite sides. Communities broken. No right answer.
