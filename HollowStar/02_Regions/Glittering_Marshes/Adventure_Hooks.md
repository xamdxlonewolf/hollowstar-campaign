# Adventure Hooks - Glittering Marshes

---

## THE SHARDS (4)

### 1. "What Lumencrest Is Looking For"
**Hook:** A Lumencrest researcher has been asking unusually specific questions at Reedgate — about the marsh floor, about the glow's consistency at depth, about what happens to metal tools left in the water overnight
**Employer:** Reedgate Elder Mira Fen (concerned, wants to know what they know before the factor house vote)
**Goal:** Assess how much Lumencrest actually knows and whether it's specifically about the shards
**Discovery:** The researcher doesn't know about shards — yet. But they've brought instruments that, if used in the deep marsh, would find them.
**Level:** 2-4

### 2. "The Deepstilt Floor"
**Hook:** A Deepstilt stilt foundation has cracked — need to inspect and repair the underwater section
**Employer:** Deepstilt settlement (practical work)
**Goal:** Dive the foundation, assess damage, repair
**Discovery:** Up close, the shard concentration beneath Deepstilt is visible — and the largest fragment is larger than anyone outside Deepstilt's elder circle knows
**Complication:** The Deepstilt elder wants the party to understand what they've seen without being told directly (they won't confirm, won't deny, will watch what the party does with the knowledge)
**Level:** 3-5

### 3. "The Seekers' Instruments"
**Hook:** A [[04_Factions/Starfall Seekers|Starfall Seekers]] team arrives at Reedgate — they're studying Hollostar trajectories, not the marsh specifically, but their equipment is reacting strangely
**Employer:** Elder council (wants someone to accompany the Seekers and manage what they find)
**Goal:** Guide the Seekers through the marsh while steering them away from the deep concentrations
**Complication:** The Seekers are smart, their instruments are sensitive, and they start asking the right questions
**Level:** 3-5

### 4. "The Fading Channel"
**Hook:** A channel that has glowed since anyone's grandmother can remember has gone dark — no biological cause found
**Employer:** Mistwatch elder (spiritually significant, practically concerning — the fishing route that follows it is now unreliable)
**Goal:** Find out why the channel went dark
**Discovery:** A shard cluster shifted (geological movement — subtle but real) and the organisms that depended on it haven't filled in yet. But *why* the shard moved is more interesting — something disrupted it from beneath.
**Level:** 3-5

## MARSH LIFE (4)

### 5. "The Factor House Vote"
**Hook:** Elder council season meeting — Lumencrest factor house proposal coming to vote
**Employer:** Multiple parties want the party's influence (Lumencrest agent, Elder Mira Fen, a merchant family who wants the trade)
**Goal:** Navigate the council meeting, understand each elder's real position
**Moral complexity:** Lumencrest money would genuinely help some struggling settlements. The cost is presence, and presence leads somewhere nobody wants to go.
**Level:** 2-4

### 6. "Guide Work"
**Hook:** Party needs to cross the marsh — hire a guide or go alone
**If they hire:** Standard crossing, relationship-building with guide, introduction to marsh culture
**If they don't:** The marsh is navigable if you know it. They don't know it. The hook is surviving the attempt and what they learn about the marsh from the experience.
**Level:** 2-3

### 7. "The Unlicensed Guide"
**Hook:** Someone is guiding outsiders without a license — taking them places they shouldn't go
**Employer:** Elder council (this is a serious violation, needs to stop before something goes wrong)
**Goal:** Find the unlicensed guide and the outsiders they're leading
**Discovery:** The guide is a young Ribbet from Deepstilt who needs money desperately. The outsiders they're guiding are searching for something specific — and close to finding the wrong thing.
**Level:** 2-4

### 8. "The White Breath"
**Hook:** The fog spirit (the White Breath) has been appearing at unusual times — midday instead of dawn, in places it's never been seen
**Employer:** Mistwatch Path of Spirits practitioner
**Goal:** Understand why the spirit's behavior has changed
**Discovery:** Connected to the Verdant Crescent's ancient waking — the spirits across the region are responding to the same thing. The marsh spirits felt it first.
**Level:** 3-5

## EXPLORATION (4)

### 9. "The New Channel"
**Hook:** A channel opened in the deep marsh that wasn't there last season — guide maps are wrong
**Employer:** Elder council (needs mapping before someone follows an old map into a dead end)
**Goal:** Map the new channel, determine where it leads
**Discovery:** The channel leads to a dry island that wasn't accessible before — and the island has something on it. Something old.
**Level:** 3-5

### 10. "The Shellmound Archive"
**Hook:** Shellmound's Galapa elder has records going back further than any other settlement — mentions of "the first light" predating all current settlements
**Employer:** Warden Chit of Memory (Verdant Crescent Covenant) — heard rumors, wants access
**Goal:** Convince the Galapa elder to share the records
**What's in them:** A description of the impact itself — passed down from whoever witnessed it, generations of oral-to-written transmission. The most detailed account of an ancient Hollostar strike anywhere.
**Level:** 3-5

### 11. "The Creature Below"
**Hook:** Something large is moving in the deep marsh — not attacking, just present, disrupting the channel ecosystem
**Employer:** Deepstilt fishing families (catches are down, something is disturbing the water)
**Goal:** Find the creature, assess whether it's a threat
**Discovery:** An enormous marsh creature drawn to the shard concentration at Deepstilt — following the energy. Ancient, slow, entirely peaceful. Also the size of a house.
**Level:** 4-6

### 12. "Before the Council"
**Hook:** A message arrives for the elder council — from the Green Covenant's Warden of Waters, marked urgent
**Employer:** Elder Mira Fen (wants the party to deliver the council's response)
**Goal:** Attend the emergency council session, carry the response back to Mistwatch
**The message:** The Verdant Crescent's ancient waking is accelerating. The Warden believes the marsh's shard energy may be relevant — the two ancient sites may be connected. The council needs to decide how much to share.
**Level:** 3-5

---

**Levels:** 2-4 (guide work, settlement politics, marsh life) · 3-5 (shard mysteries, spirit changes, deep marsh exploration) · 4-6 (large creatures, ancient records, cross-regional connections)
