# The Glittering Marshes

> *"At night, the marsh shows you everything it is. During the day it hides it."*

---

**Climate:** Warm, humid, heavy mist  
**Terrain:** Wetlands, shallow channels, dry hummocks, deep bog, ancient sunken shards  
**Population:** ~14,000  
**Government:** Elder council (one elder per settlement, meets seasonally at Dry Crossing)  
**Identity:** Ancient, self-contained, lit from below (shards + bioluminescent life)

---

## Core Identity

The marsh predates every current settlement. No outside power has ever successfully controlled it. The shards are the marsh's; the marsh is not for sale.
