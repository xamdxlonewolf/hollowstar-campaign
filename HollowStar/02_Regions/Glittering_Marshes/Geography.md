# Geography - Glittering Marshes

**Climate:** Humid, warm, heavy rainfall, thick fog common  
**Terrain:** Vast wetlands, waterways, scattered dry hummocks, southern coastal islands  
**Population:** ~12,000 (Ribbet majority, Fungril, Galapa, others)  
**Character:** Three zones (eastern edge, deep interior, northern border)

---

## THE THREE ZONES

### Eastern Edge
Where marsh meets Central Heartlands. Drier ground, accessible, where outside contact happens. **Reedgate** (~2,400) is the main settlement here — guide trade hub, closest to civilized lands.

### Deep Interior
The true marsh — deep water, waterways, stilt villages, fungal colonies, dangerous without guides. **Deep Bloom** (~2,100 Fungril living fungal colony), **Deepsilt** (~1,800 Ribbet over deep water), **Hush Hollow** (~250 isolation community) operate here. The Dry Crossing (~400 permanent) provides the only stable council meeting ground.

### Northern Border
Where marsh meets Verdant Crescent forest. **Mistwatch** (~600) operates at forest-marsh overlap. Green Covenant's Warden of Waters monitors this boundary.

---

## THE SHARDS

Densest Hollostar fragment concentration on the continent. Beneath Deepsilt sits the largest known shard cluster. Deep Bloom has three fragments wrapped in fungal mycelium. This is why Somnarchs and Seekers obsess over the marshes.

---

## WATERWAYS & TERRAIN

**Deep marsh interior:** Waterways navigable by flat-bottomed boats, dangerous without Ribbet guides who know the paths

**Dry hummocks:** Scattered stable ground (Shellmound, Dry Crossing), rare and valuable

**Southern coast:** Where marsh meets open water, islands suitable for settlement

**Northern forest-marsh transition:** Overlapping ecosystems, unique resources

---

## SOUTHERN ISLANDS

Two inhabited islands south of main marsh where waters open toward gulf:

**Tidemark** (~400) - Larger southern island, mixed Ribbet/Galapa fishing community

**Saltmere Isle** (~250) - Smaller island, Ribbet majority, salt water fishing, waystation
