# Isle of the Broken Sphere

> *"Nobody knows why it's called that. Nobody who's been there lately is laughing about it anymore."*

---

**Location:** Silver Belt Sea, southeast of Silverbelt Coast  
**Climate:** Warm maritime, unpredictable, increasing seismic activity  
**Population:** ~3,500 (and dropping)  
**Government:** None — loose community, de facto speaker when needed  
**Crisis:** The island is waking up. Ground cracking. Things coming up from below.

---

## Core Identity

Galapa (largest concentration on continent); everyone else who had nowhere else to go. The Galapa call what's stirring **the Sleeper** — the island's spirit, disturbed. Spine Stones emerging from the fault line; interior forest dying.
