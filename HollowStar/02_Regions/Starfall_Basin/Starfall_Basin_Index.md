# Starfall Basin

> *"The desert does not forgive. The lake does not forget. Together they made us."*

---

**Climate:** Arid desert, extreme heat, oasis at center  
**Terrain:** Rocky desert, salt flats, canyon systems, central lake  
**Population:** ~22,000 (majority at The Endless Eye)  
**Major Settlement:** [[02_Regions/Starfall_Basin/Locations/Cities/The_Endless_Eye|The Endless Eye]] (~20,000)  
**Government:** The Merchant Circle  
**Crisis:** The Endless Eye lake shrinking — early stages

---

## Core Identity

Vast arid desert with one impossible miracle at its heart: **The Endless Eye**, a deep freshwater lake created when a Hollostar fell 700 years ago. Two worlds: the Basin Desert (nomadic tribes, merchants, hermits) and The Endless Eye (oasis city above, underwater community below).

**Central Tension:** The lake is shrinking. The question isn't whether to act, but how — and who decides. Three factions: Traditionalists, Pragmatists, Profiteers.
