# Adventure Hooks - Starfall Basin

---

## THE LAKE CRISIS (5)

### 1. "The Measurement"
**Hook:** A Ribbet scholar slips party secret data — lake declining faster than Council admits
**Employer:** Voice Kessa (Ribbet council member, wants truth public)
**Goal:** Verify measurements independently, present findings to full council
**Complication:** Profiteer faction (Merchant Brek) wants data buried — hired thugs
**Level:** 2-4

### 2. "Into the Deeps"
**Hook:** Researcher Pell Ashwick needs unauthorized samples from Hollostar
**Employer:** Pell (desperate, will pay everything he has)
**Goal:** Sneak past Circle Guards, descend to The Deeps, collect samples
**Complication:** The Deepssharks, depth pressure, AND Ribbet who patrol The Deeps
**Twist:** Samples reveal the star ISN'T failing — something is DRAINING it
**Level:** 5-7

### 3. "The Glow Dims"
**Hook:** Lake luminescence visibly dimmer for 3 nights running — public panic starting
**Employer:** Merchant Circle (emergency contract — restore confidence)
**Goal:** Investigate cause, determine if crisis accelerating
**Discovery:** Lumencrest spy network installed device draining star's energy (testing)
**Level:** 4-6

### 4. "The Traditionalist's Gambit"
**Hook:** Elder Suu proposes ritual to "heal" the star — needs rare desert components
**Employer:** Elder Suu (pay in Ribbet pearls — extraordinary payment)
**Goal:** Travel to Redwall Canyons, Saltwalker territory, retrieve materials
**Complication:** Materials guarded, sacred — tribes want something in return
**Twist:** Ritual actually works slightly (Path of Spirits approach has merit)
**Level:** 3-5

### 5. "The Leak"
**Hook:** Someone's selling Hollostar water (from near The Deeps) on black market
**Employer:** Merchant Circle (illegal, dangerous, undermining trust)
**Goal:** Find source of leak, stop operation
**Complication:** Buyers include desperate people (sick, hopeful — sympathetic)
**Twist:** Merchant Brek is running it (using Profiteer access)
**Level:** 3-5

## LUMENCREST PRESSURE (4)

### 6. "The Diplomatic Incident"
**Hook:** Lumencrest delegation "accidentally" crossed into restricted Deeps area
**Employer:** Merchant Circle (handle it without war)
**Goal:** Investigate, gather evidence, present diplomatic response
**Discovery:** Not accident — they planted a monitoring device
**Level:** 3-5

### 7. "The Double Agent"
**Hook:** Circle Guard member identified as Lumencrest spy
**Employer:** Senior Merchant Voss (quietly — can't trust Circle officially)
**Goal:** Track spy's network without alerting Lumencrest they're compromised
**Twist:** Spy is feeding info but also secretly warning Endless Eye of Expansionist plans
**Level:** 4-6

### 8. "The Offer"
**Hook:** Lumencrest sends genuine offer — massive resources in exchange for Deeps access
**Employer:** Player's choice (multiple factions want them to influence council vote)
**Goal:** Investigate offer's true nature, advise council
**Reality:** Offer is real AND a trap — accepting starts dependency relationship
**Level:** 3-5

### 9. "The Evacuation Plan"
**Hook:** Someone's been quietly buying property outside Basin — insider selling before collapse?
**Employer:** Dust-Chief Orah (suspicious, wants answers)
**Goal:** Follow money trail, identify who's preparing to flee
**Discovery:** Profiteer faction Clanks created contingency — they KNOW how bad it is
**Level:** 4-6

## DESERT ADVENTURES (5)

### 10. "The Broken Road"
**Hook:** Main caravan route blocked (flash flood damage + new sand dune)
**Employer:** Stranded merchant caravan (trapped, supplies running low)
**Goal:** Find alternate route through Basin with Katari guide
**Complication:** Alternative route goes through contested tribal territory
**Level:** 2-4

### 11. "The Petroglyphs Speak"
**Hook:** New petroglyphs appeared overnight in Redwall Canyons — no one made them
**Employer:** Dust-Chief Orah (frightened, needs outsider perspective)
**Goal:** Investigate origin of new markings
**Discovery:** Ancient spirit (possibly star's spirit?) is communicating through the carvings
**Level:** 4-6

### 12. "Water Rights"
**Hook:** Saltwalker tribe accuses Duneborn of poisoning a shared spring
**Employer:** The Crossroads merchants (trade halted, inter-tribal war brewing)
**Goal:** Mediate dispute, find actual culprit
**Real Culprit:** Neither tribe — a passing Lumencrest agent poisoned it to destabilize region
**Level:** 2-4

### 13. "The Shard Hunt"
**Hook:** [[04_Factions/Starfall Seekers|Starfall Seekers]] hire party for Basin desert survey
**Goal:** Survey salt flats for Hollostar shards (instruments detecting something)
**Discovery:** Small shard field in eastern salt flats — also salt flat sinkholes everywhere
**Complication:** Veilmarkets already know about it (waiting for Seekers to find it)
**Level:** 3-5

### 14. "The Broken Clank's Journey"
**Hook:** Damaged Clank desperately needs escort to [[02_Regions/Desert_of_Glass/Desert_of_Glass_Index|Desert of Glass]] (can't travel alone)
**Employer:** The Broken Clank (pays in masterwork repairs — extraordinary value)
**Goal:** Escort across Basin and through initial Desert of Glass terrain
**Complication:** Lumencrest retrieval team following them
**Level:** 3-5

## SOCIAL & INTRIGUE (4)

### 15. "The Pearl's Secret"
**Hook:** Dying Ribbet elder's pearl is stolen before they can give it to their chosen recipient
**Employer:** The recipient (devastated, desperate)
**Goal:** Recover the pearl before elder dies (limited time)
**Complication:** Thief is the elder's estranged offspring — grief + anger
**Resolution:** Multiple outcomes (confront thief, negotiate, expose, keep secret)
**Level:** 1-3

### 16. "The Glimmer Show Gone Wrong"
**Hook:** Glimmerfish school vanished — festival ruined, significant income lost
**Employer:** Ribbet community (cultural + economic loss)
**Goal:** Find where glimmerfish went
**Discovery:** They fled deeper — something in The Deeps disturbed them (Lumencrest device?)
**Level:** 2-4

### 17. "The New Faith"
**Hook:** Aggressive First Flame (Purifying Flame) missionaries arrived, causing friction
**Employer:** Merchant Circle (disrupting harmony, bad for business)
**Goal:** Mediate situation without religious conflict
**Complication:** Missionaries have followers (some locals converted, now divided)
**Twist:** Missionaries working for someone (stirring conflict deliberately?)
**Level:** 2-4

### 18. "The Council Seat"
**Hook:** Wanderer seat coming up for rotation — two tribe candidates, neither acceptable
**Employer:** Senior Merchant Voss (needs stable council, hires party for political work)
**Goal:** Investigate both candidates, ensure trustworthy representative elected
**Discovery:** Both candidates bribed by different outside factions
**Level:** 3-5

---

## RECOMMENDED LEVELS
- **1-3:** Social, simple investigations, desert errands
- **3-5:** Factional politics, desert exploration, medium threats
- **4-6:** Lumencrest espionage, deep lake dangers, high-stakes
- **5-7:** The Deeps, major crisis intervention
