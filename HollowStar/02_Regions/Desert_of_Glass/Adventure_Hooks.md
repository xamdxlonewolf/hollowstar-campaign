# Adventure Hooks - Desert of Glass

> *"If you're here, you're either desperate, greedy, or insane. Probably all three."*

---

## CLANK STORYLINES (5)

### 1. "The Retrieval Mission"
**Hook:** [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Locations/Cities/Lumencrest|Lumencrest]] hires party to retrieve escaped Clanks
**Employer:** Council Expansionist (secretly)
**Goal:** Infiltrate Ember Refuge, "convince" Clanks to return (or forcibly retrieve)
**Moral Dilemma:** Clanks are sentient—are you slave-catchers?
**Twist:** Clanks offer better pay to fake retrieval (bring broken parts, claim Clanks destroyed)
**Level:** 4-6

### 2. "The Sentience Test"
**Hook:** Clank philosopher Conduit-07 wants proof of Clank sentience
**Employer:** Conduit-07 (pay in experimental tech)
**Goal:** Travel to Lumencrest University, present Clank case to scholars
**Challenge:** Lumencrest will try to capture/destroy Conduit-07
**Twist:** Some scholars sympathetic; others want to dissect
**Level:** 3-5

### 3. "The Rogue Unit"
**Hook:** Radical Clank (Destroyer-Prime) plans to bomb Lumencrest
**Employer:** Ember Refuge leadership (stop extremist)
**Goal:** Find Destroyer-Prime in Glass Flats, stop bombing
**Moral Choice:** Kill? Reprogram? Let him try?
**Twist:** Destroyer-Prime has valid points
**Level:** 5-7

### 4. "The Power Source"
**Hook:** Clanks discovered massive Hollostar shard in deep lava tube
**Employer:** Mechworks (extract it)
**Goal:** Navigate lava tubes, fight magma oozes, retrieve shard
**Complication:** Dwarves claim they found it first
**Twist:** Shard unstable—might explode if moved
**Level:** 4-6

### 5. "The Spy Network"
**Hook:** Lumencrest planted spy Clanks among refugees
**Employer:** Conduit-07 (suspects, needs investigation)
**Goal:** Identify spies without causing paranoia
**Twist:** One spy genuinely converted; will help if spared
**Level:** 3-5

## VOLCANIC MYSTERIES (4)

### 6. "The Sealed Door"
**Hook:** Dwarves found ancient sealed door in Scorchpeak caves (Skyspine)
**Employer:** Crystal-Keeper Dura Gemvein
**Goal:** Bypass wards, open door, explore
**Danger:** Might trigger Scorchpeak eruption (local belief)
**Twist:** Pre-volcanic civilization vault (treasure + guardian construct)
**Level:** 6-8

### 7. "The Whispering Vents"
**Hook:** Geothermal vents "speak" prophecies—recently disturbing
**Employer:** Infernis priest (fears demon rising)
**Goal:** Investigate vents, determine source
**Truth:** Ancient being trapped in magma (wants freedom, dangerous)
**Twist:** Freeing it prevents eruption OR causes catastrophic one
**Level:** 5-7

### 8. "Mount Ashfall's Heart"
**Hook:** Lava lake in Mount Ashfall caldera glowing brighter
**Employer:** Scorchwatch scientists (need samples)
**Goal:** Descend into crater, collect lava samples
**Twist:** Fire elemental in lake (awakening?)
**Level:** 4-6

### 9. "The Ashwraith Mystery"
**Hook:** Ashwraiths (see [[02_Regions/Desert_of_Glass/Geography|Geography]]) appearing more frequently
**Employer:** Local settlement
**Goal:** Investigate—what are they? What do they want?
**Discovery:** Victims of ancient eruption, seeking burial
**Resolution:** Find remains, perform funeral = ashwraiths rest
**Level:** 2-4

---

## PROSPECTING & TREASURE (3)

### 10. "The Shard Mother Lode"
**Hook:** Rumor: Massive Hollostar deposit in Glass Flats (Mirror Sea)
**Employer:** Prospector (split findings)
**Goal:** Journey across Glass Flats, locate deposit, survive glass storms
**Twist:** Deposit real BUT surrounded by shard-mutated glass serpents
**Level:** 3-5

### 11. "The Rainbow Obsidian Vein"
**Hook:** Rare rainbow obsidian discovered; lava flow blocking extraction
**Employer:** Dwarf miner
**Goal:** Divert lava flow, mine obsidian
**Twist:** Diverting lava opens new lava tubes (unexplored, rewarding)
**Level:** 4-6

### 12. "The Glass Cathedral Expedition"
**Hook:** Ancient ruins in Glass Flats (see [[02_Regions/Desert_of_Glass/Locations/Location_Index|Locations]])
**Employer:** Archaeologist
**Goal:** Escort scholar, protect during exploration
**Dangers:** Glass storms, guardian constructs, rival looters
**Level:** 5-7

---

## POLITICAL/FACTIONAL (3)

### 13. "The Scorched Accord Crisis"
**Hook:** Annual Accord meeting descending into violence (dwarves vs. Clanks)
**Employer:** The Saddle merchants (war bad for business)
**Goal:** Mediate dispute, prevent breakdown
**Twist:** Outside agitator (Lumencrest agent) stirring conflict deliberately
**Level:** 2-4

### 14. "The Verdant Incursion"
**Hook:** [[04_Factions/Green Covenant|Green Covenant]] raid from [[02_Regions/Verdant_Crescent/Verdant_Crescent_Index|Verdant Crescent]] (attacking Ashgarde outpost)
**Employer:** Drakona Captain Vrax Emberclaw
**Goal:** Repel raid, capture Green Covenant, negotiate or execute
**Twist:** Ashgarde accidentally started fire (covering it up)
**Level:** 3-5

### 15. "The Water War"
**Hook:** Caravan bringing water from [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Central_Heartlands_Index|Central Heartlands]] ambushed
**Employer:** Multiple settlements
**Goal:** Find caravan, recover water, punish attackers
**Culprit:** Desperate nomads (dying of thirst)
**Moral Choice:** Kill? Help? Negotiate water-sharing?
**Level:** 2-4

---

## MONSTER HUNTS (3)

### 16. "The Magma Ooze Infestation"
**Hook:** Lava tubes infested with magma oozes (see [[02_Regions/Desert_of_Glass/Geography|Geography]])
**Employer:** Cinderhome Mine-Master
**Goal:** Clear oozes from tubes
**Strategy:** Trap, freeze (imported ice? magic?), or lead away
**Level:** 4-6

### 17. "The Glass Serpent Nest"
**Hook:** Glass serpents hunting near settlement; population exploding
**Employer:** Settlement leader (pay per serpent + bonus for nest)
**Goal:** Find nest in Shardfields, destroy eggs, kill adults
**Twist:** Nest guards Hollostar shard (reason for overpopulation)
**Level:** 3-5

### 18. "The Rogue Fire Elemental"
**Hook:** Fire elemental rampaging
**Employer:** Scorched Accord (collective bounty)
**Goal:** Banish or contain
**Solution:** Elemental bound to specific lava pool—seal the pool
**Level:** 6-8

---

## SOCIAL/INTRIGUE (2)

### 19. "The Hellmouth Gladiator"
**Hook:** Friend enslaved in Hellmouth fighting pits
**Goal:** Infiltrate Hellmouth, win or buy or break out friend's freedom
**Complication:** Friend doesn't want to leave
**Level:** 2-4

### 20. "The Forge-Master's Secret"
**Hook:** Forge-Master Karg Ironheart accused of sabotage (mine collapse)
**Employer:** Karg (swears innocence)
**Goal:** Investigate, find true culprit
**Twist:** Karg IS guilty (reckless experiments) but not malicious
**Level:** 3-5

---

**Levels 2-4:** Social conflicts, minor hunts, escort. **3-5:** Factional, shard prospecting. **4-6:** Volcanic dangers, Clank storylines. **6-8:** Major mysteries, rogue elementals, sealed vaults.
