# Culture and Society - Desert of Glass

> *"Soft people die here. Hard people thrive."*

---

## CORE VALUES

**Freedom Above All:** Fled authority elsewhere; "No gods, no masters"; government minimal or absent.

**Self-Reliance:** Survive or die; asking for help is weakness (cultural bias); earn respect through endurance.

**Strength & Toughness:** Physical and mental resilience valued; complaining is shameful; scars are badges of honor.

**Pragmatism:** Survival trumps ideology; alliance with enemies if necessary; honor means keeping your word.

**Adaptation:** Environment demands flexibility; those who don't adapt die; innovation celebrated (especially Clanks).

---

## DAILY LIFE

**Work:** Dwarves (mining, smelting, crafting); Clanks (manufacturing, research, continuous); Drakona (herding, hunting, guiding); Infernis (mercenary, alchemy); Fungril (cultivation, moisture, cave maintenance).

**Food:** Limited — imported grain, preserved meat, mushrooms; Clanks don't eat; Drakona hunt; water imported (expensive from [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Central_Heartlands_Index|Central Heartlands]]), geothermal condensation, or Fungril moisture.

**Shelter:** Obsidian buildings, lava tubes, fire-resistant tents, open-air platforms.

**Entertainment:** Fighting pits (Hellmouth), endurance contests, storytelling, Clank debates, crafting competitions.

---

## CUSTOMS & TRADITIONS

**Greetings:** Dwarves (clasp forearms); Clanks (state designation); Drakona (touch foreheads or nod); Infernis (eye contact); Fungril (spore exchange).

**Rites of Passage:** Drakona Fire-Walk (cross lava pool at 16); Infernis Scar-Rite (self-inflicted burn); Dwarf Forge-Trial (masterwork using lava heat); Clank Designation (new name at Ember Refuge).

**Death Customs:** Dwarves (burial in mines); Clanks (recycle parts); Drakona (lava cremation); Infernis (pyre); Fungril (composting).

---

## ANCESTRY-SPECIFIC ADAPTATIONS

**Drakona:** Scales reflect heat; at home in fire. **Infernis:** Fire resistance; heat awakens demon within. **Clanks:** Immune to heat; geothermal power; independence test. **Dwarves:** Tough; forges turn disadvantage to advantage. **Fungril:** Fire-resistant strain; geothermal caves; provide moisture; declining.

---

## RELIGION (Minimal)

Six-Fold Faith barely present. The First Flame moderate (Purifying Flame, Infernis). Path of Spirits rare. Unique: volcano worship (Infernis, Drakona); Clank debate on machine consciousness/souls.

---

## LEGAL SYSTEM

No regional law; each settlement makes own rules. Don't steal (exile/death); contracts sacred; violence allowed if "fair." **Justice:** Trial by combat, arbitration (at The Saddle), exile ("Let the desert judge you"). **Crimes:** Theft (return + fine or lose hand); murder (execution or blood debt); oath-breaking (social exile); arson (death).

---

## CLANK INDEPENDENCE MOVEMENT

**Goals:** All Clanks freed; recognition as people; self-governance. **Ember Refuge (idealists):** Peaceful, coexistence. **Radical Clanks:** Violent revolution. Lumencrest officially demands return; can't enforce; some factions secretly negotiate.

---

## RELATIONSHIPS BETWEEN ANCESTRIES

Dwarves & Clanks (trade, mutual respect, unease with sentient machines). Drakona & Infernis (kinship, competition). Fungril & everyone (symbiosis — moisture for protection). Organics & Clanks (philosophical divide; younger generations more accepting).

---

## CULTURAL IDENTITY

**"Scorched":** Collective term; pride in surviving. **Outsider views:** [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Central_Heartlands_Index|Central Heartlands]] — "Madmen in hellscape"; [[02_Regions/Verdant_Crescent/Verdant_Crescent_Index|Verdant Crescent]] — "Fire-lovers destroying the world"; [[02_Regions/Central_Heartlands/Locations/Cities/Lumencrest|Lumencrest]] — "Criminals and escaped property." Reality: mix of outcasts, opportunists, and those suited to fire.
