# Desert of Glass

> *"Fire makes the glass. Heat keeps the living out."*

---

**Climate:** Extreme heat, arid desert transitioning to volcanic  
**Terrain:** Glass plains, obsidian fields, lava pools, Skyspine volcanic mountains  
**Population:** ~35,000 (concentrated in Skyspine settlements)  
**Government:** Independent settlements, no unified authority  
**Crisis:** None directly — region already chaotic

---

## Core Identity

Two environments: **Skyspine Volcanic Region** (livable valleys, dwarven mines, Clank refuges, Drakona) and **The Glass Flats** (deadly heat, nomadic Drakona, prospectors). Freedom vs. survival; no one wants to submit to authority.
