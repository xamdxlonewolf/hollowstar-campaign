## WHERE TO START

**First session prep:** Read Running the World in full. You don't need answers to the three mysteries before you run — but it's worth knowing which possibilities feel most resonant to you, so you can lean into clues and player theories that point in interesting directions.

**Ongoing prep:** Countdown Clocks tells you what the world is doing between sessions. Faction Tension Tracker tells you who's doing it. Check both before each session block.

**When players dig into secrets:** Secrets & Revelations tells you who knows what, how players can find it, and what changes when they do. Use the pacing guide to avoid front-loading all revelations.

## THREE MYSTERIES TO HOLD BEFORE SESSION 1

These don't need answers — but knowing which possibilities feel most alive to you helps you seed clues, respond to player theories, and let the truth emerge through play rather than being declared upfront. The options for each are in Running the World. Any combination can be true. None may be fully resolved by the end of a campaign.

The vault has three open mysteries that have no canonical answer — they're yours to decide:

**1. What are the Hollowstars actually?**  
Options: Pruning Mechanism / Hunger / Divine Mechanism / Unknown origin  
→ See [[08_GM_Notes/Running_the_World|Running the World]] — Mystery 1

**2. What did Thessara see at the third fall's impact site?**  
Options: A figure / An opening / An absence  
→ See [[08_GM_Notes/Running_the_World|Running the World]] — Mystery 2

**3. What is the Sleeper beneath the Isle of the Broken Sphere?**  
Options: Surviving entity / Star fragment / The world's answer  
→ See [[08_GM_Notes/Running_the_World|Running the World]] — Mystery 3
