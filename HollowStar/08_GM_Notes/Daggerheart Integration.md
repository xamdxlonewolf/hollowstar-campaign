# Daggerheart Integration

Tips for using this setting with Daggerheart mechanics.

## Hope & Fear Tokens

**Generate Hope when:**
- Communities band together
- Players solve problems through cooperation
- Small victories against [[The Darkness]]
- Genuine connections formed
- Beauty found in dark times
- Acts of courage despite fear

**Generate Fear when:**
- [[The Darkness]] manifests
- Systems fail (power, water)
- Transformed creatures attack
- Political betrayals
- Mysteries deepen without answers
- Reality feels unstable

## Stress & Armor Slots

**Use Stress for:**
- Factional reputation
- Community stability
- Power/water reserves
- Investigation progress
- Countdown to star fall

**Modified Armor:**
- [[Hollowstars|Shard]]-enhanced equipment
- Glasswright weapons (Desert of Glass)
- Lumencrest technology

## Collaborative Worldbuilding

**Encourage players to:**
- Define their relationship to Hollowstars
- Create backstories tied to factions
- Propose theories about mysteries
- Narrate victories and moments of hope
- Describe how Darkness makes them feel

**GM responds to:**
- Player theories (some become true)
- Character goals (build plotlines)
- Background details (incorporate into world)
- Emotional moments (reward with mechanical benefits)

## Community Mechanics

Use Daggerheart's community rules for:
- [[Lumencrest]] districts
- [[The Endless Eye]] underwater city
- [[Verdant Crescent]] groves
- Player-founded settlements
- Faction headquarters

## Countdown Clocks

Track:
- Star fall approach (major clock)
- Lumencrest power depletion
- Endless Eye water levels
- Factional conflicts escalating
- Investigation progress
