# THE SILVERHAND DOSSIER

  

**Player Reference for Basil Silverhand**  

*What your character knows. What your character has done. Where your character is going.*

  

---

  

## WHO YOU ARE

  

You are **Basil Silverhand**, youngest of five, and the one everyone underestimates. Your family has been in the business of spectacular, principled theft for generations, and you have spent your entire life trying to prove you belong at the head of it.

  

You are **Half Katari, Half Emberkin**.  

The Katari blood comes from your mother, Parsley, a woman who built an empire and never married to protect it. Your father was Emberkin: practical, self-sufficient, and absent from your life. What you carry from him is subtle: steadiness, resilience, quiet control.

  

### The Core Problem

You are the **most capable** of your siblings. You know it. Your mother knows it.  

But tradition says the firstborn leads.  

  

**Sage will inherit, unless you prove otherwise.**  

You are in Lumencrest to prove it.

  

---

  

## YOUR FAMILY

  

The Silverhands began with a theft, and a marriage.  

Old Deuteronomy, a street thief, was guided by Cassandra, a noblewoman who broke into her own father’s vault. That act defined the family.

  

### The Three Traditions

- **The Calling Card**: Every job ends with the Silverhand seal.  

- **Spectacle**: Theft is art. If it isn’t impressive, it doesn’t count.  

- **Charity**: The poor benefit. Always.

  

**Parsley Silverhand: Your Mother**  

Head of the family. Brilliant, calculating, and emotionally complex. She has never revealed her stance on succession.

  

**Your Brothers**  

- **Sage Silverhand** (Firstborn): Lazy. Arrogant. Assumes leadership is his by right. Nearly got the family killed during a failed training heist.  

- **Chicory Silverhand**: Master of connections and relationships. Expanding the family’s influence across the continent.  

- **Thyme Silverhand**: The muscle. Reliable, grounded, family-focused. No longer interested in leadership.  

- **Rue Silverhand**: Small, deceptive, and extremely dangerous. A master safecracker who weaponizes perception.

  

---

  

## THE HEIST THAT CHANGED EVERYTHING

  

A “training” job led by Sage went wrong. You were spotted by **Katya**, an elf-blood assassin. You escaped. Barely. She has been tracking you ever since.

  

**What your party believes**: Katya is your obsessive ex-girlfriend.  

**This is a lie.**

  

**What you know**: She is a professional doing her job. There is also… *something else* between you. Complicated. Unresolved.  

That failure is why you left.

## NOTABLE PAST JOBS

  

**The Merchant Circle Ledger: Endless Eye**  

You infiltrated a merchant house and stole a sensitive ledger documenting illegal trade.  

- *Entry*: Forged shipping credentials  

- *Target*: Private study  

- *Exit*: Undetected  

  

**Result**: The ledger was delivered. The theft discovered days later.  

**Charity**: Dockworkers exploited by the merchant house received anonymous compensation.

  

**The Kingsrest Inheritance Dispute**  

You intercepted a sealed assessment report. You didn’t steal jewels, you stole leverage.  

**Result**: The legal dispute collapsed. The truth was revealed through absence.  

**Charity**: Tenant farmers were indirectly relieved of financial pressure.

## LUMENCREST: WHAT YOU KNOW

  

You are not overwhelmed by the city. That alone gives you an advantage.

  

**Key Realities**  

- No entry papers required  

- Cable system connects major districts  

- The city hums, the **Lumen Network**  

- The Radiant Core is heavily controlled  

- Shadowside has no power and minimal law enforcement  

- The Night Market operates in darkness  

  

**The Lamplight Theatre**  

A perfect cover environment: formal crowds, controlled chaos, movement without scrutiny. You know the layout, not the administration.

  

**The Vault District**  

Heavily fortified. Not your target. Something extremely valuable lies beneath it, likely tied to the city’s failing power.

  

**The Underground**  

A network of tunnels (sewers and infrastructure passages). Difficult navigation. Useful for escape, not entry.

## THE LUMENCREST JOB

  

**The Target**: House Vael  

- Political power  

- Carefully curated reputation  

- Quiet diplomacy with the Empire  

  

**The Job**: Intercept a sealed diplomatic pouch from a courier in the Radiant Core, **in plain sight**.

  

**Why This Matters**  

This is not Sage’s kind of job. This requires:  

**Precision • Patience • Observation • Control**

  

**The Spectacle**  

No chaos. No distractions. No noise.  

**Just perfection.**
