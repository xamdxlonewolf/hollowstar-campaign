**Location(s):** Ember Grove → Emberford → Road to Lumencrest  
**Estimated Runtime:** 3–4 hours

## Session Goal

Ground the party in Ember Grove and Emberford, run the House Vael lumber negotiation as the inciting incident, and end on the first view of Lumencrest at night — wonder mixed with something not quite foreboding. Every thread that pulls them west should be planted in Act 2.

**Plateau ending:** Road crests a ridge. Lumencrest glows below. Some outer-district lights flicker. Stop on the image. Do not explain.

## Act Structure

### Act 1 — Ember Grove at Dawn (~40 min)

**Goal:** Character intros before complications. Thorn summons Greer to council.

**Beats:**
1. Opening narration — Gold Month morning, grove sensory detail, Heart Stone warm
2. **Greer** — Ask Tara her morning routine first; whatever she says is true
3. Layer in: louder crows, warm Heart Stone, watchful spirit web, low Emberfork
4. **Lurielle** — Two weeks at grove, journal near Stone, academic language failing
5. **Basil** — Three days in Emberford, watching road; Vael rep arrived yesterday
6. Thorn calls Greer; companions invited to council hall

**Spotlight:** ~5 min each player, no dice

**Fear windows (max 1):** Crows scatter / Heart Stone pulse / Lurielle bioluminescence flicker  
→ `fear_options.html#fs-a1-*`

---

### Act 2 — The Council Meeting (~60 min)

**Goal:** Plant every thread pulling party to Lumencrest.

**Beats:**
1. **Routine business** — Bridge repair, harvest reports (Aldric from grove)
2. **Green Covenant rumors** — Bren Ashfield: logging crew returned without equipment; Coppertown road survey fire
3. **Caelen dismisses rumors** — Council of Lumens intelligence; "fringe elements"
4. **Lumber offer** — House Vael: 50 acres/year eastern managed woodland, 18 gold/acre, reforestation clause, operations after Frostmonth, **final offer**
5. **Fen Crane** supports — bridge money, Accord subsidy stalled 14 months
6. **Thorn speaks** — "The eastern edge borders the grove's spirit boundary." / "This isn't a timber question."
7. **Caelen responds** — Survey mapped spirit boundary; prepared for Thorn by name
8. **Question window** — Marta opens floor to guests (ask, not vote)
9. **Greer's moment** — Prompt Tara: Goldwood memory; Marta needs reason to say no
10. **Deferral** — One week to review; word by end of Goldmonth
11. **Pickpocket window** — Caelen's satchel unattended ~30s; Basil Finesse DIF 14

**Pickpocket outcomes:**
- **Fail:** Guard blocks; Caelen knows someone tried
- **Partial:** Letter taken; Caelen notices later, more careful
- **Success:** Letter + schedule card — Verdean ships "refugee transit Phase 2," Unseen Guild eye sigil; RC handoff Gold Month 14 mid-afternoon, Courtyard entrance, confirm with V

**Fear windows (1–2 total):** Thorn "No" / council member relaxes / pickpocket guard drift / Greer questioned / Caelen's exit look  
→ `fear_options.html#fs-c2-*`, `#fs-c4-*`, `#fs-pp-*`

---

### Act 3 — After the Meeting (~30 min)

**Goal:** Party chooses Lumencrest themselves.

**Beats:**
1. Hall empties; Thorn gone to grove; Caelen at tavern
2. Each PC's reason now visible (Greer/Thorn, Lurielle/archives, Basil/heist)
3. **Route choice:**
   - **Road** (~2 days) — Coppertown trade road; Caelen on road; **Hired Thugs**
   - **Grove path** (+half day) — spirit territory edge; quieter; **Shard-Infected Bear**

**Thorn after meeting (if Greer speaks):** Timberfall heard same terms 15 years before Goldwood. "Watch the people around the Vael name. Not what they want from the grove. Where what they take goes next."

---

### Act 4 — The Road (~45 min)

**Goal:** First memorable Daggerheart combat. See `session1.html` combat primer.

**Road — Hired Thugs (×3):** DIF 12, 14 HP, 1d8+1, Fear 1 each. Retreat at 2 down. Social: Presence DIF 13. Guild scouts; report party composition if runner escapes → Session 2 awareness.

**Grove — Shard-Infected Bear:** DIF 14, 32 HP, Claw 2d6+2, Shard Pulse 1d10 force 10ft (Fear). Tame: Greer Instinct DIF 16. Blue-white fragment = Endless Eye origin; Lurielle Knowledge DIF 13.

**Fear windows:** Road `#fs-rt-*` / Bear `#fs-sb-*`

---

### Session End — The Plateau (~10 min)

Read Lumencrest vista slowly. Per-character feeling (one at a time). Final line: someone takes first step down the hill. **Stop.**
