## Act Structure

### Act 1 — Morning City + Protest Building (~40 min)

**Goal:** Normal morning, then rising noise at border. Position party within 3–6 blocks of hub OR protest.

**Beats:**
1. **Dawn carryover** — Fresh chalk: *WE PAID FOR THIS LIGHT.* Coffee at Lida's if they stayed Eastwick — **Lida Birch** (human, 55) serves line; mentions watch asked organizers to "keep it peaceful."
2. **Brinne Halloway** — At Eastwick Cooperative hall if party visits. Organizing candle/oil stock quietly. **Presence DIF 12** for trust → admits Cooperative has been "preparing for brownouts longer than the Council admits."
3. **Morning errands** — Archives letter for Lurielle (Hen Brask clerk: appointment **Day 4**), Basil scouting Courtyard approach (Radiant Core visible, not enterable without referral), Greer at Speakers' Corner fringe hearing rationing stories.
4. **Late morning — protest forms** — Lamplight Ward / Shadowside border, three blocks west of **Eastwick secondary hub** (alley junction: **Wire Road & Ninth Lamp**). ~200 people, Ironwright banners mixed with residents. Organizers **Tessa Morr** (human, 34) and **Docken Hale** (dwarf, 50s) — not radicals; want rationing rollback.
5. **City watch** — ~20 officers, containment not hostility. Captain **Isen Varr** (human, 45) on scene.
6. **Positioning prompt:** Where is each PC at **2:30pm**? Hub is 3 blocks east of protest. Entertainment Quarter (Copperstring) one block south of hub.

**Spotlight:** Brinne — practical mutual aid; Lida — 4am neighborhood knowledge; Greer — protest anger is real; Basil — watch deployment patterns.

### Act 2 — Mid-Afternoon Explosion (~60 min)

**Goal:** Fully beat-mapped blast and 10-minute aftermath. **Time: ~2:47pm.**

**Location:** Secondary Lumen Network distribution hub — brick housing on Wire Road eastern edge, serves Eastwick residential + partial Entertainment Quarter feed. Not primary node. **Targeted.**

#### Beat Map (run in order)

| Min | Beat | GM action |
|-----|------|-----------|
| 0 | Protest peak | Chant: "We paid — we paid —" Watch line holds. |
| 1 | Pre-blast tell | Lurielle: hum **stutters** once (pattern break). Greer: spirits **recoil** east. |
| 2 | **Detonation** | Deep crack, not fireball. Hub housing blows **outward** — glass, ceramic, blue-white spark along conduit. Sound hits protest 3 blocks west. |
| 3 | Light dies | Eastwick lamps **out** in rolling wave east→west. Copperstring stage lights **cut** mid-song. |
| 4 | Crowd split | **Panic fork** — see encounter. Watch surges east; some protesters run **toward** blast. |

| 6 | Ash witness | **Ash** (human, 29) on **rooftop two blocks south** — saw pre-blast figure at hub door. Met eyes with party if outside. **Leaves quickly.** |
| 7 | Wren (GM only) | **Wren** (elf, 134) on distant roofline north. Watching. Not present at site. Calculating. |

| 9 | Captain Varr | Shouts triage points; orders witness holds; sends runner to Accord Chamber. |

### Act 3 — Immediate Response Forks (~45 min)

**Goal:** Player choice under pressure. No single correct path.

**Fork A — Help injured**
- **Medicine DIF 13** stabilize Edda; **Presence DIF 12** clear crowd
- Success → referred to **Ora Finch** (Mending Room, Halfling Quarter) via Brinne's network
- **Ora Finch** (halfling, 67): takes Edda; asks who pulled conduit shrapnel — **Insight DIF 14** she reads blast as deliberate

**Fork B — Pursue witness (Ash)**
- **Agility or Instinct DIF 14** rooftop chase through Eastwick (dark)
- Partial → glimpse hood; Success → corner Ash in alley; he won't talk long: "I didn't do it. I saw someone who knew the door." **Presence DIF 15** honest approach → "They weren't running like guilty. They were running like done."

**Fork C — Blast site forensics**
- **Investigation DIF 14** (Basil/Lurielle) before watch seals site (15 min window)
- Success: residue **ceramic capacitor**, Network-frequency tuned — not military incendiary
- **Knowledge DIF 13** (Lurielle): secondary feed hub — disabling here hurts residential before commercial priority

**Fork D — Caught in watch sweep**
- **Presence DIF 13** talk down; **Deception DIF 15** slip away in dark
- Held → 20 min questions; logged names; released unless outstanding warrants

**Fork E — Protest organizers**
- Tessa Morr publicly condemns violence: "This is not us." **Insight DIF 12** — genuine horror, not performance

**Encounter — Panic Crowd**
- **Type:** Social / environmental
- **Roll:** Presence DIF 12 calm **or** Agility DIF 13 move through
- **Outcomes:** Fail — separated 1d4 min, minor trample 1d6; Partial — reach goal delayed; Success — pull someone clear (+1 Eastwick rep)

**Encounter — Stabilize Edda Venn**
- **Type:** Skill challenge
- **Roll:** Medicine DIF 13 (or magical heal)
- **Outcomes:** Fail — watch medic takes over, Edda critical; Partial — stable for transport; Success — mobile on stretcher, Ora Finch grateful

### Act 4 — Evening Fallout + Watch Sweep (~30 min)

**Goal:** Dark Eastwick, Copperstring partial outage, rumor layer.

**Beats:**

2. **Copperstring** — Partial power; acoustic set by candlelight. Patrons scared, staying anyway.
3. **Evening Broadsheet extra** — "HUB ATTACK ON WIRE ROAD" — no perpetrator, casualty count, Council statement pending.
4. **Watch sweep** — Officers door-to-door Eastwick. **Investigation DIF 13** to hear captain tell sergeant: "23 interviews by tomorrow or I get demoted."
5. **Solt (if contact):** Dark Lantern busier; won't say he expected this; mentions Derrin still missing.
6. **Theron Ashwick statement** (heard, not seen): "Act of anti-civilization terrorism." **Sevan Vael** (heard): "Measured response."
