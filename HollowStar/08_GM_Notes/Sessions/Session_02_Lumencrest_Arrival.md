## Act Structure

### Act 1 — Descent and Gate Entry (~40 min)

**Goal:** Transition from Session 1 vista to inside the wall. Establish default entry (Pauper's Gate) and Session 1 branch consequences.

**Beats:**
1. **Plateau descent** — Morning Gold Month light; outer-district lamps still visible; some sections dimmer than others. Read `Arriving_in_Lumencrest.md` approach/at-gates as needed.
2. **First smell/sound** — Coal, river, density, metallic Network undertone. Hum audible before the gate.
3. **Pauper's Gate queue** — City watch: name, origin, lodging intent, business. **Presence DIF 12** to give vague answers without raising flags; **Deception DIF 14** if actively wanted or using false identity.
4. **Gate texture (don't explain):** Frostwell refugees along interior wall — quiet, exhausted. Vendor cluster with bad prices. North Expanse cable car terminus overhead.
5. **Old Pen's Taproom** — First building left inside gate. Old Pen serves; watches everyone.
6. **Session 1 branches at gate:**
   - **Scout escaped:** A watch sergeant (**Sergeant Maret**, human, 40s) has a rough description — "three travelers, grove road, one with a staff." Not arrest; extra questions. **Insight DIF 13** (Maret) to connect Greer to Ember Grove.
   - **Basil pickpocket success:** Letter/schedule safe; no immediate heat unless Caelen branch active.
   - **Caelen knows Basil:** If `fs-pp-exit` or partial pickpocket — a Vael-clothed courier at the gate registry desk glances twice. **Perception DIF 14** (Basil) to notice. Courier does not approach today.
7. **Border chalk (optional stop):** If party walks south toward Ward border before lodging — workers scrubbing yesterday's protest writing. Legible remnant: **WE PAID FOR THIS LIGHT.**

**Spotlight:** Greer — spirits feel thin, layered under stone; Lurielle — hum as physical pressure; Basil — who's watching the gate vs. who's performing indifference.

**Fear windows:** `fs-s2-descent-flicker`, `fs-s2-gate-scout`, `fs-s2-gate-caelen`, `fs-s2-gate-chalk`, `fs-s2-pen-watch`

### Act 2 — First Hours: Sensory City + Lodging (~50 min)

**Goal:** Overwhelm (pleasantly), orient, secure beds. Cable car as geography lesson.

**Beats:**
1. **Street narration** — Electric lamps, cable car overhead, Clanks in grey robes, crowd density. Paraphrase `Arriving_in_Lumencrest.md` first-impression block.
2. **Old Pen briefing (if they stayed):** Buys drinks; doesn't gossip free. **3 silver** or two rounds → she mentions "people asking about the northern feed lines" last week — "not watch. Not Guild. Wrong boots."
3. **Lodging fork:**
   - **Stay north (budget):** Kess Aldman's boarding (Manufactories, 7 silver) or cable car south to **Wren House** (Eastwick, 6 silver, community breakfast). Henris/Accord path requires travel.
   - **Cable car to Copper Gate:** **Traveller's Mark** (Henris Callow, 1 gold/night) or **Accord Arms** (Joss Aldeth, 2–5 gold). **Tomm** at Welcome Gate if they want a guide — 2 silver intro tour, honest about exchange scams.
4. **Cable car ride** — North Expanse Station → Central Hub or Merchant Square. **2 copper/person.** Attendant **Pella Marr** (dwarf, 30s) — professional, bored; rings bell at stops.
5. **Orientation beats on car:** Vault wall, Manufactories smoke, dome of Accord Chamber. **Knowledge DIF 12** (Lurielle) to recognize Network architecture parallels to Endless Eye theory.
6. **Merchant Square fountain** — Normal. City operating. One lamp post eastside flickers and holds.

**Spotlight:** Lurielle — Network hum sharpens in transit, like standing inside a held note; Greer — no single tree spirit, only aggregate exhaustion; Basil — maps cameras, uniforms, pickpocket marks at station.

**Fear windows:** `fs-s2-car-hum`, `fs-s2-car-brownout`, `fs-s2-lodge-greer`  
→ `fear_options_session2.html`

### Act 3 — PC Spotlights, Evening (~45 min)

**Goal:** One substantive beat per PC tied to their thread. Social/exploration, not combat.

**Beats:**
1. **Greer — land reading** — **Instinct DIF 13** at any green edge (Halfling Quarter market planters, Cooper Square patch). Success: spirits present but starved, "drinking through pipes." Failure: dull ache, no detail. Thorn's mission hook: someone at **Speakers' Corner** (Bram Kettlemarch, evening) mentions eastern logging and Covenant frustration — not a quest, a voice.
2. **Lurielle — Network hum** — **Knowledge or Instinct DIF 14** near a street junction box or Hub approach. Success: secondary periodicity in the pulse — not random brownout; patterned. Failure: headache, nausea. She knows the archives are the next step; **Hen Brask** (Hall of Records) takes appointments — not today after hours.
3. **Basil — heat check** — Silver Thread eyes use **Perception DIF 13** on Basil in crowds. His contacts: none established yet. **Streetwise DIF 12** in Night Market fringe (if they go): hears Gold Month 14 "courtyard handoffs" mentioned as rumor — no RC named. If pickpocket intel: **confirm V** on schedule card matches a Guild sigil chalked on a Merchant Square alley (Investigation DIF 13).
4. **Night Market (smaller than usual)** — Three substance-section stalls empty. Mama Voss's cart still running. **Mama Voss** (human, 58) — excellent food, 2–3 copper; notices who's new.
5. **Optional Shadowside — Dark Lantern:** **Solt** quieter than usual. If engaged: friend **Derrin** missing three days — "ran an errand to the Maze." Doesn't ask party to find him. **Presence DIF 12** for honest sympathy → Solt pours tea, says nothing more.

**Encounter — Mama Voss's Line (social):**
- **Type:** Social texture
- **Stakes:** Information direction, not combat
- **Roll:** **Presence DIF 11** to get her "weather report" — who's nervous in the market
- **Outcomes:** Fail — good food only; Partial — "substance boys absent, supply trouble"; Success — points toward Night Pharmacy or "a half-elf asking about feed hubs near Eastwick" (Ash rumor, no name)
