## What Is It?
Growing sense that something is coming/changing

- Felt more than seen
- Measurable in some ways (star failures, creature transformations)

- [[The Endless Eye]] shrinking  
- Stars looking wrong
- Approaching [[Hollowstars|Hollostar]]
