# Themes and Tone

## Core Themes
1. Hope amidst darkness
2. The beautiful unknowable  
3. Community & collaboration
4. Political intrigue & moral complexity

## Tone Levels
- **Cosmic Horror:** 2/10 (mysterious, not sanity-breaking)
- **Political Intrigue:** 7/10 (factional conflicts, gray morality)
- **Hope:** 5/10 (real stakes, real agency)
