# Hollowstars

See main [[README]] for full details.

## Quick Reference
- Fallen stars containing cosmic energy
- Two known intact: Lumencrest's Heart, The Endless Eye
- Last fall: 700 years ago
- New star approaching [[Frostwell Tundra]]
- Effects: Transform creatures, power cities, create impossible geography

## Current Crisis
- Old stars are failing (dimming, shrinking)
- New star approaching for first time in 700 years
- No one knows why
