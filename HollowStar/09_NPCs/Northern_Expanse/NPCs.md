# NPCs - Northern Expanse

---

## THE CROWN (3)

### King Elowen Arathas
**Role:** King of the Northern Expanse, ~280 years on the throne  
**Ancestry:** Elf, age ~490  
**Appearance:** Silver hair, grey-green eyes, spare and still — moves as if time around him runs slightly slower  
**Personality:** Thinks in decades. Genuinely loves his people — all of them, not just elves. Privately lonely in a way that 280 years of watching people die makes inevitable. More aware of the Hollostar's implications than he shows.  
**Goals:** Navigate the refugee crisis without fracturing the kingdom; appoint the right Warden; not make the fast decision that feels correct but creates a generation of problems  
**The frustration:** He is not wrong that slow decisions can be better. He is also not present in Highwatch at 3am.  
**Quote:** *"The river does not hurry. It arrives nonetheless."*  
**Hook:** Grants audiences rarely; his grief after his advisor's death is a rare window into what he actually thinks

---

### Lord Chancellor Miren Ashvale
**Role:** Day-to-day governance of the kingdom — the person who actually runs things  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 58  
**Appearance:** Ink-stained, efficient, has learned to translate between elvish patience and human urgency  
**Personality:** Devoted to the kingdom, not the king — will say so if asked. Has been translating Elowen's pace for 30 years. Exhausted in a way he's learned to ignore.  
**Goals:** Get the Warden appointed; manage the refugee crisis before it becomes a civil one; convince Elowen that 6 weeks is not "soon"  
**Quote:** *"The king is wise. I translate his wisdom into decisions. Sometimes this takes longer than we have."*  
**Hook:** The party's most likely crown contact; pays in crown resources and information; genuinely grateful for competent help

---

### Captain Brek Coldmarch
**Role:** Warden candidate, Nine Hearths refugee, Giant  
**Ancestry:** Giant, age 35  
**Appearance:** Came south through Silver Pass four months ago with nothing; somehow looks like he's always belonged here  
**Personality:** Unassuming, perceptive, trusted by both refugees and locals because he is visibly both  
**Goals:** Help the people he walked south with; not be used as a political symbol  
**Quote:** *"I know what it's like to need a gate to open. I also know what it's like to have a town run out of food. I don't think either side is wrong."*  
**Hook:** The most interesting Warden candidate; his appointment could heal the frontier divide; he doesn't want the job

## FRONTIER FIGURES (3)

### Captain Brennan Tusk
**Role:** Town Captain of Highwatch, frontier's most desperate voice  
**Ancestry:** Giant, age 44  
**Appearance:** Built for the cold, currently built for worry  
**Personality:** Fair, competent, has sent 11 messages to the crown and received 3 responses — still hasn't given up, which says everything  
**Goals:** Keep Highwatch alive through winter; get the Warden appointed; get resources before he has to make a choice he doesn't want to make  
**Quote:** *"I didn't close the gate. I know what it means to be outside one. But I need something. Anything."*  
**Hook:** Hires party for supply runs, scouting, and anything else that keeps Highwatch functional

---

### Captain Sera Vane
**Role:** Town Captain of Ironmere — the one who closed the gate  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 38  
**Appearance:** Looks like she hasn't slept since she made the decision (she hasn't slept well since she made the decision)  
**Personality:** Decisive, not cruel — made the call she thought was necessary, is living with what it cost  
**Goals:** Keep Ironmere stable; survive the crown's censure; prove she was right without being able to forget who paid the price  
**Quote:** *"I made the decision I could defend. I can still defend it. I'm not sure that matters."*  
**Hook:** Central to any moral reckoning about the frontier crisis; can become ally or antagonist depending on party approach

---

### The Coldfast Exile — Elder Hara
**Role:** De facto leader of the Coldfast Exile community in Greypass  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 61  
**Appearance:** Tundra-weathered, carries herself like someone who left something behind  
**Personality:** Grief made functional. Left Coldfast the night after her grandchildren did — told herself it was for them. Mostly believes it.  
**Goals:** Build something real in the Northern Expanse; get a message to Elder Durn; stop grieving long enough to start living  
**Quote:** *"Durn is right that the land makes us who we are. He's wrong that it has to be that land."*  
**Hook:** Message carrier to Frostwell; best source of Nine Hearths knowledge in the kingdom; her community needs help

## RIVER TOWN FIGURES (2)

### Mayor Davan Cole
**Role:** Mayor of Farhold / Ithilvast, the kingdom's loudest political voice  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 49  
**Appearance:** Always dressed for a meeting, even when there isn't one  
**Personality:** Politically skilled, genuinely frustrated, represents real concerns with real self-interest mixed in — hard to separate which is which  
**Goals:** Force the crown to act faster; gain more regional autonomy for river towns; be present when history is made  
**Quote:** *"The king is wise. He is also very far away and very slow and people in Farhold are sleeping in the street."*  
**Hook:** Useful ally (resources, information, crown access); unreliable (his own political interests always present)

---

### Mira Greenwater
**Role:** Halfling elder of Millhaven / Aranthem farming community  
**Ancestry:** Halfling, age 72  
**Appearance:** River mud on her boots always, has spent 70 years watching the Arathandë  
**Personality:** Practical, warm, more politically astute than she appears  
**Goals:** Keep her community fed and stable through the refugee influx; figure out what the river is trying to tell her  
**Quote:** *"Six generations of my family have farmed this valley. The river is talking. I just can't quite hear it yet."*  
**Hook:** River mystery; refugee integration success story; deep knowledge of the Arathandë's normal behavior

## THE FOREST (2)

### Elder Valandil
**Role:** Oldest living elf in the Deepwood — remembers the last Hollostar fall  
**Ancestry:** Elf, age ~800  
**Appearance:** Ancient even by elvish standards — bark-like skin, eyes the color of deep forest  
**Personality:** Speaks slowly. Means everything he says.  
**Goals:** Share what he knows before it's needed and too late; protect the Deepwood's ancient cavern network  
**Secret:** The Deepwood caverns run deep enough and far enough to shelter thousands. He has been quietly preparing them for 40 years.  
**Quote:** *"I have seen one. I will see another. What I can tell you is what matters is where you are standing."*  
**Hook:** The single most valuable information source about surviving a Hollostar impact; deeply distrustful of Seekers and Lumencrest

---

### Thornweave
**Role:** Firbolg elder, forest-edge community leader  
**Ancestry:** Firbolg, age 180  
**Appearance:** Part of the forest more than apart from it  
**Personality:** Direct, territorial about the forest edge, not unkind  
**Goals:** Protect Firbolg territory from agricultural expansion; maintain the old arrangement with the crown  
**Quote:** *"The farmers need more land because the king sent them more mouths. The king should give them land that isn't ours."*  
**Hook:** Forest dispute mediator; Path of Spirits connection; knows things about the forest spirits that even Valandil doesn't

## EAGLE RIDERS (3)

### Rider Commander Sera Windmane
**Role:** Commanding officer of the Great Eagle Riders; the Northern Expanse's most capable military mind in a generation  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 58  
**Appearance:** Sun-weathered and wind-scarred in the specific way of someone who has spent decades at altitude. Silver-streaked hair kept short. Moves like she's always calculating wind direction. Her eagle Ashwind is twenty-six years old — one of the oldest bonded birds in the tradition's history.  
**Personality:** Methodical, direct, and carrying the particular weight of someone who knows exactly what needs to be done and is waiting on authorization that may not come in time. Not bitter about the wait. Focused on what she can do while waiting.  
**Goals:** Get formal authorization to support the Frostwell evacuation; share aerial survey data with whoever can use it; keep the Rider tradition's integrity intact while the king deliberates  
**Current operation:** Running unauthorized supply pre-positioning through civilian brokers in coordination with Marshal-General Corvain (Steelwatch). Three months of back-channel communication. Neither has told their respective chains of command.  
**Her eagle Ashwind:** The bond between Windmane and Ashwind is old enough that both have developed the particular stillness of long partnerships. Ashwind chooses when to fly. Windmane has never once asked him to fly somewhere he refused. This has never been a problem.  
**Quote:** *"The Accord hasn't authorized anything in fourteen months. The refugees are moving now. These two facts require me to make a decision."*  
**Hook:** Needs couriers without official connection to either Steelwatch or the Riders; her survey data about the Hollostar approach vector matches Coldridge's and she doesn't know this yet

---

### Rider Kael Swiftperch
**Role:** Youngest active Rider; bonded two years ago; currently the fastest long-distance courier in the tradition  
**Ancestry:** Halfling, age 19  
**Appearance:** Conspicuously small even among Halflings, which makes the bond with his eagle Copperwind (enormous, temperamental, chose Kael at a ceremony where three more prestigious candidates were passed over) a source of ongoing surprise to everyone except Kael.  
**Personality:** Earnest, observant, and possessed of the specific confidence of someone who was chosen by something that knows what it's doing. Not arrogant — just genuinely not worried about whether he belongs.  
**Goals:** Complete his courier runs without incident; learn everything Windmane is willing to teach; understand what he's seen on recent northern routes  
**What he's seen:** On his last three northern runs, he has flown over refugee columns that were significantly larger than the official estimates the Accord is working from. He reported this up the chain. It was noted. He does not believe it was acted on.  
**Quote:** *"Copperwind sees further than I do. I've learned to trust what he circles."*  
**Hook:** The party's best source for current aerial intelligence on the northern routes; his numbers contradict official estimates; he is young enough to talk to people Windmane can't

---

### Elder Rider Vaen Ashwing
**Role:** The tradition's institutional memory; bonded for thirty-eight years; technically retired but physically present at the Aerie every day  
**Ancestry:** Elf, age 312  
**Appearance:** Ancient even for an elf who has spent his life outdoors. His eagle Dawnbreak died six years ago. He has not accepted a new bond. The Aerie staff have stopped expecting him to.  
**Personality:** Quiet, occasionally devastating, speaks rarely and always means it. The grief of losing a bonded eagle after three decades is something the tradition acknowledges but doesn't have adequate language for. Vaen has found his own language for it: he stays.  
**Goals:** Ensure the tradition survives the current crisis intact; pass on everything he knows before he can't; prevent the political pressure for deployment from overriding the eagles' choice  
**What he knows:** He flew during the last major crisis the Northern Expanse faced — not the Hollostar (before his time) but the border wars with the Drakona riders, 180 years ago. He is the only living person in the Expanse who understands what aerial combat in the Arathandë actually looks like.  
**Secret:** He has been approached by Lumencrest's Expansionist faction about the possibility of Rider support for their Endless Eye operation. He has not responded. He has not told Windmane. He is deciding whether what he knows would help or harm her position.  
**Quote:** *"The eagles chose us because they see something worth choosing. I have spent thirty-eight years trying to deserve that."*  
**Hook:** Deepest institutional knowledge in the tradition; the Lumencrest approach is a thread that could become very important very fast

## ELVISH HEARTLAND (2)

### Lady Aelith Caranthor
**Role:** Senior member of the king's Advisory Circle; the voice in the room who says what the king will not  
**Ancestry:** Elf, age 220  
**Appearance:** The particular elegance of someone who grew up at the Silverwood Palace and has never needed to perform it. Dresses practically. The understated quality of her clothing is more expensive than the elaborate clothing of most people she meets.  
**Personality:** Precise, politically acute, and operating from a position of deep loyalty to the kingdom that occasionally conflicts with her loyalty to the king. She has disagreed with Elowen three times in twenty years. Each time she was right. Each time the disagreement cost her something. She keeps disagreeing.  
**Goals:** Accelerate the Warden appointment; get the king to acknowledge that his timescale and the refugees' timescale are not the same timescale; protect the Advisory Circle's authority from Miren Ashvale's growing operational control  
**Her frustration with Miren:** She respects Ashvale. She is aware that his practical management of the kingdom has been essential. She is also aware that in managing the king's decisions, he has begun making them. This is not a small distinction.  
**Quote:** *"The king has watched a hundred generations of us live and die. He mourns us all. He still needs to decide before the snow."*  
**Hook:** Will work with party if they advance the Warden question; her insight into court politics is invaluable; her disagreements with the king are better-kept secrets than most

### Archvist Solenne
**Role:** Keeper of the Silverwood Palace archives; one of the few people with access to records predating the Heartland Accord  
**Ancestry:** Elf, age 445  
**Appearance:** Pale in the way of someone who has spent most of four centuries indoors. Ink-stained fingers. The practiced stillness of someone who handles fragile things for a living.  
**Personality:** Deeply uninterested in politics, deeply invested in accuracy, occasionally startling in her directness because she has long since stopped worrying about whether people like her answers.  
**Goals:** Protect the archives; find the pre-Accord records the king has asked for regarding previous Hollostar falls; not be drawn into the current political crisis any further than her role requires  
**What she's found:** Three documents in the palace archives that describe the second Hollostar fall (1,200 years ago) from the Northern Expanse's perspective. Two are factual accounts. The third is something else — a personal record by a Rider of that era that describes what the eagles did when the star fell. She has shown the first two to the king. She is still reading the third.  
**Quote:** *"The records say what they say. What they mean is somebody else's department."*  
**Hook:** Archive access; the third document contains information relevant to the current situation; she will share it with someone who demonstrates they will treat it with appropriate care

## L'EMPIRE VERDIENNE BORDER (2)

### Factor-General Corenne Duvray
**Role:** L'Empire Verdienne's senior trade representative in the Northern Expanse; based in Riveaulx  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 46  
**Appearance:** Imperial precision in dress and posture, warm in person — a combination that takes years to develop and signals someone who has been doing this long enough to stop performing either quality.  
**Personality:** Genuinely likes the Northern Expanse. Has been stationed here for nine years and considers it a posting she chose rather than one she was assigned. Manages the empire's interests here with an eye toward the long relationship, not the current season.  
**Goals:** Keep the trade corridor functional as the refugee crisis strains northern infrastructure; get the empire's formal position on Frostwell aid established before Lumencrest fills the vacuum; protect Riveaulx's position as the empire's preferred northern port  
**Her calculation:** The empire does not officially recognize Hollostar cycles as a political concern. Corenne does. She has been writing reports for eight months. The reports have been received. She does not know what is being done with them.  
**Quote:** *"The empire will be helpful. The question is whether 'helpful' arrives before it's needed or after."*  
**Hook:** The imperial connection — access to L'Empire resources and diplomatic weight; her frustration with the empire's response time is a genuine crack in official positioning

---

### Harbormaster Bren Tillivast
**Role:** Harbormaster of Sainteloise, the Northern Expanse's main border port with the empire  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 53  
**Appearance:** Practical and perpetually damp. Has run Sainteloise's harbor for nineteen years. Knows every captain who regularly runs the northern trade route and a significant number who don't.  
**Personality:** Cheerful in the way of someone who has accepted that their job is to manage chaos and finds this more interesting than the alternative. Genuinely good at improvising solutions. Currently improvising solutions every day.  
**Goals:** Keep Sainteloise's harbor functioning under refugee-era traffic; manage the growing backlog of imperial ships waiting for clearance; figure out where the three missing cargo manifests went  
**The missing manifests:** Three ships with imperial registration have arrived at Sainteloise in the past month with documentation gaps that are technically in order but practically anomalous. Tillivast has not flagged them officially because he is not certain they're a problem. He is certain they're worth noticing.  
**Quote:** *"Every ship has a story. The ones with no story are the ones that keep me awake."*  
**Hook:** Gateway to imperial shipping intelligence; the missing manifests lead somewhere; he will work with people who help him keep the harbor moving
