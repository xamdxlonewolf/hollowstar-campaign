# NPCs - Silverbelt Coast

---

## THE FOUR HOUSES (6)

### Admiral-Lord Cassius Vethara
**Role:** Head of House Vethara, presiding member of Council of Banners  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 61  
**Appearance:** Silver-streaked hair, naval uniform even at dinner, rings on right hand only (left always bare — "ready")  
**Personality:** Brilliant, cold, three moves ahead. Warmth reserved for those who've earned it — and few have.  
**Goals:** Absorb Caldren territory, cement Lumencrest partnership, ensure Vethara dominates next generation  
**Flaw:** Believes everyone is ultimately controllable if you find their price. Wrong about some people.  
**Quote:** *"Sentiment is expensive. I prefer investments."*  
**Hook:** Hires party for sensitive work — pays well, expects discretion, tests loyalty before trusting

---

### Lady-Admiral Sera Mourne
**Role:** Head of House Mourne  
**Ancestry:** Human (Coral ancestry, unacknowledged), age 44  
**Appearance:** Sea-green eyes (Coral trait), graceful, always perfectly composed, small pearl earring she never removes (grandmother's)  
**Personality:** Politically brilliant, genuinely uncertain beneath the composure. Plays the game better than anyone but questions whether she wants to.  
**Goals:** Keep Mourne independent — from both Vethara AND Lumencrest. Find balance.  
**Secret:** Has been in quiet contact with Coral elders at Silverspan Reef. Beginning to acknowledge what she is.  
**Quote:** *"Patience is a strategy, not a weakness."*  
**Hook:** Needs discreet agents; her wavering alliance makes her dangerous to work for and valuable to know

---

### Lord-Admiral Brennan Aldric
**Role:** Head of House Aldric  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 57  
**Appearance:** Weathered, scarred jaw (pirate fight at 17), stands like he's still on a deck  
**Personality:** Deeply honorable, deeply inflexible. Genuinely believes in what he stands for. Tragic in the way all true believers are when the world changes.  
**Goals:** Back a legitimate Caldren heir, drive out Lumencrest influence, maintain Silverbelt traditions  
**Conflict:** His heir Lysa disagrees with him. He knows. He's choosing not to see it.  
**Quote:** *"My grandfather held this coast. My father held it. I will hold it. And if I fail, Lysa will hold it."*  
**Hook:** Hires for anti-piracy, anti-Lumencrest work; honorable employer — if you share his values

---

### Commander Lysa Aldric
**Role:** Brennan's heir, completed naval service, waiting to inherit  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 28  
**Appearance:** Looks like her father minus 30 years and the certainty  
**Personality:** Her father's discipline, different conclusions. Pragmatic where he's principled.  
**Goals:** Inherit without betraying father; find a way to bring Aldric forward without destroying it  
**Secret:** Has had two secret meetings with Lumencrest representatives. Nothing agreed. Just listening.  
**Quote:** *"Tradition is worth preserving. Not all of it. The hard part is knowing which."*  
**Hook:** Needs go-betweens who won't report to her father; offers future favor from next Aldric head

---

### Steward Orvyn Glast
**Role:** Caldren steward — managing House in crisis  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 63  
**Appearance:** Exhausted, ink-stained, reading glasses perpetually pushed up on forehead  
**Personality:** Deeply competent administrator who never wanted political power. Just wants someone legitimate to hand this to so he can retire.  
**Goals:** Find a legitimate heir, keep Caldren towns stable, not start a war  
**Quote:** *"I manage estates. I do not manage crises. Apparently no one told the crisis."*  
**Hook:** Hires party to find/protect Mira; pays fairly, asks honestly, genuinely grateful for help

---

### Mira Caldren-Ashvale
**Role:** Distant Caldren heir — doesn't want the title  
**Ancestry:** Human/Katari, age 22  
**Appearance:** River pilot build (wiry, weathered), Katari father's warm brown complexion, Caldren green eyes  
**Personality:** Practical, direct, uncomfortable with grandeur. Knows every meter of the Oldrun River. Knows nothing about court politics. Learning fast because she has to.  
**Goals:** Keep her community (Calmwater) safe. Not become a puppet. Maybe, quietly, do right by the people Caldren should have been protecting.  
**Conflict:** Doesn't want power. Increasingly can't pretend someone else should have it.  
**Quote:** *"I know where every sandbar on this river is. That's what I know. That's all I know."*  
**Hook:** Central to succession crisis; rewards honesty; will remember who helped her before she had power

## THE GREY SAILS (3)

### Captain Ilysse Dawnrak
**Role:** Leader of the Grey Sails pirate fleet; the most strategically sophisticated criminal operator on the Silver Belt Sea  
**Ancestry:** Human-Katari mixed, age 39  
**Appearance:** Dark complexion, salt-bleached hair cropped close, missing the tip of her left ring finger (has told three different stories about why, all plausible). Dresses practically. Her one visible luxury is a compass that predates the Four Houses — she says it belonged to someone who knew where they were going.  
**Personality:** Patient, calculating, charismatic in the way of someone who has led dangerous people through dangerous situations and come out ahead. Does not raise her voice. People pay more attention when she speaks quietly. Tells the truth about her goals when asked directly. Considers deception wasteful.  
**Goals:** Transition the Grey Sails from piracy toward legitimacy — specifically, to be the private naval force that the incoming Caldren heir hires rather than fights. The Caldren succession crisis is the best window she will get. She intends to use it.  
**What she doesn't know:** The Tide King Voss has extended his protection network into Silver Belt merchant traffic. Several ships the Grey Sails have raided in the past eight months were paying Voss's cut. Voss knows. He is building a response.  
**Her intelligence source:** A single contact inside the Four Houses who has been providing patrol schedule information for six years. She will not say who. If revealed, it would be the worst kind of betrayal in House politics.  
**Quote:** *"I want the same thing everyone who has ever sailed these waters has wanted. To matter. I've simply chosen a more direct route."*  
**Hook:** Needs intermediaries for her legitimacy negotiation with the incoming Caldren heir; will tell the party the truth about her goals if asked; the Voss collision course is an approaching crisis neither side has named yet

---

### Captain Orren Blackwater
**Role:** Commander of the Saltwind, the Grey Sails' primary warship; tactical mind of the fleet  
**Ancestry:** Dwarf, age 82  
**Appearance:** Former House Mourne naval officer — still carries himself like one. Broad, deliberate, never rushes. The most formally trained sailor in the fleet by a significant margin.  
**Personality:** Precise, professionally loyal to Dawnrak, personally complicated about the circumstances that led him here. His defection from House Mourne was a scandal. He has not explained it to anyone in the fifteen years since. He carries the explanation the way people carry things they have decided to live with.  
**Goals:** Keep the Saltwind operational; ensure the fleet's tactical decisions are sound; survive long enough to see whether Dawnrak's legitimacy plan works  
**Lady-Admiral Sera Mourne connection:** He left House Mourne under circumstances Sera Mourne has never fully accepted. She has consistently avoided aggressive action against the Saltwind specifically, which several of her naval officers have noticed. He has noticed that she has noticed. Neither has acted on this.  
**Quote:** *"I know what I left. I know what I came to. I have made my accounting. It is not your accounting to make."*  
**Hook:** The Mourne connection is a thread that could be pulled from either direction; his tactical knowledge is exceptional; the story of why he left is worth knowing and hard to get

---

### Tarn
**Role:** Grey Sails' intelligence cell coordinator in Tidewall; the fleet's eyes in the city  
**Ancestry:** Half-Elf, age 31  
**Appearance:** Harbor worker presentation — appropriately weathered, appropriately unremarkable, the product of deliberate effort rather than circumstance  
**Personality:** Professional, careful, increasingly aware that the Harbor-Master Dael has been watching them for three months with the particular attention of someone who knows but hasn't acted. This is either a courtesy or preparation. Tarn has not determined which.  
**Goals:** Maintain the intelligence cell's cover; collect and transmit advance warning of House naval movements; not be the reason the cell collapses  
**Known status:** Harbor-Master Dael has identified them as a Grey Sails contact. This information has been reported up the House Vethara intelligence chain. It has not yet been acted upon. Tarn does not know they are known. Dael does not know that Tarn doesn't know.  
**Quote:** *"I work the docks. I notice things. Things noticed are things worth something. That's all."*  
**Hook:** The known-but-unacted-upon intelligence situation is a ticking scenario; the party can exploit it, expose it, or use it as leverage with either side

## THE CORAL (3 expanded)

### Reef-Elder Solen
**Role:** The Silverspan Reef's most senior elder; the Coral community's primary voice on questions of change and outside contact  
**Ancestry:** Coral, age 187  
**Appearance:** Old enough that his bioluminescent patches have settled into a constant warm amber rather than the responsive patterns of younger Coral — they no longer shift with emotion but glow steadily, which the reef community reads as a form of deep calm. Moves slowly. Everything he does is deliberate.  
**Personality:** The particular patience of someone who has watched the reef through three generations of outside threat and one period of genuine crisis. Not dismissive of urgency — rather, he has seen what urgency without understanding costs, and he is cautious about it.  
**Goals:** Understand the changes happening in the coastal water conditions before deciding how the reef responds; maintain the Coral community's independence while managing the increasing surface-world pressure; keep the knowledge of the reef's deeper sections from reaching Lumencrest  
**What he's tracking:** The coastal water conditions have been shifting for two years — temperature, mineral content, the trace energy from the river system that keeps the reef healthy. He connects this to something large happening inland. He does not know what. He has sent two scouts to the surface to find out. They have not yet returned with answers.  
**Quote:** *"The reef has survived every storm that has come for it. It has survived them by being older than the storms. I do not know if that holds for what is coming."*  
**Hook:** The reef's knowledge of the Silver Belt Sea is unmatched; his concern about the water changes is a thread connecting the Silverbelt to the Endless Eye crisis; the surface scouts he sent have found something they're not sure how to bring back

### Crest
**Role:** Young Coral living on the surface; the reef community's most connected liaison with surface culture  
**Ancestry:** Coral, age 24 (they/them)  
**Appearance:** Tall for Coral, with bioluminescent patches that run more teal than the reef community's typical blue-white — a variation that marks them as belonging to one of the older reef lineages. Dresses in surface clothing with the slightly-off quality of someone who learned it from observation rather than growing up in it.  
**Personality:** Curious, adaptable, and managing the particular identity complexity of someone who belongs to two worlds and is completely comfortable in neither and both simultaneously. Not troubled by this — interested in it.  
**Goals:** Build genuine relationships on the surface rather than just functional ones; understand what the surface world knows about what's happening to the coastal waters; carry what they learn back to Reef-Elder Solen  
**Their situation:** Has been living in Tidewall for eight months. Has friends on the docks and a complicated acquaintance with Harbor-Master Dael who is professionally curious about everything and therefore curious about them. Has not told the reef community that Dael knows what they are.  
**Quote:** *"Surface people find it strange that I don't want things. I want things. I just want different things than they expect."*  
**Hook:** The bridge between the reef and any surface-world party; Dael's knowledge of their identity is a vulnerability; their observations about what's changing in the water are more detailed than anyone on the surface has

---

### Deepkeeper Miru
**Role:** The reef's most experienced diver into the outer sea — the sections beyond the inhabited reef structure  
**Ancestry:** Coral, age 41  
**Appearance:** Marked by deep-water exposure in ways that distinguish Coral who regularly go beyond the reef: slightly paler bioluminescence, pressure adaptations visible in posture. Moves differently in shallow water than deep — more comfortable below.  
**Personality:** Quiet by Coral standards, which is very quiet. Processes information slowly and thoroughly. The deliberateness is not hesitation — it is the habit of someone who has learned that incorrect assessments in deep water have permanent consequences.  
**Goals:** Map what is happening to the deeper sea floor structure south of the reef; find the source of the new current pattern that has been pushing warm water northward for the past three months; not alarm the reef community before she knows whether alarm is warranted  
**The current:** Something is warming the deep southern waters in a pattern that doesn't match seasonal variation. Miru has been tracking it for a year. Her best theory — still unconfirmed — is that it connects to volcanic activity near the Desert of Glass. If she is right, the implications for the entire Silver Belt Sea coastline are significant. She has told only Reef-Elder Solen.  
**Quote:** *"The water doesn't lie. It just takes patience to hear what it's saying."*  
**Hook:** The warm current mystery connects Silverbelt to Desert of Glass; her mapping data is something Starfall Seekers, Lumencrest, and the Four Houses would each want for different reasons; she will only share it through Coral channels

## VETHERIS TRADE HUB (3)

### Master Factor Aldric Veyne
**Role:** Senior factor of House Vethara's Vetheris operation; the most powerful commercial figure in the city  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 61  
**Appearance:** The cultivated neutrality of someone who has spent forty years ensuring that no one in a negotiation can read his position. The tells are there — they just require knowing him for a decade to see.  
**Personality:** Professionally pleasant, substantively calculating, and possessed of an accurate model of what every merchant house in Vetheris actually wants versus what they say they want. He trades in this gap constantly.  
**Goals:** Maintain House Vethara's commercial dominance in Vetheris while the Caldren succession leaves a power vacuum; identify which of his counterparts are taking Lumencrest money; not be on the wrong side of whatever Cassius Vethara is planning  
**His concern:** Three trade routes that normally run through Vetheris have rerouted south in the past month. Not dramatically — just enough to notice if you're tracking volume. Someone is quietly redirecting commerce away from Vethara-controlled channels. He has a list of three suspects. He does not yet know which one is right.  
**Quote:** *"Every merchant in Vetheris tells me they're doing well. I believe the ones whose numbers match what they're telling me."*  
**Hook:** Commercial intelligence on the Four Houses' actual state; the rerouted trade routes are a mystery with political implications; he will pay for information and pay more for discretion

---

### Linn Driftmarck
**Role:** Independent factor, the largest non-House commercial operation in Vetheris  
**Ancestry:** Halfling, age 44  
**Appearance:** Compact, efficient, carries a ledger that she has maintained herself for twenty-two years and which contains more commercially sensitive information about Vetheris than the Four Houses combined would be comfortable knowing she has.  
**Personality:** Scrupulously fair, genuinely independent, and in possession of the specific confidence of someone who has survived twenty years of not belonging to any of the Houses and has found this to be an advantage. She owes favors to no House. She is owed favors by all of them.  
**Goals:** Keep her operation's independence as the Caldren vacuum attracts opportunists; find a buyer for information she has obtained about the rerouted trade routes that she cannot act on herself; navigate the Lumencrest infiltration without becoming a target  
**Her information:** She knows which Vetheris factor is rerouting the three trade routes. She knows because she traced the paperwork. She has not sold this yet because the buyer she has in mind is not someone she wants to approach through normal channels. She needs an intermediary.  
**Quote:** *"I don't have a House. That means I can do business with all of them. It also means none of them protect me. I have found this acceptable."*  
**Hook:** The intermediary role — she needs someone to carry sensitive commercial intelligence to a buyer who can act on it; her ledger is a weapon and she knows how to use it

### Tomas Veth (Saltblood)
**Role:** Harbormaster of Vethport — the coastal transfer point where river boats become ocean ships  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 57  
**Appearance:** The look of someone who has been solving logistical problems since before the party was born and has developed a certain pleasure in impossible ones. Missing three fingers on his left hand from a rope accident thirty years ago that he mentions cheerfully when relevant.  
**Personality:** Practical, direct, and operating with the particular authority of someone whose cooperation is essential to literally every piece of commerce flowing through the Silverbelt's main transit point. He is polite about this authority. He does not need to be anything else.  
**Goals:** Keep Vethport functional through the commercial disruption; manage the increase in unscheduled ships arriving from the Endless Eye direction; figure out why three of those ships have had unusual cargo manifests  
**The unusual manifests:** Similar to what Harbormaster Tillivast noticed in Sainteloise — ships with documentation that is technically complete but practically anomalous. The pattern suggests coordination. He has told Master Factor Veyne. Veyne said he would look into it. That was three weeks ago.  
**Quote:** *"Every ship goes somewhere. Every cargo comes from somewhere. The ones that don't have a clear answer to both questions are the interesting ones."*  
**Hook:** Vethport intelligence on commercial flows; the unusual manifests connect to something larger; he controls physical access to the most important transit point on the coast
