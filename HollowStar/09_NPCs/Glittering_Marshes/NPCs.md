# NPCs - Glittering Marshes

---

## ELDER COUNCIL (3)

### Elder Mira Fen
**Role:** Reedgate elder, de facto council voice to the outside world  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 56  
**Appearance:** Marsh-guide practical — weatherproof, always has a pole within reach, eyes that read water surfaces instinctively  
**Personality:** The marsh's diplomat, which is a role she holds carefully. Warm to people who deserve it, utterly immovable on certain things. Has said "no" to Lumencrest four times. Is preparing to say it a fifth.  
**Goals:** Keep Lumencrest out; keep the guide trade clean; make sure whatever the Seekers' instruments picked up doesn't get followed up on  
**Quote:** *"You're welcome in Reedgate. The marsh beyond it is a different conversation."*  
**Hook:** First point of contact for any outsider; knows more about external threats to the marsh than she lets on

---

### Elder Broum Shell
**Role:** Shellmound elder (Galapa), keeper of the oldest records  
**Ancestry:** Galapa, age 203  
**Appearance:** Shell worn smooth by decades of marsh water, faint permanent luminescence at the shell's edges (lifetime of shard-light exposure)  
**Personality:** Moves and speaks at Galapa pace — deliberate, thorough, takes as long as it takes. Has outlived six other elders. Remembers things nobody else does.  
**Goals:** Protect the archive; share what is useful when it becomes necessary; outlive whatever crisis is coming (likely)  
**Quote:** *"The records say the light came before us. I see no reason to argue with the records."*  
**Hook:** The archive with the impact account; will share if convinced it serves the marsh

---

### Elder Pip
**Role:** Deepstilt elder (Ribbet), guardian of the deepest shard knowledge  
**Ancestry:** Ribbet, age 71  
**Appearance:** Small even for Ribbet, moves through the stilt network faster than people half their age  
**Personality:** Cheerful, chatty, will tell you everything about Deepstilt except the one thing — and does so in a way that takes outsiders a while to notice they've been told nothing about the floor  
**Goals:** Keep the largest shard fragment undisturbed; maintain Deepstilt's relationship with the light beneath them  
**Quote:** *"Beautiful down there, isn't it? The marsh has always been beautiful here. Will you stay for dinner?"*  
**Hook:** The gentle gatekeeper to the most significant secret in the marsh

## GUIDES (3)

### Wren Mudpath
**Role:** Senior licensed guide, Reedgate — most experienced in the marsh  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 48  
**Appearance:** Can read water depth by color, channel age by smell, weather by how the bioluminescence pulses  
**Personality:** Professional warmth — genuinely likes people, genuinely manages what they see  
**Goals:** Keep the guide tradition's reputation intact; retire eventually; not let Lumencrest establish a beachhead  
**Quote:** *"I'll take you where you need to go. The marsh decides where that is."*  
**Hook:** Best guide for any deep marsh work; their route management is artful — party may not realize how much they're being steered until later

---

### Tessla
**Role:** The unlicensed guide — young Ribbet, desperate, in over their head  
**Ancestry:** Ribbet, age 19  
**Appearance:** Nervous, knows the marsh well (grew up in it), doesn't know the politics well enough  
**Personality:** Not malicious — genuinely needed money, genuinely didn't think it through  
**Goals:** Not get caught; pay off the debt that started this; figure out what to do about the outsiders who are now asking about the floor  
**Quote:** *"I just needed — look, I know the routes. I know them as well as Wren does."*  
**Hook:** The unlicensed guide hook; also knows something about what the outsiders she's been guiding are actually after

---

### Old Chorus
**Role:** Retired Ribbet guide, Deepstilt — knows the marsh better than anyone alive  
**Ancestry:** Ribbet, age 89  
**Appearance:** Doesn't move fast anymore; doesn't need to  
**Personality:** The marsh's oral historian — has guided three generations of outsiders and remembers every one. Tells stories constantly. The stories contain information if you listen correctly.  
**Goals:** Pass on what they know before they can't; make sure Deepstilt's secret survives them  
**Quote:** *"I took a Lumencrest man through the marsh forty years ago. He left without what he came for. They always do, eventually."*  
**Hook:** Deep marsh history; may tell the party things nobody else will, in story form

## OUTSIDE CONNECTIONS (2)

### Researcher Dolt Cassen (Lumencrest)
**Role:** Lumencrest researcher stationed at Reedgate — officially studying marsh ecology  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 37  
**Appearance:** Academic, out of his depth physically but not intellectually  
**Personality:** Genuinely scientifically curious, also genuinely carrying instructions about what to find. Uncomfortable with how much these two things are starting to conflict.  
**Goals:** Document marsh bioluminescence officially; find evidence of unusual energy sources unofficially; not think too hard about what Lumencrest will do with either  
**Quote:** *"The readings are — look, the readings shouldn't be what they are. Something is producing energy at a wavelength I can't explain biologically."*  
**Hook:** His instruments are getting close to the right answer; what he does when he finds it depends on who he's talked to

---

### Warden Sable of Waters (Green Covenant)
**Role:** Covenant's Warden of Waters, based at Mosshaven  
**Ancestry:** Elf, age 140  
**Appearance:** The specific look of someone who spends equal time in forest and water — practical for both  
**Personality:** Careful, long-view, has been watching the marsh-forest border for sixty years  
**Goals:** Maintain the Verdant Crescent-marsh relationship; manage the ancient waking's potential spread; warn the elder council without causing panic  
**Quote:** *"The forest is responding to something. The marsh has been sitting on top of something for millennia. I don't think that's a coincidence."*  
**Hook:** The connection between the ancient waking and the marsh shards; brings the two regions into the same story
