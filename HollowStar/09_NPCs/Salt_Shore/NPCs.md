# NPCs - The Salt Shore

---

## THE TIDE KING'S CIRCLE (4)

### Voss — The Tide King
**Role:** De facto ruler of the Salt Shore  
**Ancestry:** Firbolg, age 51  
**Appearance:** 7'2", grey-streaked fur, amber glass eye (left), necklace of tide tokens — one per town  
**Personality:** Calculating and fair. Listens more than he speaks. When he speaks, people stop talking. Genuinely cares about the Salt Shore — doesn't need you to know that.  
**Goals:** Stop Saltmere's creep without starting a war; eventually die in his bed (not guaranteed)  
**Quote:** *"Tell me what you actually want. We'll get to what you think you want later."*  
**Hook:** Hires party for sensitive work; The Anchor is the access point

---

### Harbormaster Liss
**Role:** Brinehaven harbormaster, Shore Eyes coordinator, the person who actually runs things day-to-day  
**Ancestry:** Ribbet, age 44  
**Appearance:** Compact, efficient, always has three conversations happening at once  
**Personality:** Organized, loyal to Voss without being blind about him, protective of her harbor  
**Goals:** Keep Brinehaven functional; identify the Shore Eyes leak before Voss does (she blames herself for missing it)  
**Quote:** *"Ship's in. Ship's out. Everything else is a problem. I have a list."*  
**Hook:** First point of contact for anyone approaching Voss; has information nobody else has

---

### Sela (Voss's Daughter)
**Role:** Voss's information operative — unofficial, essential  
**Ancestry:** Firbolg, age 23  
**Appearance:** Her father's height, not yet his stillness — moves like she's always about to go somewhere  
**Personality:** Smarter than she lets on (which is already smart), impatient with her father's caution, will take risks he won't  
**Goals:** Deal with the Saltmere problem more aggressively than her father allows; prove herself  
**Secret:** Has her own source inside Saltmere — hasn't told Voss  
**Quote:** *"Dad waits to see what people do. I prefer to find out."*  
**Hook:** Approaches party independently for jobs Voss hasn't authorized; can become long-term ally

---

### The Ledger
**Role:** Financial administrator of the cut system  
**Ancestry:** Unknown — genuinely won't say, not as affectation but as policy  
**Appearance:** Unremarkable by design (medium height, neutral clothing, forgettable face)  
**Personality:** Purely transactional in professional context; rumored to be completely different off-duty (no one's confirmed this)  
**Goals:** The books balance. That's it. That's everything.  
**Quote:** *"The number is the number. Feelings about the number are your business."*  
**Hook:** Comes to party when there's a financial dispute to resolve; knows where every coin in the Salt Shore flows

## TOWN FIGURES (4)

### Elder Tobas
**Role:** Unofficial voice of Tidewrack  
**Ancestry:** Halfling, age 71  
**Appearance:** Ancient, slower than he was, eyes still sharp  
**Personality:** Warm, stubborn, protective of his town's independence with a fierceness that surprises people who underestimate old Halflings  
**Goals:** Keep Tidewrack free; make sure the town remembers what Voss did for them; outlive his grandchildren's skepticism  
**Quote:** *"I was there. You weren't. That matters."*  
**Hook:** Wants party to talk to the defecting family; will share everything he knows about Saltmere's approach

---

### Mayor Bess Creel
**Role:** Driftmere mayor, Saltmere-aligned  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 52  
**Appearance:** Well-dressed (recently — Saltmere money), organized, defensive  
**Personality:** Genuinely believes she's helping Driftmere. Not corrupt in her own mind — pragmatic. Getting increasingly uncomfortable with what pragmatism is costing her.  
**Goals:** Secure Driftmere's future; begin to wonder if she's made a mistake  
**Quote:** *"I did what was best for this town. I still believe that."*  
**Hook:** She's not unreachable — someone who can show her the treaty's real implications might change her mind

---

### Madam Cress
**Role:** Unofficial leader of Saltholm, former smuggler  
**Ancestry:** Human, age (late 60s — won't specify)  
**Appearance:** Silver-haired, completely comfortable with silence, excellent at cards  
**Personality:** Has seen everything, fears nothing, takes genuine pleasure in negotiation  
**Goals:** Keep Saltholm neutral and profitable; enjoy her retirement; win more card games  
**Quote:** *"I'll pay the cut. I'll negotiate the cut. I won't pretend the cut is something other than what it is."*  
**Hook:** Neutral meeting point for all parties; knows every secret that passes through Saltholm

---

### Captain Ren Saltblood
**Role:** Voss's most trusted fleet captain  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 34  
**Appearance:** Sun-damaged skin, quick movements, never fully relaxed even on land  
**Personality:** Professionally focused, personally warm with people she trusts, deeply loyal to Voss (genuinely, not just professionally)  
**Goals:** Keep the fleet effective; protect the Salt Shore; get home safely  
**Quote:** *"I know these waters better than I know most people. The waters are more reliable."*  
**Hook:** Party's main naval contact; can get them passage anywhere on the Salt Shore; the compromised captain situation involves someone she trained

## SALTMERE AGENT (1)

### Maret Dulce
**Role:** Saltmere factor in Brinehaven — officially trade representative, actually Covenant intelligence  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 38  
**Appearance:** Pleasant, forgettable on purpose — looks like exactly what she claims to be  
**Personality:** Patient, professional, genuinely pleasant to interact with, ruthless about her objectives  
**Goals:** Expand Saltmere's trade network into Salt Shore; weaken Voss's informal authority enough that towns see Saltmere as the better option  
**Secret:** Has a third-party contact that neither Saltmere nor Voss knows about  
**Quote:** *"I'm just here for trade. Everyone says that, I know. In my case it's also true."*  
**Hook:** The party's most likely Saltmere contact; scrupulously abides by Voss's rules while undermining his authority

## ISLAND FIGURES (2)

### The Keeper of Redcliff
**Role:** Elder of the Firbolg/Faun community on Redcliff  
**Ancestry:** Firbolg, age (very old — 80s?)  
**Appearance:** Unhurried, earth-colored clothing, never seems cold despite wind  
**Personality:** Reluctant to ask for help; more reluctant to admit something is beyond the community  
**Goals:** Contain whatever came out of the cave; protect the community; not tell the mainland more than necessary  
**Quote:** *"We opened something we didn't understand. We would appreciate assistance closing it."*  
**Hook:** The only gateway to Redcliff's mystery; pays in island knowledge and old secrets

---

### The Voice of the Scatter
**Role:** No formal role — just the person everyone on The Scatter seems to ask when things go wrong  
**Ancestry:** Half-Katari, half-Ribbet, age ~40  
**Appearance:** Neither fully one thing nor another — at ease in both water and desert  
**Personality:** Endlessly curious, collects people the way others collect debts — but the people actually like it  
**Goals:** Keep The Scatter's informal community alive; understand the strange compass readings near Far Isle  
**Quote:** *"Everyone's from somewhere else here. That means nobody's a stranger."*  
**Hook:** Best source of island information; knows who's on which island and roughly why
