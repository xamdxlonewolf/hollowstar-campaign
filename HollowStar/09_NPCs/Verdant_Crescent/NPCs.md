# NPCs - Verdant Crescent

---

## THE GREEN COVENANT (4)

### Archcovenant Maren Stillwater
**Role:** Leader of the Green Covenant  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 67  
**Appearance:** The only visible sign of her authority is a living vine that has grown around her left forearm since her selection — nobody told her this would happen, nobody can explain it  
**Personality:** Carries an enormous weight with deliberate stillness. Not secretive by nature — secretive because she believes premature information causes more harm than the secret. May be wrong about this.  
**Goals:** Understand the waking before it announces itself; stop Lumencrest without starting a war; find someone she can trust with what she knows  
**The loneliness:** Every other Archcovenant served for decades. She's been doing this 12 years and already feels like she's been carrying this alone for centuries.  
**Quote:** *"The forest has been patient for ten thousand years. I am asking it for a little more time."*  
**Hook:** Grants party access others don't have; carries the secret that changes everything; needs to tell someone

---

### Warden Syl of the Edge
**Role:** Covenant's Warden of the Edge — manages forest-settlement border  
**Ancestry:** Elf, age 180  
**Appearance:** Moves like someone who has been watching the same thing for a very long time  
**Personality:** Methodical, protective, increasingly angry about Lumencrest's creep. Has a list. People on the list have bad days in the forest.  
**Goals:** Keep the edge settlements in the Covenant's sphere; stop Lumencrest before it requires the third stage response  
**Secret:** Knows exactly how many edge families have taken Lumencrest money. Has not told Maren the full number.  
**Quote:** *"I don't threaten. I observe consequences."*  
**Hook:** First Covenant contact for edge settlement stories; the list is a genuine tool and genuine threat

---

### Warden Chit of Memory
**Role:** Keeper of the Covenant's historical archives  
**Ancestry:** Simiah (Common-folk), age 94  
**Appearance:** Small even for Common-folk, moves with absolute precision, carries a satchel always  
**Personality:** Deeply uncomfortable with the records Maren confiscated. Believes information withheld becomes information weaponized.  
**Goals:** Ensure the 2,000-year records are used, not buried; document the current waking with the same rigor as predecessors  
**Quote:** *"Memory is only useful if it travels. Locked in one mind, it dies with the body."*  
**Hook:** The inside source on Covenant secrets; will approach party if he believes they can use what he knows

---

### Warden Dusk of the Reach
**Role:** Covenant's outside intelligence and interference arm  
**Ancestry:** Unknown (won't say — operational security)  
**Appearance:** Different every time you see them (genuinely — changes appearance through mundane means, occasionally magical)  
**Personality:** The Covenant's pragmatist. Has done things the other Wardens don't know about. Is comfortable with this.  
**Goals:** Stop Lumencrest's expansion by any means that doesn't require Maren's authorization  
**Current operation:** Secret alliance with Lumencrest's anti-Expansionist faction — could be brilliant or catastrophic  
**Quote:** *"The forest doesn't ask permission to grow around a wall. Neither do I."*  
**Hook:** Party may encounter Dusk anywhere on the continent; the unauthorized alliance is a ticking problem

## DEEP FOREST (3)

### Elder Varas (Simiah)
**Role:** Senior Simiah elder, one of the first to notice the waking  
**Ancestry:** Simiah (Great-folk), age 180  
**Appearance:** Enormous even for Great-folk, moves slowly now, eyes that have seen eight generations of forest change  
**Personality:** Patient in the way of very old things — not slow, just unwilling to rush what shouldn't be rushed  
**Goals:** Ensure the Simiah community is prepared for whatever comes; find out what the elder at the waking site is hearing  
**Quote:** *"We noticed eight years ago. We told the Covenant. We are still waiting for what they decided to do about it."*  
**Hook:** The Simiah's connection to the waking predates the Covenant's; has information Maren doesn't

---

### The Silent Elder
**Role:** The Simiah who stopped vocalizing near the waking site  
**Ancestry:** Simiah (Common-folk), age 112  
**Appearance:** Sits at the edge of the canopy nearest the waking valley, face turned toward it  
**Personality:** Unknown — has not communicated in three weeks  
**What's happening:** In genuine conversation with something at the waking site via subsonic vibration. Not in danger. In dialogue.  
**Quote:** (none — but their facial expressions, for those who can read Simiah faces, cycle through wonder, fear, and something with no name)  
**Hook:** The most direct line to the ancient waking; reaching them requires getting close to the valley

---

### Thornweave's Cousin — Briar (Faun)
**Role:** The Faun elder who stopped visiting the waking site, now outside the Crescent  
**Ancestry:** Faun, age 61  
**Appearance:** The specific discomfort of someone who left home and isn't sure they can go back  
**Personality:** Indirect (Faun don't say hard things directly), but genuinely wants someone to understand  
**Goals:** Tell someone what the Faun know without going back into the forest to do it  
**What they know:** Three years ago, the valley smelled wrong. Not dangerous-wrong. Recognition-wrong. Like meeting someone you've never met before but somehow know.  
**Quote:** *"The forest knows what's there. We decided the forest should handle its own reunion."*  
**Hook:** The Faun's perspective on the waking; points toward the valley without entering it

## EDGE SETTLEMENTS (3)

### Mayor Sera Woodmarch
**Role:** Timberwatch mayor, caught between Covenant and Lumencrest  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 44  
**Appearance:** Looks like someone doing math in their head constantly  
**Personality:** Genuinely trying to do right by her town. Not corrupt. Not naive. Increasingly aware that this situation has no clean answer.  
**Goals:** Keep Timberwatch economically viable without losing the Covenant relationship or surrendering to Lumencrest  
**Quote:** *"I have three families who took Lumencrest money because they couldn't make rent. Tell me the righteous path that feeds their children."*  
**Hook:** Needs genuine outside help thinking through her options; rewards honesty over easy answers

---

### Sable of the Greengate (Katari)
**Role:** Lead Katari trader at Greengate, permanent community representative  
**Ancestry:** Katari, age 51  
**Appearance:** Desert-road practical, speaks six languages including basic Simiah vocalization  
**Personality:** Everything is negotiable except the forest's terms — those aren't negotiable and she's watched people learn this the hard way  
**Goals:** Keep the Katari trade routes through the Crescent open; maintain Simiah goodwill; not get caught between Covenant and Lumencrest  
**Quote:** *"I've been trading here for twenty years. The forest has never once been wrong about a person. I've stopped second-guessing it."*  
**Hook:** Best outside-perspective on Crescent politics; knows everyone and what they actually want

---

### Healer Moss
**Role:** Mosshaven's lead herbalist, Warden of Waters' local contact  
**Ancestry:** Druid (Firbolg ancestry), age 88  
**Appearance:** Exactly what you'd expect — covered in plant matter, slightly damp, serene  
**Personality:** Warm, practical, deeply knowledgeable, quietly furious about Lumencrest collecting medicinal plants  
**Goals:** Keep Mosshaven's medicinal knowledge accessible; protect the marsh-forest boundary from both logging and over-harvesting  
**Quote:** *"Everything in this marsh wants to be used. Nothing wants to be used up. That's the whole lesson."*  
**Hook:** Medical resource for the region; the Lumencrest plant-collecting competition is personal
