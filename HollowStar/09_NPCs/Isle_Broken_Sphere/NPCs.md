# NPCs - Isle of the Broken Sphere

---

## THE COMMUNITY (4)

### Elder Tess
**Role:** Senior Galapa elder, de facto spiritual and strategic leader of the Isle  
**Ancestry:** Galapa, age 156  
**Appearance:** Shell worn with extraordinary age — dark, deeply ridged, with a faint warmth that she attributes to a lifetime of proximity to the island's sacred energy  
**Personality:** The slowest voice in any room — not from age but from the Galapa understanding that fast decisions in uncertain situations are usually wrong. Genuinely calm. The calm is earned, not performed.  
**Beliefs:** The island has a spirit — ancient, vast, and currently disturbed. She does not know what disturbed it. Her best theory is that the approaching Hollostar has agitated sacred things across the continent. She has no framework for what the Spine Stones actually are. She calls them omens and treats them accordingly.  
**Goals:** Perform the old rituals correctly; understand what is happening before deciding whether to announce it; protect the Galapa community  
**What she knows:** The old traditions — which places are sacred, what the signs mean spiritually, what the protocol is if the island becomes "unsafe for living." She does not know why the protocol exists. It was passed down without explanation.  
**What she doesn't know:** Everything in The Sleeper's GM section.  
**Quote:** *"The island has been patient with us. Something has unsettled it. I am trying to learn what before I ask others to act."*  
**Hook:** The party's most important ally; holds pieces of the truth without knowing they are pieces; the old tales she knows are breadcrumbs she cannot read

---

### Darro
**Role:** Ex-merchant, community logistics coordinator — the person who actually organizes things  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 51  
**Appearance:** Looks like someone who has been quietly panicking for six weeks and has gotten very good at not showing it  
**Personality:** Practical to the point of bluntness. Came to the Isle seven years ago to disappear (won't say from what). Has discovered he's good at community and bad at leaving well enough alone.  
**Goals:** Prepare for evacuation without triggering panic; identify the Lumencrest agent; keep the community functioning  
**Secret:** Someone on the mainland has been sending him information about the outside world's reaction to the Isle rumors. He hasn't told anyone who this contact is.  
**Quote:** *"I'm not panicking. I'm planning. There's a difference, and I need everyone to understand the difference."*  
**Hook:** Most practically useful community contact; his mainland information source is a dangling thread worth pulling

---

### Brint
**Role:** Galapa harbor master, Shellhaven  
**Ancestry:** Galapa, age 89  
**Appearance:** Methodical, always has a ledger  
**Personality:** Keeps records because records tell the truth when people won't. Has been watching the departure numbers for two months. Showed them to Elder Tess. Has not shown them to anyone else until now.  
**Goals:** Maintain harbor function; track the real situation through numbers; have an evacuation plan ready that nobody has officially asked him to prepare  
**Quote:** *"The numbers say what the numbers say. I don't interpret them. I just write them down."*  
**Hook:** The island's honest information source — his records show the real timeline of departures and arrivals

---

### Maret Shell-Young
**Role:** Young Galapa, leader of the group that wants to leave  
**Ancestry:** Galapa, age 31  
**Appearance:** Shell still showing youth-smoothness — will be beautiful when aged, currently just looks worried  
**Personality:** Loves the Isle, loves the community, does not want to die for it. Genuinely respects Elder Tess. Genuinely disagrees with her on this.  
**Goals:** Leave before it gets worse; convince enough of her generation to come with her; not abandon Elder Tess  
**Quote:** *"Elder Tess has lived through two accelerations. She hasn't lived through one with formations. Neither has anyone else."*  
**Hook:** The generational divide hook; her departure, if it happens, takes 80-100 young Galapa with her — significant community impact

## LUMENCREST (2)

### Captain Sera Holdfast
**Role:** Captain of The Meridian Survey  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 45  
**Appearance:** Naval competence — moves through a ship like it's an extension of herself, moves through the Isle like it's terrain she doesn't trust  
**Personality:** Professional, fair, increasingly aware that this assignment was not accurately described to her. Not corrupt — genuinely wants to do her job. Her job is becoming complicated.  
**Goals:** Complete the survey, protect her crew, not become part of something she doesn't understand  
**The shift:** Once she sees the formations, her priorities change. She's a naval captain, not an Expansionist. The embedded agent on her ship is a problem she is beginning to suspect.  
**Quote:** *"I was told anomalous readings. I was not told formations. There is a difference."*  
**Hook:** Potential ally if separated from the agent's influence; needs to understand what her ship has sailed into

---

### Orev Dann
**Role:** Lumencrest geologist, lead researcher  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 52  
**Appearance:** Academically disheveled, immediately energized by the wrong things  
**Personality:** Genuinely, purely scientifically excited. Has not yet processed that his excitement could get people hurt. Is about to process it.  
**Goals:** Document the formations; understand the energy readings; publish findings  
**The moment:** When Dann realizes that publishing his findings goes to Lumencrest first, and Lumencrest will use them in ways he didn't agree to when he took this posting. Has been avoiding this thought. The island is making it unavoidable.  
**Quote:** *"This is extraordinary. I need to get closer. I need significantly better instruments. I need — why is everyone looking at me like that?"*  
**Hook:** His data is double-edged — could help or devastate the Isle depending on who gets it; he can be redirected if someone makes the moral case clearly

## OUTSIDE (1)

### The Mainland Contact (unnamed)
**Role:** Darro's information source — identity unknown to anyone on the Isle including party  
**What they send:** Accurate information about the outside world's reaction to Isle rumors, shipping route changes, Lumencrest ship departure  
**Why:** Unknown  
**Who they might be:** A former Isle resident who left. A Starfall Seeker with an interest in the situation. Someone who owes Darro a debt. Someone who wants to know what's happening on the Isle and is trading information for information.  
**Hook:** The thread that, when pulled, connects the Isle to something larger — GM's choice what that larger thing is
