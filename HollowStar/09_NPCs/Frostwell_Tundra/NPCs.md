# NPCs - Frostwell Tundra

---

## NINE HEARTHS LEADERSHIP (4)

### Hearthspeaker Mava
**Role:** Frostmeet village leader, de facto confederation chair  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 47  
**Appearance:** Exhausted in a way that has become permanent, silver-streaked dark hair, always has ink on her hands (writing, writing, writing — correspondence, records, proposals)  
**Personality:** Brilliant administrator in a crisis that administration cannot solve. Keeps going because stopping means admitting it's over.  
**Goals:** Pass the evacuation vote before it's too late; keep the confederation intact through the process  
**Secret:** She's read the Sunken Library tablet the Ribbet diver brought up. It says something about the star's arrival that she hasn't shared with anyone. She needs to decide if knowing would help or destroy the vote.  
**Quote:** *"I have had this conversation forty times. I will have it forty more. Someone will change their mind eventually."*  
**Hook:** Needs outsiders for sensitive political work; holds a secret that changes everything

---

### Elder Durn
**Role:** Coldfast village elder, most respected Stayer voice  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 68  
**Appearance:** Massive (Giant blood somewhere in his past), weathered into something that looks like the rock formations he refuses to leave  
**Personality:** Genuinely believes what he says. Not stubborn — certain. Has survived things that would have made a lesser person run. Cannot conceive of running.  
**Goals:** Keep Coldfast standing; preserve what the Nine Hearths actually is  
**Tragedy:** His grandchildren left two weeks ago. He hasn't spoken of it.  
**Quote:** *"The tundra tested us before. We are still here. That is the answer."*  
**Hook:** Won't be moved by argument — but might be moved by evidence that Coldfast specifically is in the impact zone

---

### Village-Chief Petra Thornholt
**Role:** Thornholt leader, most vocal Leaver  
**Ancestry:** Giant, age 34  
**Appearance:** 8 feet, pragmatic build, moves with the efficiency of someone who has already packed  
**Personality:** Done arguing. Doing instead. Half her village is gone and she considers that a success.  
**Goals:** Get the remaining Thornholt residents out safely; convince other Leaver villages to coordinate  
**Frustration:** The confederation council is wasting time she doesn't have  
**Quote:** *"I respect what Durn built. I intend to build it again somewhere that won't kill us."*  
**Hook:** Needs escorts, scouts, and people to help coordinate the practical work of evacuation

---

### Watcher Aren
**Role:** Icewatch village record-keeper and chief observer  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 29  
**Appearance:** Young for such responsibility, always squinting (eye damage from years of staring at bright snow and aurora)  
**Personality:** Data-focused to the point of social awkwardness; completely certain of his calculations; terrified of what they mean  
**Goals:** Get his observational data to people who can use it; stay to watch the impact (intellectually compelled despite knowing the risk)  
**Quote:** *"The numbers say fourteen months. I have checked them eleven times. I am going to check them again."*  
**Hook:** His data conflicts with Lumencrest — resolving this is critical to everything

## DWARVES (2)

### Clan-Chief Berrik Stoneshield
**Role:** Ironspine clan leader  
**Ancestry:** Dwarf, age 156  
**Appearance:** Standard dwarf stockiness, silver beard, permanent calculating expression  
**Personality:** Fair within his clan, genuinely indifferent to outsiders' problems, currently profiting from the crisis without apology  
**Goals:** Ironspine survives intact; trade advantage maintained  
**Quote:** *"We live inside a mountain. The star is your problem. The supplies you need are mine. Let's discuss price."*  
**Hook:** Essential trade resource; can be leveraged for information about Greywall's secrets

---

### Deep-Keeper Varra Ironmantle
**Role:** Greywall clan elder, keeper of "the old work" knowledge  
**Ancestry:** Dwarf, age 203  
**Appearance:** Ancient even for a dwarf, moves slowly, eyes still sharp  
**Personality:** Carries knowledge like weight. Has been deciding for decades whether to share it. The approaching star has made the decision for her.  
**Secret:** The Greywall clan records on "the old work" include a construction date for the Ember Cradle and a description of what's in the sealed lower chambers. She's the last person alive who's read the original documents.  
**Quote:** *"There are things you should know before the star arrives. I am not sure I want to be the one to tell you."*  
**Hook:** Dying, wants to pass on knowledge, her clan will try to stop her

---

## DRAKONA (2)

### Flame-Keeper Sorith
**Role:** Senior tender of the Ember Cradle, Ashkeep's spiritual leader  
**Ancestry:** Drakona, age 61  
**Appearance:** Unusual for Drakona — deliberately understated (no warrior ornamentation), scales more grey than her people's norm (cold adaptation over decades)  
**Personality:** Patient, precise, increasingly frightened by what she's measuring at the Cradle  
**Goals:** Understand the warming before it becomes something she can't contain; protect Ashkeep's spiritual authority while getting the answers she needs  
**Secret:** The Cradle's lower chambers aren't sealed by ice. They're sealed by something else. And it's been responding to the warming by... softening.  
**Quote:** *"Ashkindl has been quiet for a very long time. I am no longer certain it will remain so."*  
**Hook:** Needs outside help she can trust; holds the most important secret in the tundra

---

### Ashscale Denn
**Role:** Ashkeep's guardian captain, Sorith's practical counterpart  
**Ancestry:** Drakona, age 38  
**Appearance:** Battle-ready even at rest — Drakona who chose cold over comfort don't get soft  
**Personality:** Protective of Ashkeep above all else; not hostile to outsiders who respect the rules; immediately hostile to those who don't  
**Goals:** Keep unauthorized people out of the Cradle; support Sorith's decisions  
**Quote:** *"You can ask. Sorith will answer or she won't. You cannot go in without permission. This is not negotiable."*  
**Hook:** The door that outsiders must get through — party must earn trust or find another way

## OUTSIDERS (3)

### Lead Researcher Odalys Venn (Lumencrest)
**Role:** Head of Lumencrest's Frostwell research team  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 44  
**Appearance:** Overqualified for the cold, visibly uncomfortable, brilliant  
**Personality:** Genuinely scientifically motivated; also absolutely reporting to Expansionist faction; not entirely comfortable with this contradiction  
**Goals:** Get Sunken Library access; document the approaching Hollostar; be at impact site  
**Quote:** *"The scientific opportunity here is unprecedented. I would prefer everyone survive to appreciate that."*  
**Hook:** Information resource and political complication; her messages home are being encoded for a reason

---

### Expedition Leader Tam Coldridge (Starfall Seekers)
**Role:** Leads Seekers' Frostwell expedition  
**Ancestry:** Human, age 52  
**Appearance:** Born for cold (actually grew up in Northern Expanse); most comfortable person in the tundra  
**Personality:** Methodical, fair, genuinely concerned for Nine Hearths welfare alongside research goals  
**Goals:** Document everything; resolve the calculation discrepancy; be present at the impact (willing to die for this — the Seekers understand the cost)  
**Quote:** *"We study Hollowstars. This is the moment all of that study has been building toward. Of course we're staying."*  
**Hook:** Best outsider ally in the tundra; has resources, connections, and no political agenda beyond knowledge

---

### Pilgrim-Speaker Maret of the Deep Frost (Path of Spirits)
**Role:** Leader of the Path of Spirits pilgrims  
**Ancestry:** Human (Giant heritage — tall, broad), age 55  
**Appearance:** Path of Spirits robes adapted for cold (furs over robes, practical despite the spiritual symbolism)  
**Personality:** Sincere, charismatic, absolutely certain the impact is the most important spiritual event in living memory — and not wrong about that  
**Goals:** Witness the impact; commune with the spirits being displaced by the star; consecrate the ruins before they're destroyed  
**Complication:** Her presence is inflaming the Ember Cradle territorial dispute  
**Quote:** *"The star does not come to destroy. It comes to transform. We are here to bear witness to what changes."*  
**Hook:** Valuable spiritual perspective; causing political problems; may know something about the aurora messages
