1. Firstmonth
Spirit Moon: Deep Cold
Coldest month. Winter proper. Short days, heavy snow in the north. Lumencrest brownouts are worst this month — the Lumen Network strains under heating demand.
2. Thawmonth
Spirit Moon: Stirring Below
Late winter. Ice begins melting. The Emberfork runs high and dangerous. Travel between cities resumes after the winter slowdown. Midwinter's Rest between 1 & 2
3. Seedmonth
Spirit Moon: Thaw Water
Early spring. Planting begins in the Central Heartlands. Path of Spirits communities hold planting ceremonies. The Green Covenant's most active recruiting season.
4. Bloommonth
Spirit Moon: First Green
Spring proper. Flowers and growth. The Accord quarterly meeting falls in Bloommonth. Trade routes fully open. Spring's Awakening between 3 & 4
5. Greenmonth
Spirit Moon: Birdsong
Late spring. Abundant growth. The best month for travel. Most diplomatic missions depart in Greenmonth to arrive before summer heat.
6. Sunpeak
Spirit Moon: Long Light
Early summer. Longest days. Lumencrest's festivals — the city celebrates the Lumen Network in Sunpeak with public light displays. The approaching star is most visible on clear Sunpeak nights. Summer's Height between 6 & 7
7. Harvestmonth
Spirit Moon: Young Growth
Mid-summer. Early harvests in southern regions. The Accord food reserve coordination is supposed to happen here. Currently a source of open suspicion between cities.
8. Heatmonth
Spirit Moon: High Sun
Hottest period. Cross-basin travel is restricted midday. The Endless Eye's market shifts to dawn-and-dusk schedule. Frostwell refugee movement slows — too hot in the lowlands.
9. Goldmonth ← NOW
Spirit Moon: Harvest Plenty
Early autumn. Main harvest season. The Emberfork ford at its lowest — safe for wagons. Lumencrest timber buyers travel this month. Politically convenient time for the Vael visit. Campaign starts: Gold Month 8, 700 AR. Harvest's End between 9 & 10
10. Fallmonth
Spirit Moon: Leaf Fall
Autumn proper. Leaves falling. The Ember Grove is at its most dramatic — amber and gold. Festival of the Hearthkeeper in most Central Heartlands towns.
11. Darkmonth
Spirit Moon: Dark Gathering
Late autumn. Days shortening fast. The veil between the spirit world and the mortal realm is considered thinnest this month by Path of Spirits practitioners. Ceremonies for the dead.
12. Frostmonth
Spirit Moon: Spirit Watch
Early winter. First snows. The Spirit Watch month is when practitioners believe ancestor spirits are most reachable. The Green Covenant holds its internal planning for the coming year. Year's Turn between 12 & 1
Accord Reckoning (AR)

Origin: Standardized ~400 years ago when the Heartland Accord formed. Year Zero = the fall of the third Hollostar and creation of the Endless Eye (700 years ago). Current year: 700 AR.

Structure: 12 months of 30 days each = 360 days, plus 5–6 festival days between certain months. 365 days total (366 every 4th year).

Writing dates: Formal — "8th of Goldmonth, 700 AR." Informal — "8/9/700." Very informal — "Gold Month 700."

Used by: Everyone for trade, governance, official records, and daily life across all five Accord cities and their territories.

Festival Days (outside the regular calendar)
  • Midwinter's Rest — between Firstmonth and Thawmonth. 1 day. Rest, family, no commerce.
  • Spring's Awakening — between Seedmonth and Bloommonth. 1 day. Planting ceremonies.
  • Summer's Height — between Sunpeak and Harvestmonth. 1 day. Lumencrest's big celebration.
  • Harvest's End — between Goldmonth and Fallmonth. 1 day. Giving thanks for harvest.
  • Year's Turn — between Frostmonth and Firstmonth. 1–2 days. New year, resolutions, promises.
Spirit Moons (Path of Spirits / Green Covenant)

Origin: Ancient — predates any known civilization, possibly 10,000+ years old. Used by Path of Spirits followers and Green Covenant for spiritual observance.

Structure: 13 lunar months of 28 days each = 364 days, plus 1–2 intercalary days. Years are not numbered sequentially — time is cyclical.

Dating style: "This is the third spring since the great fire." "Born in Deep Cold during the year of the white deer." Practitioners convert to AR for external communication.

Current moon: Harvest Plenty (corresponds to Goldmonth).

Used by: Greer uses both. Thorn uses Spirit Moons internally, converts to AR for Emberford dealings. The Green Covenant uses both — AR for diplomacy, Spirit Moons for all internal ceremony.

Other Calendar Systems (brief)
  • Lumencrest Era (LE): ~1,200 LE. Used internally by Lumencrest scholars and old families. Official docs use AR.
  • Katari Oral Calendar: No written form. Lives in memory and recitation. Tracks lineages and historical events through generational chains. Remarkably precise.
  • Somnarch Star Calendar: Tracks Hollostar falls and cosmic cycles. Four known falls — fifth predicted within 2–5 years.
  • Dwarven Deep Reckoning (DR): From mythical "First Delving." Used internally only. No surface dwellers know the current DR year.
