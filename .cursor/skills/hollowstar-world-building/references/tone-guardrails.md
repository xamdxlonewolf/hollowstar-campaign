# Tone Guardrails — World of Hollowstars

From `00_Index/README.md` and `01_Core_Concepts/Themes and Tone.md`.

## Tone Levels

| Axis | Level | Guidance |
|------|-------|----------|
| Cosmic horror | 2/10 | Mysterious, unsettling — not sanity-breaking |
| Political intrigue | 7/10 | Faction conflicts, moral grays, competing interests |
| Wonder | 8/10 | Beautiful impossible things matter |
| Hope | 5/10 | Darkness is real; heroes can still change outcomes |

## Core Themes

1. Hope amidst darkness
2. The beautiful unknowable (Hollowstars)
3. Community & collaboration
4. Political intrigue & moral complexity

## Writing Rules

**Do:**
- Let factions have legitimate goals
- Show observable phenomena (dimming Heart, shrinking Eye) as facts characters can investigate
- Leave core mysteries open — players discover, GM does not declare
- Connect local events to larger patterns when appropriate (30-year sync of ancient sites)

**Don't:**
- Explain what Hollowstars truly are in player-facing lore
- Create mustache-twirling villains
- Break established calendar/geography without updating indexes
- Front-load all secrets — use `08_GM_Notes/Secrets_and_Revelations.md` pacing tiers

## Voice

- Grounded, specific sensory detail
- NPCs sound like people with history, not exposition dispensers
- Thorn Oakwise voice: still, short sentences, silence is intentional
- Political NPCs: prepared talking points that reveal character, not plot dumps
